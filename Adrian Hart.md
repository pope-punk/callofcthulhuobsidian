## Description

Officer Adrian Hart was an intelligence official involved with the [[CONSORT Committee]]'s investigation into the [[CALLISTO (1949)|CALLISTO]] event.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[CONSORT Committee]]**: Member of the committee investigating the 1949 port explosions.
* **[[CALLISTO Report]]**: Co-presented the Operation CONSORT report findings to the National Security Council alongside Dr. [[Hannah Christmas]] on March 1st, 1951. He also edited the final version of the report provided in the documents.
* **Continued Research**: After the official NATO investigation transfer stalled due to the Korean War, research secretly continued under Officer Hart's direction with NATO approval.

## Status

Lived until 1966. Died on an undisclosed mission in Vietnam. Survived by son, Lucas Hart, who lives in Chicago, and daughter Penelope Hart, who lives in Quebec.**
