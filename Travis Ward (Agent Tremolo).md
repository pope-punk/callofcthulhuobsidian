## Description

Travis Ward, known by the code name **Agent Tremolo**, is identified as a Senior Field Agent within the [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]]. Mid 40s black man with unbelievably deep voice.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]]**: Senior field agent based at the BNJC headquarters in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].
* **Interaction with Players**: Met with the player characters at the BNJC facility. Conducted a cryptic interview/assessment involving improvisational answers to prompts based on strange cards. Shared limited information about BNJC operations, the destruction of the [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] facility (attributing it to [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]]), the factional split regarding Agent Caraway, [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s rogue status, [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]]'s disappearance, and confirmed [[Katherine Hill|Agent Kayak]]'s identity.
* **Misinformation/Perspective**: Believed [[Syd Foxglove]] might have used the [[The Foxglove Algorithm|Foxglove Algorithm]] to mind-control Alice Redmond. Expressed general BNJC operational inertia and confusion regarding Alice's actions.
