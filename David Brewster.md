## Description

David Brewster was one of the unwitting hosts of the "Axon and Dendrite" radio show during [[Operation Colossus]] (1987-1992). He was executed by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] in mid-1992.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **"Axon and Dendrite"**: Co-hosted the avant-garde radio art show with [[Maria Chen]], which was used by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] to broadcast [[The Echo]] basilisk from the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. Funded via the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]].
* **[[Operation Colossus]]**: Unwitting participant in the BSSA operation.
* **SDEC Contact & Disruption:** In mid-1992, [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] contacted Brewster and Chen, likely revealing some danger associated with the broadcasts. SDEC provided them with a **randomizer tool** and **mapping rules** ([[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]]) to disrupt the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN by altering the predictable structure of [[The Echo]]-laden broadcasts.
* **Final Testament & Security Measure:** Aware that SDEC had likely been compromised and that BSSA was hunting him, Brewster prepared a final, unaired "testament" episode of Axon and Dendrite. He deliberately structured its **six segments** using a specific **four-digit code** entered into the SDEC randomizer. He documented this final sequence ([[Brewster's Final Segment Sequence Log]]). His desperate motivation was to use the SDEC-specific system as a security measure, believing only surviving SDEC agents or their allies could decipher the code needed to access sensitive information he wanted to protect from BSSA.
* **The Lockbox:** Brewster entrusted a physical lockbox – containing primarily contact information for [[Maria Chen]] and potentially final words or evidence related to [[Operation Colossus]] – to a trusted friend (e.g., [[Professor Vance]]). The **four-digit code** derived from his final broadcast sequence serves as the **passphrase**; the friend was instructed to only yield the lockbox upon hearing this specific code, proving the seeker understood the SDEC system and was likely an ally.
* **Execution:** Captured, possibly tortured, and executed by BSSA at the [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb|Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] site in Ellensburg during the termination of Operation Colossus in mid-1992. BSSA likely covered this up, potentially listing him as a casualty of EUROPA.

## Status

**Deceased** (Executed mid-1992). His final actions created the [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]], a key step for players to locate [[Maria Chen]] and uncover more about BSSA's past.
