## Overview

A **basilisk** is a pattern — visual, auditory, sometimes spatial — that does something deleterious to the human brain when perceived. The effect is materialist, not magical: the basilisk works because it resonates with cognitive pathways the brain happens to have, most of which [[Rhea]] engineered into humans over evolutionary time. An **infohazard** is the basilisk plus whatever larger emergent process forms around it. Where a basilisk is the pattern, the [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]] is what arises around the pattern once enough minds and systems start processing it.

The two halves are inseparable in practice. Trying to weaponize a basilisk — even just to understand one — feeds a RONIN. This is true whether the weaponization is deliberate (BSSA cultivating [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]] around [[The Echo]]) or accidental (IHRU analyzing CALLISTO data and generating [[Black Coral]] in the process).

## Major basilisks

* **[[The Hexacle]]** — visual, hexagonal. The Ur-Basilisk. Triggers a targeted amnesia so complete that subjects don't notice they've forgotten anything. Rhea's primary tool for concealment.
* **[[The Snowflake]]** — visual / spatial. Winston's warehouse layouts. Produces a passive compulsion in workers; built the systemic feedback loops behind [[CALLISTO (1949)|CALLISTO]].
* **[[Black Coral]]** — visual fractal. Accidentally generated by IHRU during CALLISTO analysis. Causes violent insanity and a compulsion to reproduce the pattern by hand, which is how it spread. Vector for [[GANYMEDE (1979)|GANYMEDE]].
* **[[The Echo]]** — auditory. Modulated tone sequences. Initially produced religious hallucinations, drifted into cryptid hallucinations as the Axon-and-Dendrite feedback loop developed. Vector for EUROPA.
* **[[Fauxflake]]** — visual / spatial; derived from The Snowflake. BSSA's current cultivation target ([[Operation Parthenon]]); intended vector for the [[IO (Emerging 1997)|IO]] RONIN. Exists as a 3D pew arrangement in the sub-sanctuary at the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]], and as 2D translatable diagrams Katherine wants to weaponize.

The basilisks are not unrelated — they're all derivatives, applications, or accidents of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]], which is the underlying pattern Rhea engineered into the geometry of human cognition. See [[Hexagonal Patterns]] for the survey.

## Defense

Two strategies, philosophically opposed:

* **Chemical (BSSA).** [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] suppresses the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]], which is the part of cognition basilisks exploit. F7 users perceive a basilisk as meaningless. The cost is severe cognitive impairment in everything else the System Pole handles. [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]] and the theoretical [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] reverse F7 by restoring or amplifying the System Pole — which, in the limit, walks the user straight back into the original vulnerability.
* **Cognitive (Mirror-Breaker).** The [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] tries to avoid the vulnerability by refusing to operate in the modality the basilisk targets — improvising, working in metaphor, compartmentalizing, breaking up patterns rather than analyzing them.
