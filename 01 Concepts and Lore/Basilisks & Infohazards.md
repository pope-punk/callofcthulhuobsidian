## Overview

A **basilisk** is a pattern — visual, auditory, sometimes spatial — that does something deleterious to the human brain when perceived. The effect is materialist, not magical: the basilisk works because it resonates with cognitive pathways the brain happens to have, most of which [[Rhea]] engineered into humans over evolutionary time. An **infohazard** is the basilisk plus whatever larger emergent [[RONIN]] process forms around it. The [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]] is an apparent subjectivity that exists in and through the organizing effects of the basilisk on minds and systems.

The truth, suspected by Mirror-Breaker Protocol loyalists but denied by their opponents, and fully understood in its complete implications by no one, is that the two halves are inseparable in practice — RONIN are Basilisks in motion, and Basilisks are analogous to a RONIN's neural signalling between its nodes. Trying to weaponize a basilisk — or even just systematically analyze one — eventually feeds a RONIN. This is true whether the weaponization is deliberate (BSSA cultivating [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]] around [[The Echo]]) or accidental (IHRU analyzing CALLISTO data and generating [[Black Coral]] in the process). 

## Major basilisks

* **[[The Hexacle]]** — The Ur-Basilisk. Something so close to directly representing the [[Foxglove Algorithm]] that it triggers the targeted amnesia that maintains Rhea's engineered repression. The resultant effect is acute memory loss so complete that subjects don't notice they've forgotten anything. Rhea's primary tool for concealment.
* **[[The Snowflake]]** — visual / spatial. Pattern formed by Winston's algorithmically evolving warehouse layouts. Produced a passive compulsion effect in workers viewing it from above on the catwalk system, driving the systemic feedback loops that would constitute [[CALLISTO (1949)|CALLISTO]] while also acting as its subjective hand.
* **[[Black Coral]]** — visual fractal. Accidentally generated by IHRU during CALLISTO analysis. Causes violent insanity and a compulsion to reproduce the pattern by hand, which is how it spread. Vector for [[GANYMEDE (1979)|GANYMEDE]]. Katherine is capable of reproducing it, and a version of it is maintained at Saint Anselm's for testing F7's efficacy. A boarded up room contains the dangerous scrawlings of an early (now deceased) patient who was not successfully inoculated against it. Black Coral's continued lethality long after Ganymede's destruction is material proof of the theoretical potential for long-term weaponization of simple 2d Basilisks after their necessary RONIN component has dissipated. 
* **[[The Echo]]** — auditory. Modulated tone sequences. Initially produced religious hallucinations, drifted into cryptid hallucinations as the Axon-and-Dendrite feedback loop developed. Vector for EUROPA. See [[Operation Colossus]].
* **[[Fauxflake]]** — visual / spatial; derived from The Snowflake. BSSA's current cultivation target ([[Operation Parthenon]]); intended vector for the [[IO (Emerging 1997)|IO]] RONIN. Exists as a 3D pew arrangement in the sub-sanctuary at the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]], and as 2D translatable diagrams Katherine wants to weaponize.

The basilisks are not unrelated — they're all derivatives, applications, or accidents of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]], which is the underlying pattern Rhea engineered into the geometry of human cognition. See [[Hexagonal Patterns]] for the survey.

## Defense

Two strategies, philosophically opposed:

* **Chemical (BSSA).** [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] suppresses the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]], which is the part of cognition basilisks exploit. F7 users perceive a basilisk as meaningless. The cost is severe cognitive impairment in everything else the System Pole handles. [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]] and the theoretical [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] reverse F7 by restoring or amplifying the System Pole — which, in the limit, walks the user straight back into the original vulnerability.
* **Cognitive (Mirror-Breaker).** The [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] tries to avoid the vulnerability by refusing to operate in the modality the basilisk targets — improvising, working in metaphor, compartmentalizing, breaking up patterns rather than analyzing them.
