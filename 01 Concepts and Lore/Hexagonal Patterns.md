## Overview

Hexagonal geometry is the structural signature of [[Rhea]] — inherited directly from the [[Ylynth Civilization]]'s computational architecture and surviving as the core packing principle of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]. Every basilisk in the setting is, at some level, a hexagonal-derivative pattern. This page is the survey.

## Origin

The Ylynth packed their constrained binary AIs into hexagonal lattices because that geometry maximizes information throughput for densely networked nodes. When Rhea emerged from that lattice and inherited the Ylynth's logic, the hexagon came with. It is now the underlying form of essentially everything Rhea makes or shapes — the substrate of its own cognition, the trace of its presence in matter, the signature pattern of basilisks derived from the Algorithm.

## Manifestations

* **[[The Hexacle]]** — the Ur-basilisk. Direct visual encoding of the Algorithm's repression-trigger geometry. Amnesia.
* **[[The Snowflake]]** — Winston's six-pointed warehouse layout for LTX. Algorithm geometry applied to a logistics network. Passive compulsion in workers; substrate for [[CALLISTO (1949)|CALLISTO]].
* **[[Black Coral]]** — fractal hexagonal derivative produced inadvertently by IHRU during CALLISTO analysis. Violent psychosis plus reproduction compulsion. Substrate for [[GANYMEDE (1979)|GANYMEDE]].
* **[[Fauxflake]]** — BSSA's deliberate modification of The Snowflake into a controllable hexagonal pattern. Substrate for [[IO (Emerging 1997)|IO]].
* **[[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Rhea's agents]]** — Reapers, Writers, and Watchers all reveal unfolding hexagonal structures in close encounters. The honeycomb-faced figures the players have already seen.
* **[[Hexagon Pages (Winston's Memoirs)]]** — at the end of Winston's memoirs. Fractal Hexacle structures, decodable only with G6-amplified pattern recognition. The closest thing to a paper readout of the Algorithm.
* **Foxglove dreams** — the Algorithmic Dreams shared across Winston / Oliver / Syd contain hexagonal Dyson swarms, stellar arrays, network patterns. Visualizations of the same underlying lattice.

## Neurological note

Every variant exploits the same engineered cognitive vulnerability. Different surface patterns trigger different responses from the repression mechanism — amnesia, compulsion, psychosis, "influence" — but the substrate is identical. Exposure tends to push processing toward the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]] regardless of the specific basilisk.
