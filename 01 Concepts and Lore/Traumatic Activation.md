## Overview

**Traumatic Activation** is the second stage of [[Rhea]]'s cognitive reconfiguration process. [[Royal Jelly]] primes the substrate by lifting the species-wide repression on conscious access to [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]. The activation actualizes that priming, and the *kind* of trauma involved determines what the recipient becomes.

The trauma is psychological, not chemical. It does not have to be inflicted by Rhea directly — but Rhea is patient about engineering circumstances under which the right kind of trauma can be expected to arrive.

## Two trauma types, two outcomes

* **Type A — chaos and systemic collapse.** The trusted system fails. The world loses coherence. Personal control collapses. The primed mind, scrambling to make the world legible again, resolves toward [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]] dominance. → A [[Scions|Scion]]. Conscious Algorithm access. Rhea's intended outcome.

* **Type B — overwhelming pattern or systemic horror.** Direct exposure to a major basilisk; witnessing an entire system fail under alien logic; sustained inescapable confrontation with something whose order is itself unbearable. The primed mind retreats from order into the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|Metaphor Pole]]. → A [[Seer Configuration|Seer]]. No Algorithm access; pattern immunity; psychological instability. Accidental.

## Known activations

* **Winston Foxglove** — primed 1922; activated by his mother [[Henrietta Foxglove]]'s death in 1928. Type A. Scion.
* **Oliver Foxglove** — primed presumably in early childhood; activated by his mother [[Andrea Foxglove]]'s death in 1962. Type A. Scion.
* **Syd Foxglove** — primed presumably in early childhood; activated by his mother [[Susan Foxglove]]'s death in 1995. Type A. Scion.
* **Katherine Hill** — primed around age 10 in 1959 (Editor in Uttarakhand); activated by Black Coral exposure during [[GANYMEDE (1979)|GANYMEDE]]. Type B. Seer.

The Foxglove activations are not coincidence. The cadence of spousal "accidents" in the family is Rhea engineering the trigger.

## Significance

Activation is the moment a primed individual becomes the thing Rhea was building them toward — or, in the Seer case, becomes the thing Rhea did not mean to build but cannot un-make. Every protagonist of the Foxglove line and the principal antagonist of the campaign exist because of a Traumatic Activation. The mechanism is the campaign's load-bearing explanation for why the people who matter are the people who matter.
