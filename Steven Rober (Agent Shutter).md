## Description

Steven Rober, known by the code name **Agent Shutter**, is presented as a recent addition to the [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]] but is secretly a mole working for the [[NOPG (New Orleans Photography Group)]] and, by extension, [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]]**: Posed as a new BNJC recruit in Sequoia Falls as of October 1997.
* **[[NOPG (New Orleans Photography Group)]]**: His true affiliation. NOPG is noted as supporting BSSA operations in Montreal.
* **Mole/Spy**: Introduced at BNJC after the players returned from [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] around October 10th. Overheard the [[Montreal Phone Number]] clue during a player debriefing.
* **Intelligence Leak**: Relayed the Montreal phone number to his NOPG handlers, which was then passed to BSSA. This intelligence allowed BSSA to locate and recapture [[Syd Foxglove]] in Montreal shortly after October 10th.

## Status

Active mole within BNJC as of October 1997. His betrayal directly led to Syd's recapture.
