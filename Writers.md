## Overview
A type of [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Editor]] serving [[Rhea]]'s long-term goals of societal influence and concealment, primarily through the placement of [[The Hexacle]]. 

## Appearance & Capabilities
* **Camouflage:** Can take mundane forms, often large mammals common to the region (e.g., wolves, elk mentioned). Typically takes form of humans or Yylynthii. 
* **Primary Function:** Place [[The Hexacle]] patterns in strategic locations (e.g., around Hive entrances like [[Dead Horse Quarry]], potentially areas needing secrecy like [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]]) to induce amnesia and prevent discovery or interference.
* **Method:** Use a **black, viscous substance** secreted or produced by their bodies to paint the Hexacle patterns.
* **Secondary Actions:** Also responsible for the internal destruction and elimination of personnel at the [[SDEC Facility|SDEC]] facility in 1992 (though the specific agent may have been the specialized [[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)]]).

## Known Actions
* Placed [[The Hexacle|Hexacles]] around [[Dead Horse Quarry]] post-1922.
* Placed [[The Hexacle|Hexacles]] around the I-90 corridor near COTU during 1960s construction.
* Destroyed [[SDEC Facility|SDEC]] personnel/facility internally (1992) (or facilitated the [[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)|Infiltrator]]).

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Active agents maintaining [[Rhea]]'s concealment fields and potentially eliminating minor obstacles. Less likely to directly target investigators unless they interfere with Hexacle placement or protected sites.
