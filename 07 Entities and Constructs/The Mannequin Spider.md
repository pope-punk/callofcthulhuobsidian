## Overview
An autonomously self-assembled defense construct at the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]], cobbled together from [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]] parts. It is not a designed product; it is an emergent security response to the [[Rhea]]-managed production environment. Multi-limbed, fast, and silent.

## Confirmed Kills
* **Jason Tillman** — April 3, 1997 (ErgoMatic employee).
* **Gabby Hollingsworth** — September 8, 1997 (ErgoMatic employee).

Disappearances at the facility have been suppressed by the compromised [[Sheriffs Department (Kittitas County)]].

## Role in Campaign
Potential combat encounter on the ErgoMatic manufacturing floor during the Ellensburg arc. Disabled by the same methods as ordinary Signal Dummies (cattle prod, sustained electrical disruption), but its limb count and speed make engaging it directly dangerous.

## Status
Active. Lurks among the production lines.
