## Overview
A type of [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Editor]] serving as specialized **assassins** for [[Rhea]]. Tasked with eliminating specific individuals or lineages deemed threats to Rhea's plans or secrecy. They have directly guided evolution on earth by acting as "unnatural selection".

## Appearance & Capabilities
* **Camouflage:** Can likely take mundane forms, but descriptions often involve **elk-like** or **tall, gaunt humanoid** figures.
* **Attack Method:** Possess tentacles (often concealed) ending in sharp, drill-like protrusions used for lethal attacks. Capable of extreme violence and dismemberment.
* **"Honeycomb Face" / Unfolding:** Known to possess heads or faces that unfold to reveal a lattice of hexagonal holes, possibly linked to sensory input or attack deployment. May emit disorienting light flashes ("alien camera flash") from these holes, potentially related to [[Spectator Grubs|Grub]] activation or a defensive mechanism.
* **Tracking:** Implied to be persistent trackers (pursued [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] from [[Edmund White Estate]] to [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]]).
* **Stealth/Infiltration:** Capable of hiding effectively (e.g., in ventilation systems).

## Known Actions
* Murdered [[Oliver Foxglove]] (Jan 10, 1997) to ensure LTX-ErgoMatic merger proceeded.
* Elk Reaper Attacked investigators and killed [[Colin Reeves]] at [[Alice Redmond's House]] (Oct 8, 1997).
* Wolf Reapers attacked and killed [[Calvin Pierce]] at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] - attacked players, subsequently defeated by them.
* [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] at the [[Edmund White Estate]] (Sept 8, 1997), forcing her into the tunnels. This same Reaper is likely still active and may be encountered there.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Active agents deployed by [[Rhea]] for targeted eliminations. Represent a significant direct physical threat to the investigators and their allies.
