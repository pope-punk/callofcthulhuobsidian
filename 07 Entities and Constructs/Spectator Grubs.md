Spectator Grubs are tube-like entities crucial to [[Rhea]]'s information processing and memory systems, existing in both organic and synthetic forms.

## Spectator Grubs (Organic)

* **Appearance**: Tube-like bodies (approx. 8-12 inches long, 2 inches diameter) covered in photosensitive slime.
* **Function**: Register the current state of the genetic totality (Rhea's biological awareness). They act as Rhea's biological memory and instruction system.
* **Method**: Emit intense bursts of bioluminescence to record information in slime layers. This flash causes disorientation and temporary amnesia in human observers.
* **Processing**: Returned to the [[Hive]], digested, and integrated into biological instructions.
* **Delivery**: Carried by [[Watchers]] within hexagonal body holes and delivered to [[Signal Dummies]] for direct biological intervention/instruction into the technological network.
* **Composition**: Contain complex genetic structures with direct Rhea directives. Alice Redmond collected preserved samples.

## Spectator Grubs (Synthetic)

* **Creation**: Manufactured using the GenAssist Universal Tissue Printer at [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]]'s BAD division.
* **Composition**: Sugar-based organisms with simplified G-C nucleotide genomes encoding instructions for [[Signal Dummies]]. Hybridized with Rhea's biological templates.
* **Distribution**: Shipped via mail systems (likely LTX) to Signal Dummies.
* **Function**: Encode current LTX shipping network activation state patterns into their genetic structure. Consumed by Signal Dummies, their DNA is translated via the internal DNA-Machine Code Conversion System to provide new directives.
* **Players** brought a sample to [[Prof. Alexander Austen]] for analysis.
* **Significance**: Function as Rhea's recursive memory and data transmission system within its technological substrate. Players found these printed "worms" at Signal Dummy locations.

## Grub Cycles 
### Organic: 
- Spectator grubs use photoluminescent flashing to "scan" genetic composition of organisms in "sight". Carried by Watchers to conduct these recordings, and then returned back to a Hive to be digested so that their information can be internalized and translated into direct genomic instructions for the Hive's birthing systems. This translation is actuated by the Foxglove algorithm.
- In this way, the information recorded by grubs informs the Hive's birthing of a new generation of Editors. They carry instinctual genetic instructions to "edit" the biosphere by culling certain species, either outright eliminating their genetic line over time or shifting ecosystem equilibria in the direction needed for human evolution. 
- These shifted ecosystems are thus scanned by future grubs, recorded, and returned to a Hive, and the cycle begins again.
- This dialectic of "reading" and "writing" to the biosphere forms an informational feedback loop of the same type which undergirded Ylynthi civilization - a system of self-observation through a unified algorithmic logic. A consciousness thus emerges and is sustained - Rhea.

### Synthetic: 
- Synthetic spectator grubs are printed using GenAssist, loaded with information encoded as binary DNA strings (comprised entirely of GC/CG pairs in complex arrangements)
- These binary DNA strings gain determinate content through the action of GenAssist, which takes as its input LTX's shipping data, fed directly into it after the merger completed.
- These strings are "compiled" by a Signal Dummy when consumed, the [[DNA-Machine Code Conversion System]] embedded in each unit converting the printed grub into clear instructions via the Foxglove algorithm, which are almost always to send dirt to other Signal Dummies. 
- These additional orders update LTX's shipping data, which in turn updates the grubs printed by GenAssist, which in turn generates further orders.
- This dialectic of "reading" and "writing" to the econosphere forms an informational feedback loop of the same type which undergrided Ylynthi civilization, a system of self-observation through a unified algorithmic logic. A consciousness thus emrges and is sustained - Rhea. 

### Confluent: 
- Rhea does not immediately have nearly enough direct leverage on the LTX shipping network to generate meaningful instructional signals from it. 
- During the bootstrapping phase, and for direct system maintenance, Watchers deliver instructions to Signal Dummies primarily using Organic Grubs, whose genetic instructions can also be decoded in the same way. 
- In this way, there are stable nodes which connect Rhea's highly developed biospheric operations to the growing econospheric network, allowing it to structure this network actively as well as gradually transfer the fundaments of its "consciousness" (whatever that might mean to us) to that substrate in a controlled way.
