## Overview
A specialized [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Rhea agent]] — almost certainly a highly evolved [[Writers|Writer]] — dispatched in late 1992 to breach the [[SDEC Facility]] in San Diego. SDEC's research had connected [[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)|Winston's memoirs]] to the [[Dead Horse Quarry]] anomaly, threatening to expose [[Rhea]]'s primary West Coast [[Hive]] entrance.

The Infiltrator systematically eliminated SDEC's personnel and triggered the facility's terminal containment measure ("the Ant Trap"), transforming SDEC into an automated deathtrap rather than allowing the information to escape.

## Status
Unknown. Possibly destroyed in the 1992 conflict; possibly still resident within the sealed facility as a lingering hazard for any on-site visit.

## Role in Campaign
A potential late-arc threat if the players elect to physically enter SDEC after the remote-terminal extraction at PCBOT. Otherwise, the Infiltrator remains a historical menace that explains *why* SDEC is sealed.
