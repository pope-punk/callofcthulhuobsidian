## Overview
A large, likely secluded estate in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. Formerly owned by [[Edmund White]] (deceased). Used by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] as a safe house and operational base after leaving the FBI and joining [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]].

## Key Events
* **Alice's Safehouse:** Used by Alice for several years. Likely contains remnants of her investigations into [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].
* **Dylan Hill Interrogation & Death (July 15, 1997):** Alice brought [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]] here after ambushing him. She interrogated him, learned about BSSA operations, was injected with [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]], and subsequently killed him here.
* **Reaper Attack (Sept 8, 1997):** A [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reaper]] tracked Alice here, forcing her to flee into the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] tunnels. Planned site for player encounter with this specific Reaper according to future trajectory.

## Significance
* Site of [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]]'s death. Finding concrete evidence here linking his demise to [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] (directly or indirectly via [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]) could be valuable leverage with [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)|Nine-String]].
* Evidence of the [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reaper]] attack confirms the direct threat from [[Rhea]]'s agents.
* Likely location where Alice stored the bulk of her [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] investigation notes (provided by [[Agent Chrysalis]]), which she was reviewing before the Reaper attack forced her to flee.
* May contain residual clues from Alice's earlier investigations or personal items.
* Protected by [[The Hexacle|Hexacles]] or similar defenses (implied by future trajectory needing F7 to navigate).

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Abandoned by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]. Site of past violence (Dylan's death, Reaper attack). Requires [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] exposure to navigate safely according to future trajectory. Potential investigation site for players seeking leverage or further clues about Alice/Dylan/BSSA/Reapers. Site of planned Reaper confrontation.
