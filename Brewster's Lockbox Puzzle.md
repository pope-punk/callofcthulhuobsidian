## Overview
A multi-stage investigative puzzle encountered in Act II. Solving it allows the players to locate [[Maria Chen]] by uncovering a four-digit passphrase [[David Brewster]] created using [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] technology before his death.

## The Goal
To obtain a physical lockbox entrusted by [[David Brewster]] to a trusted friend (e.g., [[Professor Vance]]). The lockbox contains crucial contact information for [[Maria Chen]].

## Required Components
1.  **[[Brewster's Final Segment Sequence Log]]**: Found at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. This document details the specific order of the six segments in Brewster's final, unaired "Axon and Dendrite" episode. (The *Known Output*).
2.  **[[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]]**: Found at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. This document explains the logic of the SDEC tool provided to Brewster, detailing how a specific **four-digit code** dictates the permutation of the six standard broadcast segments. (The *Rules/Algorithm*).
3.  **The Lockbox Holder:** Identifying and locating the trusted friend (e.g., [[Professor Vance]]) whom Brewster entrusted with the physical lockbox. (The *Keeper*).

## Solution Process
1.  **Acquire Clues:** Investigators must find both the Segment Sequence Log and the Randomizer Mapping Rules at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]].
2.  **Reverse Engineer:** Apply the logic described in the Mapping Rules *in reverse* to the known Segment Sequence from the log. This requires careful deduction to determine the *single unique four-digit code* that Brewster must have used to generate that specific sequence.
3.  **Locate the Keeper:** Find the person Brewster entrusted with the lockbox (likely through clues found alongside the puzzle components, related documents, or perhaps information from [[Maria Chen]] herself later).
4.  **Provide Passphrase:** Present the deduced four-digit code to the Keeper. Recognizing the code as the proof Brewster required (verifying understanding of the SDEC system, thus likely not BSSA), the Keeper yields the lockbox.
5.  **Obtain Reward:** Open the lockbox to find [[Maria Chen]]'s contact details and potentially other final messages from Brewster.

## Narrative Significance
* **Grounded Puzzle:** Integrates historical lore ([[Axon and Dendrite]], [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]], [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]], [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]) into a tangible investigative challenge.
* **Character Motivation:** Reflects [[David Brewster]]'s paranoia and desperate attempt to secure vital information from BSSA using methods only SDEC allies might understand.
* **Gates Progress:** Provides a logical pathway to locating [[Maria Chen]], crucial for furthering the investigation into [[Operation Colossus]] and BSSA's past actions.
