## Overview
Nigel "Nine-String" Christmas, born in 1932, is the current and long-standing leader of the [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|Blue Note Jazz Collective]], a key [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations|Mirror-Breaker Organization]]. Operating from the clandestine [[BNJC HQ]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]], Nine-String is a seasoned veteran in the shadow war against [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONINs]] and infohazardous threats. He embodies the improvisational, metaphor-driven ethos of the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] and serves as a primary, albeit cautious, ally and information source for the investigators.

## A Legacy of Loss and Dedication
Nigel Christmas's life has been profoundly shaped by the phenomena his organization combats. His father, [[Andrew Christmas]], an FBI Assistant Director, was killed during the [[CALLISTO (1949)]] event. His mother, [[Hannah Christmas]], was the founder and first Director of the [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]], and she too was killed in the line of duty during the [[GANYMEDE (1979)]] disaster. This personal history of loss has fueled his dedication to the cause, leading him to become the leader of BNJC after IHRU's dissolution and the subsequent formation of the Mirror-Breaker network.

He is deeply knowledgeable about the history of RONINs, infohazards, the legacy of IHRU, and the methodologies of the Mirror-Breaker organizations. His leadership style reflects the core tenets of the Protocol: jazz-based metaphorical communication, compartmentalization, and strategic unpredictability.

## Navigating Treachery and Supporting Assets
Nine-String operates within a complex and treacherous landscape of inter-agency rivalries and existential threats.
* He was aware of [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice's]] covert surveillance of [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] prior to Dylan's death in July 1997.
* When Alice went rogue after her F7 exposure, Nine-String made the difficult decision to support her covertly, assigning [[Agent Chrysalis]] as her handler and secure communication link.
* He used BNJC resources to suppress the official police investigation into Alice's F7-induced actions.
* Following the investigators' return from [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU]] around October 10th, 1997, Nine-String introduced [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)|Agent Shutter]] into the BNJC's Sequoia Falls operation, unaware that Shutter was a mole for [[NOPG (New Orleans Photography Group)|NOPG]] and, by extension, [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]].

### What Nine-String Knows (and Does Not) About Montreal
Nine-String is the only BNJC officer who knows that **Éric Blanc was Alice's longtime EUROPA-era contact** and that Alice sent Syd to Blanc in July 1997. He does **not** know:
* Blanc's apartment address (Alice never gave it to him; their channel ran through Alice).
* That Blanc and Alice assembled and stored a PCBOT-bypass methodology on Blanc's hard drive.
* That Blanc was captured (rather than killed or merely silent).

As of the players' return from COTU, Nine-String has lost contact with Alice (via Chrysalis), the burner phone in Syd's possession has gone dead, and he cannot raise Blanc through any old channel. His default move will be to direct the players to find Alice — both to cure her and to recover whatever institutional memory she carries — and to use [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)|UMSE]] field support to escort them in Montreal once they have an address. He cannot give them Blanc's address; that has to come from [[Maria Chen]] via [[Brewster's Lockbox]].

## Current Status and Planned Interactions (Late October 1997)
Nigel is active and leading BNJC from their Sequoia Falls headquarters. The investigators have recently debriefed him on their COTU findings, including the [[Montreal Phone Number]]. Unbeknownst to him, this information has already been compromised by Agent Shutter.

In the immediate future, Nine-String will:
* **Confirm Leads:** Acknowledge the significance of the [[Montreal Phone Number]], reveal Éric Blanc as Alice's primary contact in Montreal, and admit he has no current address or working channel for Blanc.
* **Direct Toward Chen:** Suggest that any operational path to Blanc has to come through someone Alice had compartmentalized away from BNJC — pointing the investigators (perhaps obliquely, perhaps not) toward [[Maria Chen]] and the Brewster lockbox lead.
* **Reveal SDEC Importance:** Confirm that the sealed [[SDEC Facility]] holds vital information (including [[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)|Winston's memoirs]]) and that the facility is sealed under the "Ant Trap" protocol. He will note that Chrysalis was once the only known person who might bypass the seal, but is now missing.
* **Task the Investigators:** With grave concern, Nine-String will task the investigators with finding the F7-impaired Alice — both to cure her and to extract what she knows about Chrysalis's plans and contingencies. He has no idea yet that Alice's mind is itself the operational key.

This briefing is likely to be overheard in part or whole by Agent Shutter, further compromising BNJC's objectives — though Shutter's most damaging leak (the Montreal phone number) is already in BSSA's hands.

Nine-String is a figure of cautious wisdom and deep experience, a spymaster playing a dangerous game with incredibly high stakes. He holds many crucial pieces of the puzzle but releases information judiciously, testing the loyalty and competence of those he chooses to trust.
