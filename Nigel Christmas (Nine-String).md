## Overview
Nigel "Nine-String" Christmas, born in 1932, is the current and long-standing leader of the [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|Blue Note Jazz Collective]], a key [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations|Mirror-Breaker Organization]]. Operating from the clandestine [[BNJC HQ]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]], Nine-String is a seasoned veteran in the shadow war against [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONINs]] and infohazardous threats. He embodies the improvisational, metaphor-driven ethos of the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] and serves as a primary, albeit cautious, ally and information source for the investigators.

## A Legacy of Loss and Dedication
Nigel Christmas's life has been profoundly shaped by the very phenomena his organization combats. His father, [[Andrew Christmas]], an FBI Assistant Director, was killed during the [[CALLISTO (1949)]] event. His mother, [[Hannah Christmas]], was the founder and first Director of the [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|InfoHazard Response Unit (IHRU)]], and she too was killed in the line of duty during the [[GANYMEDE (1979)]] disaster. This personal history of loss has undoubtedly fueled his dedication to the cause, leading him to become the leader of BNJC after IHRU's dissolution and the subsequent formation of the Mirror-Breaker network.

He is deeply knowledgeable about the history of RONINs, the intricacies of infohazards, the legacy of IHRU, and the diverse methodologies of the various Mirror-Breaker organizations. His leadership style reflects the core tenets of the Protocol: an emphasis on jazz-based metaphorical communication, compartmentalization, and strategic unpredictability.

## Navigating Treachery and Supporting Assets
Nine-String operates within a complex and often treacherous landscape of inter-agency rivalries and existential threats.
* He was aware of [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond's]] covert surveillance of [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] prior to Dylan's death in July 1997.
* When Alice went rogue after her [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]] exposure, Nine-String made the difficult decision to support her covertly, assigning [[Agent Chrysalis]] as her handler and secure communication link. This was a calculated risk, recognizing Alice's potential value despite her compromised state.
* He used BNJC resources to suppress the official police investigation into Alice's F7-induced actions (such as the murders of Sarah Prescott and Thomas Reeves), likely having his own doubts about her sanity but prioritizing the larger mission and containment of information.
* Following the investigators' return from the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|Center of the Universe (COTU)]] around October 10th, 1997, Nine-String made the fateful decision to introduce [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)|Agent Shutter]] into the BNJC's Sequoia Falls operation, unaware that Shutter was a mole for the [[NOPG (New Orleans Photography Group)|New Orleans Photography Group]] and, by extension, [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]. This move would have immediate and severe consequences.

## Current Status and Planned Interactions (Late October 1997)
Nigel Christmas is currently active and leading BNJC from their Sequoia Falls headquarters. The investigators have recently debriefed him on their findings at COTU, including the critical [[Montreal Phone Number]]. Unbeknownst to him, this information has already been compromised by Agent Shutter.

In the immediate future (as per the campaign's planned trajectory), Nine-String is expected to:
* **Confirm Leads:** Acknowledge the significance of the [[Montreal Phone Number]], revealing [[Éric Blanc]] as Alice Redmond's primary contact in Montreal and providing contact information (though this information is now dangerously outdated due to Blanc's elimination by BSSA).
* **Reveal SDEC Importance:** Confirm that the sealed [[SDEC Facility]] in San Diego holds vital information (likely [[Winston Foxglove|Winston Foxglove's]] memoirs, as hinted by clues the players may have found, such as [[Oliver Foxglove Emails|Oliver Foxglove's emails]]). He will explain that the facility is sealed by the "Ant Trap" protocol.
* **Identify Chrysalis as Key:** Name [[Agent Chrysalis]], the last surviving field agent of SDEC, as the only known individual potentially capable of bypassing the SDEC seal.
* **Task the Investigators:** Expressing grave concern that Chrysalis is now missing (his last known contact being Alice at COTU before he fled to [[PCBOT Headquarters]]), Nine-String will task the investigators with a high-priority mission: find and secure the F7-impaired Alice Redmond to learn Chrysalis's current whereabouts or any fallback plans he might have shared with her. This crucial briefing, outlining the path towards SDEC, is likely to be overheard in part or in whole by Agent Shutter, further compromising BNJC's objectives.

Nine-String is a figure of cautious wisdom and deep experience, a spymaster playing a dangerous game with incredibly high stakes. He holds many crucial pieces of the puzzle but releases information judiciously, testing the loyalty and competence of those he chooses to trust. His actions and the information he provides (or withholds) will significantly shape the investigators' path forward.
