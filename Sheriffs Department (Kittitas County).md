## Overview
The local law enforcement agency responsible for Kittitas County, Washington, which includes the city of [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]] and the location of the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] facility.

## Status & Allegiance (GM Note)
* **Compromised by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]**: According to authorial intent, the entire department is compromised or heavily influenced by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]], likely through embedded personnel like [[Bruce Kennedy]] (a known [[Hammerskull Crew]] member) and potentially compromised leadership cultivated during BSSA's [[Operation Colossus]] presence in the area.
* **Implications:** This means any official investigations originating from this department regarding events at [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] (like disappearances caused by the [[The Mannequin Spider|Mannequin Spider]]) are likely to be suppressed, misdirected, or reported directly to [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] / [[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill]] rather than pursued impartially. This explains why disappearances might not have triggered effective external scrutiny.
