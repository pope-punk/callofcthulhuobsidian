## Overview
The United States Central Intelligence Agency. Plays a significant background role influencing events related to [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations|Mirror-Breaker Organizations]] and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].

## Known Activities & Influence
* **Historical Operations:**
    * Ran **Operation POUTINE** (North American numbers stations network, including relays in [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]] and [[Montreal, QC]]) from 1970s until decommissioning in 1986. Infrastructure later repurposed by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] for [[Operation Colossus]].
    * Maintained connections with **neo-Nazi organizations** (like the [[Hammerskull Crew]]) as part of potential "domestic GLADIO" programs, possibly using them for drug trafficking to fund covert operations (1960s-1980s).
* **Influence on Mirror-Breaker Orgs:**
    * Seems to exert influence via [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]], particularly after assisting [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)]]'s coup in 1994.
    * Likely supports Caraway's goal of consolidating Mirror-Breaker agencies under centralized PCBOT/CIA control, dismantling the original [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]'s compartmentalization.
    * Provided psychological warfare techniques that formed the basis for [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s [[Operation Colossus]].
    * Assassinated [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Jacqui Lopez]]'s government ally, Robert Toomey, in mid-1994.

## Status
* Active and influential behind the scenes, primarily through its connections to [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]] and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]. Represents a force pushing for centralized control and potentially weaponization of infohazards.
