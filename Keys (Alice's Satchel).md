## Overview
A set of keys recovered by the investigators from the satchel dropped by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] during her escape from the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]] confrontation on October 7th, 1997.

## Keys Included
The set includes keys belonging to:
* **[[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]:** Likely includes keys to her apartment ([[Alice Redmond's House]]) and potentially other personal locks or vehicles.
* **[[Sean Fielding]]:** Includes keys to his house ([[Sean Fielding's House]]), his car (found at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]]), and crucially, his **[[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] employee keycard** and a key to the **[[White Stag Bar]]**.
* **[[Thomas Reeves]]:** Includes keys to his apartment (Lakeview Place #203) and his Nissan car.
* **[[Sarah Prescott]]:** Includes keys to her apartment (881 3rd Ave NE) and her Toyota car.
* **[[BNJC HQ]] Elevator Key?:** Possibly includes the saxophone key or another item used to access the [[BNJC HQ]] elevator (code 13762). *(Confirm if this was separate or part of the satchel).*

## Significance
* Provided investigators with immediate access to multiple key locations ([[Sean Fielding's House]], victim apartments, potentially [[White Stag Bar]], [[LTX Headquarters]] second floor via Sean's keycard).
* Directly linked [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] to the victims whose keys she possessed.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* In the possession of the player investigators. Have likely been used to access several locations already. Sean's keys remain relevant for accessing the White Stag and potentially revisiting LTX.
