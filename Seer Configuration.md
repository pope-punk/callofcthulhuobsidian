## Overview

The Seer Configuration is what happens when [[Royal Jelly]] priming is followed by a *Type B* [[Traumatic Activation]] — an overwhelming exposure to pattern or systemic horror, rather than chaos or systemic collapse. The de-repressed cognition resolves toward [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|Metaphor Pole]] dominance instead of System Pole dominance. The result is the structural inverse of the [[Scions|Foxglove Configuration]]: no conscious access to [[The Foxglove Algorithm]], but immunity to the basilisks that operate by exploiting Algorithm-linked vulnerabilities. The mind has fled the channel the basilisks use.

[[Katherine Hill]] is the only confirmed modern example.

## How a Seer is made

* **Priming.** Royal Jelly, same as for a Scion. Lifts the genetic repression.
* **Activation.** Type B trauma — direct exposure to a major basilisk, or to a system whose alien logic is itself overwhelming. The mind, rather than seeking to master order (as a Type A activation would), retreats from order into intuition, analogy, fractured improvisation.

Katherine was primed in childhood (around 1959, by a Rhea Editor in Uttarakhand) and activated in 1979 by Black Coral exposure during the [[GANYMEDE (1979)|GANYMEDE]] collapse. The activation was almost certainly not what Rhea intended — Katherine had been kept in reserve as a possible backup Scion-line in case Winston's family failed, and the wrong trauma type came along first.

## Characteristics

* **Pattern resistance.** Immune to [[The Hexacle]] and to [[Black Coral]]. Strongly resistant to other basilisks. Her cognition no longer has the vulnerability they target.
* **Metaphor Pole dominance, disrupted.** Intuitive, non-linear, leaping. Often inspired; just as often paranoid, fragmented, or delusional. Useful conclusions emerge from a process the conclusion-haver cannot reliably explain.
* **No Algorithm access.** She cannot do what the Foxgloves do. Her own technical work (Operation Parthenon, Project Ozymandias, Fauxflake) is done through her System Pole instrumentally, not through any direct Algorithm fluency. This is exactly why she needs Syd.
* **Polyglot facility.** Katherine speaks seven languages. Metaphor-pole structural fluency expressed at the level of natural language.
* **Underlying psychosis.** Pre-existing in Katherine's case; her Seer activation amplified it without crossing the line into incapacitation. Her judgment is preternaturally good and intermittently catastrophically wrong.

## Rhea's read on Seers

Rhea did not design for this outcome and does not cultivate it. From its position, Seers are unstable, unpredictable byproducts of its [[Royal Jelly]] program. It may opportunistically use them (their pattern immunity is genuinely useful to its agents), but its long-term plan is for Scions.

This matters because Katherine doesn't know any of this. She believes she's exceptional, ascendant, and that her ambition for IO is hers. The cosmic horror at her shoulder is that she is the equivalent of a corrupted printer cartridge — a tolerated defect in a process designed to produce something else.

## Significance

The Seer is the campaign's wildcard configuration: not aligned with Rhea, not aligned with the Mirror-Breaker network, not even consciously aware of being a configuration. Her own self-understanding is intact and entirely wrong. She is competent enough to drive [[Operation Parthenon]] toward [[IO (Emerging 1997)]] essentially single-handed, and she is poised to be one of the campaign's two principal antagonists alongside Caraway.
