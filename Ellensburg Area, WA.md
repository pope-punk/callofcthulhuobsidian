## Overview
A location east of the Cascade mountains in Washington state, significant for its connection to [[Winston Foxglove]], [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]], [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s past operations, and [[Rhea]]'s primary local Hive entrance.

## Key Sites
* **[[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] Facility:** Founded by [[Winston Foxglove]], expanded by [[Oliver Foxglove]]. The source of [[Signal Dummies]], GenAssist/[[Spectator Grubs]], and the DNA-conversion tech. Key node in [[Rhea]]'s network. Site of potential [[The Mannequin Spider|Mannequin Spider]] and covered-up disappearances managed by the compromised [[Sheriffs Department (Kittitas County)]].
* **[[Dead Horse Quarry]]**: Formerly Harrison Gold Prospect. Site of [[Winston Foxglove]]'s 1922 encounter with [[Rhea]] and receiving "Royal Jelly". Location of the primary entrance to [[Rhea]]'s local Hive network. Protected by [[The Hexacle|Hexacles]]. Potential endgame location.
* **[[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]**: Abandoned former [[CIA]] Operation POUTINE relay station. Used by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] (1987-1992) to broadcast the [[The Echo]] basilisk for [[Operation Colossus]] and the "Axon and Dendrite" show, leading to the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN. Equipment hastily removed in 1992. Contains remnants/evidence of broadcasts.
* **Former [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] Site:** Location in Ellensburg used as [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s front for [[Operation Colossus]] (1986-1992). Hub for early experiments and site of [[David Brewster]]'s execution. Likely sanitized/abandoned by BSSA in 1992.

## Significance
Crucial historical location tied to the Foxglove Algorithm's origin, the founding of ErgoMatic, BSSA's early basilisk research (Colossus/EUROPA), and the primary entrance to Rhea's Hive. Site of Rhea's active technological manufacturing base.
