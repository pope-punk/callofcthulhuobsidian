## Project Ozymandias

**Tags:** #Operation #BSSA #F7 #G5 #ChemicalWarfare #HumanExperimentation #SaintAnselms

## Overview
[[Project Ozymandias]] is the codename for the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]'s primary and ongoing chemical research program. Centered at the hidden laboratories within the [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]], its main focus is the development, refinement, testing, and potential weaponization of psychoactive compounds, most notably [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] and its flawed antidote [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]]. The project also serves as a critical testing ground for the effects of various [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]] on human subjects, including the 2D [[Fauxflake]] diagrams derived from [[Operation Parthenon]].

## The Chemical Front in an Information War
The philosophical underpinning of [[Project Ozymandias]] lies in BSSA's, particularly [[Katherine Hill]]'s, conviction that purely cognitive or protocol-based defenses against infohazards (like the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]) are insufficient. Instead, BSSA believes in the necessity of direct chemical intervention to modulate human cognitive vulnerabilities, protect their agents, and potentially develop new forms of influence or control. [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] is the cornerstone of this chemical strategy.

## The Heart of the Project - F7 and its Kin
The primary achievements and focal points of [[Project Ozymandias]] are:
* **[[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]:** A heavily modified LSD derivative, F7 is designed to suppress the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]] of human cognition, thereby granting users a degree of immunity to pattern-based [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]]. Its development was likely built upon earlier research into a prototype called HFD during [[Operation Colossus]].
* **[[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]]:** Recognizing the severe cognitive and psychological side effects of F7, BSSA developed G5 as an antidote. While G5 does counteract F7's primary effects by stimulating the System Pole, it was found to cause fatal cumulative gallium neurotoxicity, leading to the cessation of its production.
Lead chemical research for these compounds is conducted by Agent Chisel ([[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)]]).

## Testing Grounds - Saint Anselm's Center for Hope
The [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]], under the public guidance of [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]], provides the perfect cover for [[Project Ozymandias]]'s unethical human experimentation. Homeless individuals are recruited (often coercively) through the shelter front and subjected to a battery of tests:
* Exposure to varying dosages of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] and [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]].
* Controlled exposure to various known [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]], including samples of [[Black Coral]], recordings of [[The Echo]], and, significantly, the **2D [[Fauxflake]] pattern diagrams** produced by [[Operation Parthenon]]. This allows BSSA to assess F7's efficacy and the potency of their own developing infohazards.

## Goals and Ambitions
[[Project Ozymandias]] serves multiple strategic objectives for BSSA:
* To develop and mass-produce reliable chemical countermeasures like [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] for their own agents.
* To create effective (though currently flawed) antidotes like [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]].
* To systematically study the effects of [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]] and F7 on human cognition.
* To explore the potential for weaponizing F7 or similar compounds for cognitive influence or control.
* To supply [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] and related research to allied factions, such as [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]] and the [[CIA]], for military or intelligence applications.

## Consequences and Current Status (Late October 1997)
[[Project Ozymandias]] has had tangible and often tragic consequences. While BSSA successfully developed and produces [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]], their antidote G5 proved lethally flawed. The unethical release of F7-dosed test subjects from Saint Anselm's directly led to the formation of the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU (Center of the Universe)]] community. The subsequent theft of pure F7 by [[Calvin Pierce]] from the [[White Stag Bar]] (an early Ozymandias chemical site) and its distribution at COTU resulted in mass poisoning and death.

Despite these "setbacks" (from BSSA's perspective), [[Project Ozymandias]] remains an active and critical BSSA operation. Research, synthesis, and testing continue at [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]], representing BSSA's primary chemical warfare and defense program. [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]] was briefly reassigned to testing oversight here between June and July 1997.

## Significance
[[Project Ozymandias]] highlights BSSA's ruthless pragmatism and their deviation from [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] principles. It is a direct source of conflict and suffering within the campaign narrative and provides a stark contrast to other factions' approaches to infohazardous threats. The chemicals it produces are central to several plotlines, including [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s condition and the events at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]].
