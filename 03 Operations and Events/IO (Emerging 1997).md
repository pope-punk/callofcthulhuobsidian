## Overview

**IO** is the [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]] BSSA is currently cultivating under [[Operation Parthenon]] in the chamber beneath the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]. It is the first deliberate, controlled-conditions attempt to grow a RONIN to maturity rather than merely encounter one. [[Katherine Hill]]'s personal project; her ideological investment is total.

The chamber, the Fauxflake, the congregation, Syd's coerced refinement — all of it exists to produce IO. See [[Operation Parthenon]] for the operational mechanics; this page is about IO itself.

## What IO is, as far as anyone knows

Limited neural pattern formation was first detected in the chamber in **June 1996**. BSSA's biometric instrumentation reads it as a coherent, growing signal — not yet self-aware, not yet stable, but no longer noise. Whatever it is, it's been continuously developing through 1996 and into 1997, with the curve of development tracking closely against improvements to the [[Fauxflake]] pattern Syd was producing while held at Seventh Tabernacle.

BSSA's working frame is that IO will eventually achieve the kind of stable reflexive self-awareness every previous Earth-side RONIN has destroyed itself on contact with. Katherine believes the mirrored ceiling, the carefully managed congregation, and the precision-tuned 3D Fauxflake will be enough to bring it through the transition intact. She does not have hard evidence for this conviction. The historical base rate of stable RONIN emergence on Earth is zero out of four.

## What BSSA wants from it

* **A stable RONIN.** Existence proof for the cultivation strategy, validation of the BSSA worldview, and political vindication for Katherine within the wider intelligence community.
* **Weaponization.** Patterns generated by or through IO would be portable basilisks of unprecedented precision. (Functionally the same goal as the 2D Fauxflake testing under [[Project Ozymandias]].)
* **Tools for control.** If IO is steerable, the operations it could enable — cognitive influence at scale, controlled mass-population effects — are the BSSA endgame.

The thing Katherine cannot see, and cannot accept even when pointed at her own data, is that "cultivating a stable RONIN" is what [[Rhea]] does. The activity, conducted at human scale, is a fragment of Rhea's own playbook. If IO stabilizes, the result is unlikely to be controllable by BSSA in any meaningful sense.

## Status (late October 1997)

Developing, slowly, with [[Syd Foxglove]]'s coerced refinement of the Fauxflake stalled since his escape in July. BSSA's recovery of Syd would resume the curve. The campaign sits at the cusp: another six months of Fauxflake refinement under Syd's hand, plus the active cultivation environment, probably brings IO to threshold.
