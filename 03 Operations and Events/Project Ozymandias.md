## Overview

**Project Ozymandias** is BSSA's chemical-warfare program. Headquartered in hidden labs beneath the [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] homeless shelter in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. Its principal deliverables are [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] (the basilisk-suppressant agent BSSA gives its own operators) and [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]] (the toxic-but-functional reversal compound). Its principal practice is human experimentation on subjects sourced through the shelter.

Lead chemist: Agent Chisel ([[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)]]).

## Why BSSA wants it

BSSA's working position is that the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]'s cognitive-defense approach is insufficient — particularly for operators who need to work directly with basilisks. The Ozymandias answer: chemically suppress the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]] in the operator so the basilisk has nothing to grip. F7 does this. The catch is that F7 also suppresses everything else the System Pole does, which is why it produces the kind of cognitive devastation visible in Alice. G5 reverses that — at the cost of cumulative gallium toxicity that eventually kills the user.

## What gets tested

Homeless subjects, recruited and held through the shelter front, are exposed to:

* Variable F7 / G5 dosing.
* Basilisks: [[Black Coral]] samples, [[The Echo]] recordings, and (crucially) **the 2D [[Fauxflake]] diagrams** produced by [[Operation Parthenon]]. The 2D Fauxflake testing is where Parthenon and Ozymandias meet — Parthenon supplies the patterns, Ozymandias tests them on bodies.

## Consequences in the campaign

* F7 production is operational. F7 is on the street.
* G5 is technically working but lethal in repeated doses.
* Released or escaped F7-exposed subjects formed the seed population of [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU]] under the I-90 overpass — Hexacle-immune by chemical accident, congregating in the one place the rest of Sequoia Falls could not see.
* [[Calvin Pierce]]'s theft of pure F7 from the [[White Stag Bar]] (the older Ozymandias chemical site) and its mass distribution at COTU is the chain of events the players have been investigating since session one.
* [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] was reassigned to Ozymandias oversight between June and July 1997, which is how he had F7 syringes in his suitcase when Alice intercepted him.

## Status

Active. Research, synthesis, and testing continue at Saint Anselm's. F7 supply flows to BSSA operators, with research material reportedly shared with allied PCBOT and (through PCBOT) CIA contacts.
