## Overview
[[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s flawed, first-generation antidote for [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]. Designed to counteract F7's suppression of the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]].

## Chemical Nature & Mechanism
* **Type:** Likely a complex amphetamine derivative stabilized or potentiated by Gallium salts.
* **Mechanism:** Operates primarily on **dopaminergic pathways**. Aims to **restore or enhance the [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]]** (pattern recognition, logical processing) suppressed by F7's serotonergic action. Interacts with the baseline human Dialectic.

## Effects
* **Primary Effect:** Reverses the cognitive impairment and pattern suppression caused by [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]. Restores/enhances System Pole function.
* **Fatal Side Effect:** Causes **cumulative, fatal gallium neurotoxicity** with repeated use. Gallium builds up in the brain, leading to severe neurological damage and death.

## History & Status
* Developed by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] under [[Project Ozymandias]] ([[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)]]) as a necessary countermeasure for [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]].
* Production **halted** due to lethal toxicity (before July 1997).
* Its failure motivated [[Syd Foxglove]]'s research into the non-toxic [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]].
* Samples may exist at [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] facilities or [[PCBOT Headquarters]].

## Campaign Significance
* Represents BSSA's flawed chemical approach. Highlights danger to [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]. Provides motivation for [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]]. Interacts with the core [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|Cognitive Dialectic]].
