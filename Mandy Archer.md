## Description

Mandy Archer is described as the on-again/off-again fling of [[Sean Fielding]].

## Known Information & Connections

* **Relationship with Sean Fielding**: Identified as Sean's romantic interest. She left voicemails for him which were found on his answering machine by the investigators.
* **Personality & Family**: Perceived by the player investigators as potentially nihilistic and impulsive. She has a controlling father with whom [[Pastor Mike]] seemed to get along surprisingly well.
* **Connection to Investigation**: Players identified her as possibly the last person to see Sean Fielding alive (before his final disappearance/death). They arranged a meeting with her under the cover of interviewing for a right-wing magazine, having dealt with her controlling father first. The specific outcome or information gained from this arranged meeting is not detailed in the provided recap documents. They ended up meeting her at [[The Spigot]].

## Status
