## Overview
Older research notes created by [[Oliver Foxglove]] (likely pre-December 1996) detailing his personal cipher method. Found at the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]].

## Content & Process
These notes explain the *process* Oliver used for encoding dates into keypad codes, but *do not* contain the specific code for his home safe. The process involves:

1.  **Start with a Base-10 Date:** (e.g., Month/Day/Year).
2.  **Convert to [[Ylynthii Numerals]]:** Convert the Base-10 date into the Ylynthii base number system (Base-X, where X is likely revealed by studying Syd's cell markings - see [[Ylynthii Numerals]]). Requires understanding Ylynthii symbols and base conversion rules.
3.  **Map to Keypad:** Map the resulting Ylynthii digits to standard 0-9 keypad digits using a specific substitution scheme defined in Oliver's notes.

## Significance
* Provides the **methodology** required to solve the [[Safe Puzzle]].
* Links the puzzle to the alien **[[Ylynthii Numerals]]** system, reinforcing the cosmic origins of the Algorithm.

## Role in Campaign
* Found during the investigation of the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]] (likely in Oliver's old research files or Syd's workstation area).
* Must be combined with Syd's birthday (from [[Oliver Foxglove Emails]]) and the rules of [[Ylynthii Numerals]] (learned from Syd's BSSA cell) to derive the final safe code.
