
**Tags:** #Location #SequoiaFalls #InvestigationSite

## Overview
The House formerly inhabited by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] before she went rogue. 
## Layout & Features
* Thickly dust-covered (unused since July 1997).
* Decorated with shrouded statues.
* Contains a hidden vault (combination: 0.083).
* Extensive ventilation system.

## Key Discoveries (Made by Players)
* **Vault Contents:**
    * [[CALLISTO Report]]
    * [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol Notes]]
    * [[Alice's Map]]
    * [[Spectator Grubs]] preserved in vials.
    * Passports, cash, dossier on [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]].
* **General Apartment:** Books on jazz, philosophy, cognitive science. Saxophone.

## Key Events
* **Reaper Attack (Oct 8, 1997):** A [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reaper]] was hiding in the vents and attacked the investigators and [[Colin Reeves]], killing Detective Reeves.

## Significance
* Provided players with crucial initial lore dumps ([[CALLISTO Report]], [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol Notes]]) and investigative leads ([[Alice's Map]]).
* Established the direct physical threat posed by [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reapers]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Investigated by players. Site of [[Colin Reeves]]' death. Likely under police scrutiny or secured after the incident.
