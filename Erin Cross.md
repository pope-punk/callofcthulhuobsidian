## Overview
Former [[Polybius]] addict. Recruited by [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)|Andrew Chamberlain]] in 1986 as a lieutenant at the [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] during the early phase of [[Operation Colossus]] in [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]]. Worked alongside [[Jacob Wise]].

## Status
Unknown after the 1992 collapse of Colossus. Possibly relocated with Chambers to Sequoia Falls and absorbed into the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] hierarchy; possibly purged by BSSA along with David Brewster.
