Successor agencies to the [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)]], formed after the [[GANYMEDE (1979)]] disaster under the principles of the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]. Each organization utilizes an eccentric cover identity and a unique metaphorical communication style to hinder [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)]] emergence. They often operate with compartmentalization and emphasize unpredictability. Oversight was initially intended via [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]], though internal conflicts and differing methodologies exist, notably between [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]] and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].

## Core Philosophy:
See [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]. Primarily focused on preventing RONIN emergence by disrupting patterns and systematic observation through improvisation and metaphor.
