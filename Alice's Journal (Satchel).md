## Overview
A journal recovered by the investigators from the satchel dropped by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] during her escape from the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]] confrontation on October 7th, 1997. Reflects her deteriorating mental state under the influence of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]].

## Content
* **Crossed-Out Names:** Lists names, many crossed out. Includes the names of [[Signal Dummies]] she destroyed (e.g., "Thermos Milkshaft") alongside the names of human victims she killed, mistaking them for Dummies ([[Sarah Prescott]], [[Thomas Reeves]], likely [[Sean Fielding]]). Demonstrates her inability to differentiate due to F7.
* **Notes & Rants:** Contains fragmented notes, potentially related to her investigation of the "LTX RONIN," [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]], [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]], or [[Rhea]]'s network, filtered through her paranoid and pattern-suppressed perspective. May include references to [[The Hexacle]] (perceived as meaningless shapes) or her struggle to understand the patterns she's encountering.
* **China Brain Page (Example):** May contain philosophical musings or diagrams related to [[Consciousness & Emergence|consciousness, emergence, or distributed intelligence]] (like the "China Brain" thought experiment), reflecting her attempts to understand the phenomenon she's fighting.

## Significance
* Provides direct insight into Alice's compromised mental state and motivations.
* Confirms her actions in tracking and "destroying" targets, linking her to victims.
* May contain fragmented clues about the [[Signal Dummies|Dummy]] network or her understanding of [[Rhea]]'s operations, if deciphered carefully.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* In the possession of the player investigators. Key source for understanding Alice's perspective and actions.
