## Overview
Copies of [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] shipping manifests documenting the pattern of "dirt" shipments. Copied secretly by [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]] before his death.

## Content
* Lists numerous shipments of inert materials (potting soil, gravel) sent via [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]].
* Recipients are clearly fake/absurd names ("Thermos Milkshaft," "Quentin Cinnabon," "Alphonse Spicecube," etc.).
* Destinations are often abandoned offices, storage units, or residential addresses (like [[Sean Fielding's House]]).
* Represents the activation pattern of [[Rhea]]'s technological network, with the named recipients being [[Signal Dummies]].

## History & Significance
* Discovered by [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]] in Spring 1997 while monitoring LTX. His findings were suppressed by [[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill]].
* Dylan copied the manifests before his credentials were revoked (June 1997).
* Taken from Dylan's body by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] after she killed him (July 1997).
* Alice used these manifests to track and destroy [[Signal Dummies]], believing them to be part of a new [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]].

## Location & Status (Oct 1997)
* Currently in the possession of [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]. Recovering these manifests could provide players with direct evidence of the [[Signal Dummies|Dummy]] network and locations.
