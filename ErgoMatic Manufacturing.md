
**Tags:** #Faction #Corporation

## Overview
A technology manufacturing company founded by [[Winston Foxglove]] in 1953 in [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]], after his ouster from [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]]. Became central to the [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] tech boom under [[Oliver Foxglove]]'s leadership.

## History
* **Founding (1953):** Established by Winston. He created a list of key future technologies ([[Winston's 1953 Research List]]) inspired by his [[Rhea]] encounter, which unknowingly guided the company's future.
* **Oliver's Leadership (1981-1997):** Oliver inherited the company and [[The Foxglove Algorithm|Algorithm]] aptitude. Established parallel research divisions:
    * **EAS (Robotics/Automation):** Developed prototype humanoid warehouse automatons (became [[Signal Dummies]]).
    * **BAD (Biotech):** Focused on tissue engineering, biological integration. Resulted in [[Syd Foxglove]]'s GenAssist Universal Tissue Printer and Oliver's DNA-machine code conversion system.
* **Merger (1995-1997):** Initiated partnership with [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]], driven by Oliver's ambition and [[Rhea]]'s subtle influence. Oliver discovered the Algorithm recursion and Rhea's plan in Dec 1996, attempted to halt merger. Murdered Jan 1997. Merger completed Jan 15, 1997.

## Role in Rhea's Plan
* Provided the **key technologies** for [[Rhea]]'s transcendence: [[Signal Dummies]], GenAssist (repurposed for [[Spectator Grubs]]), and the DNA-Machine Code Conversion System interface.
* Serves as the **manufacturing platform** for Rhea's network components.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Fully merged with [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]]. Production lines modified by [[Rhea]] post-merger. Contains automated production of Dummies/Grubs. Likely site of emergent [[The Mannequin Spider|Mannequin Spider]] security. Key investigation site for understanding Rhea's mechanics. [[Sheriffs Department (Kittitas County)]] likely compromised by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] to cover up incidents here.
