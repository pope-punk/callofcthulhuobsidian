## Overview

The **Impulse-Class Radiometry Array** is a sophisticated piece of advanced sensory and potentially disruptive technology housed within the secure [[BNJC HQ]] facility in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. It represents a core component of the [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC's]] technical capabilities for monitoring and potentially counteracting infohazardous threats.

## Description & Location

Located deep within the [[BNJC HQ]], the array likely consists of a complex suite of antennas, receivers, signal processors, and powerful emitters, possibly incorporating exotic materials or principles derived from [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]] research or insights gleaned from past [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]] events. Its precise appearance is likely unique, perhaps reflecting BNJC's jazz aesthetic in some abstract way (e.g., resembling resonant chambers, complex "brass" conduits, etc.). It requires significant power and specialized knowledge to operate effectively.

## Primary Function: Passive Detection

* **High-Sensitivity Monitoring:** Designed primarily for the passive detection and analysis of a wide range of electromagnetic signals and potentially more esoteric energy signatures across the [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] region.
* **Anomaly Detection:** Its main purpose is to monitor for signs of emerging [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]] activity, active [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisk]] transmissions (like residual patterns of [[The Echo]]), unusual network traffic patterns (related to [[Rhea]]'s [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]] network), or other anomalous phenomena inconsistent with baseline reality. BNJC likely uses it to maintain situational awareness of infohazardous threats. Data analysis probably involves applying [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] principles to avoid creating dangerous feedback loops.

* **Signal Generation/Interference:** Critically, the array possesses a secondary, **active** function allowing it to generate powerful, focused electromagnetic pulses or complex interfering signals. *(GM Note: This capability MUST be hinted at or revealed to players through clues or NPC dialogue before the PCBOT Excursion)*.
* **Jamming Capability:** This allows the array to be used offensively to disrupt, overload, or "jam" targeted communication frequencies or electronic guidance systems within its operational range.
