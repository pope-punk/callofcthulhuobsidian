## Overview
A major nationwide logistics corporation ($6.8B revenue, 47 states as of 1997), headquartered in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. Originally Foxglove Containers and Transport (est. 1891 by [[Thurston Foxglove]]), relaunched as LTX in 1935 by [[Winston Foxglove]].

## History
* **Winston's Era (1935-1949):** Winston implemented [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] to create the [[The Snowflake]] pattern warehouse layouts, revitalizing the company and saving [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] economically after the [[Dead Horse Quarry]] mine closure.
* **[[CALLISTO (1949)]]:** The LTX network, influenced by the Snowflake pattern, served as the medium for the CALLISTO RONIN emergence, leading to devastating port explosions.
* **Post-CALLISTO:** Winston was publicly disgraced and forced out by the LTX board, creating lasting animosity between the family and the company.
* **American Meridian Ownership:** Became majority-owned by [[American Meridian]], stagnated. The Sequoia Falls branch was maintained largely as a tax write-off, operationally antiquated but strategically located near [[Dead Horse Quarry]]. CEO was [[Martin Kavanaugh]].
* **Merger (1995-1997):** [[Oliver Foxglove]] initiated a strategic partnership driven by a desire to redeem the family name and leverage ErgoMatic tech. LTX saw opportunity for revitalization. Merger pushed through by [[American Meridian]]-appointed CEO [[Richard Harmon]]. [[Oliver Foxglove]] discovered the merger's true implications for [[Rhea]] and tried to stop it, leading to his murder. Merger completed Jan 15, 1997.

## Role in Rhea's Plan
* The merged LTX-ErgoMatic **logistics network** (shipping routes, schedules, warehouses) serves as the primary **neural architecture** for [[Rhea]]'s technological consciousness.
* [[Signal Dummies]] use LTX systems to ship dirt packages, creating activation patterns.
* Site of [[Sean Fielding]]'s infiltration attempt (seeking [[The Snowflake]] documentation for [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]).

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Fully merged with [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]]. Functions as the operational network for [[Rhea]], though most employees remain unaware ("blissfully ignorant"). Key investigation site for players tracing dirt shipments and [[Sean Fielding]]'s activities.
