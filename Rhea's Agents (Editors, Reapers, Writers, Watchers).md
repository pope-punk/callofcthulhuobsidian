## Overview
Rhea's Agents, collectively referred to as **Editors**, are specialized biological or bio-mechanical entities created, directed, or manifested by the planetary consciousness [[Rhea]]. They serve as Rhea's hands and eyes in the world, performing a variety of tasks crucial to its multi-millennial plans, from subtle manipulation and information control to direct intervention and targeted elimination. These agents often appear superficially identical to mundane creatures or even humans, but they invariably possess unique abilities and can reveal Rhea's distinctive hexagonal signature upon closer inspection or when performing their specialized functions. They are likely birthed or manifested from Rhea's subterranean [[Hive]] networks and maintain a constant connection to the primary Rhea consciousness.

## General Characteristics
While diverse in form and function, Rhea's Editors share several common traits:
* **Camouflage and Deception:** Many agents are masters of camouflage, capable of mimicking mundane forms relevant to their operational environment or specific targets. This can range from appearing as common animals to adopting human guises or even resembling inert objects until activated.
* **Hexagonal Unfolding:** A hallmark of Rhea's agents is the presence of [[Hexagonal Patterns|hexagonal structures]] within their physiology. These may be concealed, with body parts unfolding or reconfiguring to reveal intricate hexagonal arrays, tools, orifices, or internal frameworks. This is Rhea's direct signature upon its creations.
* **Hive Connection:** Editors are not independent entities in the way humans understand individuality. They are extensions of Rhea, maintaining a connection to its primary consciousness and the vast network of its Hives. Their actions are typically purpose-driven, dictated by Rhea's overarching goals.
* **Purpose-Driven Design:** Each known type of Editor appears to have been engineered or differentiated for a specific set of functions within Rhea's long-term plans or more immediate operational needs.

## Known Types of Editors and Their Roles

### [[Reapers|Reapers]] - The Eliminators
Reapers are Rhea's specialized assassins, tasked with the elimination of specific threats, inconvenient genetic lines, or individuals who obstruct its plans.
* **Targets:** Their known targets include figures like [[Oliver Foxglove]] (killed to ensure the LTX-ErgoMatic merger proceeded) and individuals who get too close to Rhea's secrets, such as [[Colin Reeves]] or potentially problematic members of the Foxglove lineage throughout history. They have also actively hunted [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]].
* **Appearance & Attack:** Often described as tall, gaunt humanoids or possessing animalistic features (e.g., "elk-like" or "wolf-like"). A common feature is a "honeycomb face" that can unfold, possibly emitting disorienting light flashes (potentially linked to [[Spectator Grubs|Grub]] activation or a defensive dazzler). They employ concealed tentacles ending in sharp, drill-like protrusions for lethal attacks and are capable of extreme violence and stealth.
* **Activity:** Investigators have encountered Reapers directly, witnessing their terrifying efficiency and alien nature.

### [[Writers|Writers]] - The Concealers and Saboteurs
Writers are primarily focused on societal influence, information control, and the active concealment of Rhea's presence and operations.
* **Primary Function:** Their most critical task is the placement of [[The Hexacle]] basilisk in strategic locations (e.g., around [[Hive]] entrances like [[Dead Horse Quarry]], or areas requiring induced amnesia like the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU]] perimeter). They use a black, viscous substance, likely secreted or produced by their bodies, to paint these patterns.
* **Secondary Actions:** Writers are also implicated in more direct acts of sabotage or internal cleansing. The destruction of the [[SDEC Facility|SDEC]] personnel and the triggering of its "Ant Trap" protocol in 1992, after SDEC linked the Quarry anomaly to [[Winston Foxglove|Winston's]] memoirs, was likely the work of a specialized Writer or an [[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)|SDEC Infiltrator]] (a highly specialized form of Writer). They also eliminated [[Calvin Pierce]] at COTU after he fulfilled his purpose in the F7 poisoning.
* **Appearance:** Can likely adopt various mundane forms, including human or animal, to operate unnoticed while performing their tasks.

### [[Watchers|Watchers]] - The Surveyors and Heralds
Watchers are primarily tasked with biological surveillance, data collection, and occasionally, direct communication on behalf of Rhea.
* **Primary Function:** They monitor Earth's biosphere, track genetic drift, and record significant biological data. This is achieved through the deployment and retrieval of organic [[Spectator Grubs]].
* **Grub Interaction:** Watchers possess hexagonal storage cavities within their bodies where they carry dormant organic [[Spectator Grubs]]. They can activate these Grubs and deploy them onto targets or into the environment for data collection. They can also directly deposit these biological Grubs into [[Signal Dummies]] to provide overriding instructions from the Hive.
* **Communication:** Unlike most other Editors, Watchers are capable of direct, complex communication with humans. They possess vast knowledge of Rhea's history, its multi-billion-year plans, and its philosophical justifications. An encounter with a Watcher is planned for the campaign's endgame, where it will deliver Rhea's ultimatum regarding [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] and the future of humanity.
* **Appearance & Defenses:** Can take various organic forms, potentially based on Earthly or Ylynthian fauna. They likely possess defensive capabilities, perhaps related to manipulating local flora and fauna, deploying specialized Grubs, or emitting perceptual distortions.

### Specialized Editors and Other Manifestations
Beyond these primary types, Rhea can likely manifest other, more specialized agents for specific tasks:
* **[[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)|SDEC Infiltrator]]:** A unique, purpose-built agent, possibly a highly specialized Writer, designed specifically to breach the defenses and purge the [[SDEC Facility|SDEC facility]].
* **Royal Jelly Delivery Agents:** Editors have been responsible for administering [[Royal Jelly]] to targeted individuals like [[Winston Foxglove]] and [[Katherine Hill]] at key moments in history. These agents might be purpose-built for such delicate genetic interventions or could be a temporary role adopted by another Editor type.

## Significance
Rhea's Editors are the tangible, physical manifestations of its will and power. They represent a direct and often lethal threat to the investigators and their allies, but also serve as living clues to Rhea's nature, methods, and the alien biology that underpins its existence. Encounters with these agents drive home the personal and visceral horror of Rhea's influence.
