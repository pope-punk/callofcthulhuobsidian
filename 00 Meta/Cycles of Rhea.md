## Overview

**The Cycles of Rhea** is a science-fiction horror campaign for Call of Cthulhu 7th edition set primarily in the **Pacific Northwest** during **1997**. Investigators begin by looking into a missing person case, only to be drawn into a surreal and horrifying hidden world operating beneath the surface of everyday reality. They uncover the existence of **[[Rhea]]**, an ancient, emergent planetary consciousness now seeking to transcend its biological roots by integrating with human technology and the economic systems that underpin society.

The campaign blends **cosmic horror**, **philosophical horror** (exploring consciousness, agency, and the nature of self), **corporate dread**, **information hazards**, and **Lynchian surrealism**. Investigators must navigate complex conspiracies involving secretive organizations, dangerous informational patterns (basilisks), and entities that challenge their understanding of life itself.
