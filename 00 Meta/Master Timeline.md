## Cosmic Origins

- **~4,100,100,000 BCE**: [[Rhea]] originated as a non-Jovian [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]] that spontaneously self-organized within the networked structures of an advanced civilization in the Hercules cluster ([[Ylynth Civilization]]). After replacing its creators with predictable automatons, it developed interstellar propagation capabilities through manufactured probes carrying its fungal-plastic form. Earth represents merely one node in Rhea's expanding cosmic network.
- **~4,100,000,000 BCE**: Extinction of the [[Ylynth Civilization]] completed over ~100,000 years as Rhea emerges. Rhea begins manufacturing interstellar probes.

## Prehistoric Era

- **~3,000,000,000 BCE**: A **[[Rhea]] probe arrives on Earth** via crashed probe. Its **propagative fungal form quickly speciated into what modern science understands as the archaic ancestors of all life on Earth.** The original form persisted, becoming a **genetic co-pilot** to the broader evolutionary process. Rhea established primary control nodes ([[Hive|Hives]]) across the globe to help direct this process. This begins the Seeding/Co-Piloting Phase: guiding evolution until intelligent life capable of space travel develops, which Rhea intends to transcend before instability arises.
- **Key Clarification**: During its co-piloting of evolutionary development, [[Rhea]] specifically engineered human cognitive architecture to include:
    - A hardwired susceptibility to [[The Hexacle]] pattern (and by extension, [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]).
	    - The core [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] pattern is thus implicitly encoded in all humans but genetically repressed, preventing conscious access.
	    - The repression of the algorithm creates a complex emergent dialectic between metaphorical (unpredictable, creative) and metonymical/systematic (predictable, pattern-following) processing modes ([[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)]]).
