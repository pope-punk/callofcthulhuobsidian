- **Mid-1992: EUROPA Disrupted, SDEC Destroyed**
    
    - **Actual Events:** [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] disrupts the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]-created [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]] RONIN by interfering with [[The Echo]] basilisk broadcasts. [[Agent Chrysalis]] reports BSSA Agent [[Raymond Vaughn (Agent Rococo)|Rococo's]] involvement to [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Lopez]] at [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|PCBOT]]. Shortly after, SDEC detects the [[Dead Horse Quarry]] anomaly and links it to [[Winston Foxglove|Winston's]] memoirs. A [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Rhea agent]] infiltrates, eliminates personnel, and triggers the "Ant Trap" containment protocol to silence this discovery.
    - 
    - **Factional Interpretation:** Lopez, knowing of BSSA's role in EUROPA, finds the official narrative (EUROPA destroyed SDEC) highly suspect and pushes to investigate SDEC for evidence/survivors. Caraway, allied with BSSA's [[Katherine Hill]], promotes the official narrative, argues SDEC proves the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] failed, and insists SDEC must remain sealed due to extreme hazard.
    
- **Late 1992 - Mid-1993: Political Maneuvering**
    
    - **Actual Events:** [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]] executes [[David Rubinsky]], abandons/sanitizes Ellensburg sites ([[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]], [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]), blaming EUROPA for SDEC's destruction. [[Katherine Hill]] sees SDEC's fate as proof the Protocol failed. Intense debate occurs within [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|PCBOT]] regarding how to proceed.
    - **Factional Conflict:** Lopez argues for investigating SDEC immediately. Caraway argues for investigating the EUROPA source in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] first to assess risks before considering the SDEC breach.
    
- **Mid-1993: Compromise & New Deployments**
    
    - **Actual Events:** A compromise is reached: SDEC remains sealed for now. BNJC, under Lopez's influence, is relocated to [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] to investigate EUROPA remnants, with [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] (then aligned with Lopez) as liaison. A temporary field office is used while construction begins on the [[BNJC HQ]] facility. [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]] transfers to the new BNJC branch, tasked with tracing EUROPA's technical infrastructure (like the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] and [[Operation POUTINE]] relays). BSSA begins planning [[Operation Parthenon]] and [[Project Ozymandias]].
    - **Factional Outlook:** Lopez secures a foothold for Protocol-adherent investigation near BSSA territory. Caraway accepts the delay on SDEC and likely begins working to control information flow via Dylan Hill and undermine BNJC's independence.
    
- **1993-1994: The EUROPA Investigation & Éric Blanc**
    
    - **Actual Events:** Alice Redmond's official BNJC investigation focuses on EUROPA's technical vectors. She identifies the [[Montreal, QC]] [[Operation POUTINE]] relay connection and leverages [[FBI]] contacts to connect with former Canadian intelligence analyst [[Éric Blanc]], who possesses relevant technical knowledge. Their collaboration deepens, becoming 'off the books' as political tensions rise within PCBOT. They interview [[Chrissy Perkins]] of the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] but find no initial leads due to purged records.
    - **Underlying Currents:** While Alice conducts the official probe, Caraway's faction is consolidating power.
    
- **Mid-Late 1994: The Coup**
    
    - **Actual Events:** Lopez's government ally [[Robert Toomey]] is assassinated by the [[CIA]]. Soon after, [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Caraway]] seizes control of [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|PCBOT]] in a [[CIA]]-assisted coup. Lopez is ousted and sidelined. [[Katherine Hill]] takes a dual BSSA/PCBOT role. The first synthesis of modern [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] is achieved by BSSA.
    - **Factional Resolution:** Caraway's faction wins decisively. Lopez's influence is broken. The official push to investigate SDEC is dead.
    
- **Post-1994 - 1997: Suppression & Surreptitious Investigation**
    - **Actual Events:** With Caraway in control, BNJC's official investigation into EUROPA/BSSA is likely suppressed, defunded, or officially closed. Alice Redmond's access and official channels are hampered. However, [[Éric Blanc]], now operating independently, continues his own unofficial investigation into the [[Operation POUTINE]] network and the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]]. He eventually discovers evidence (equipment purchase orders, payment records) linking the SCAI directly to the reactivation of relays for EUROPA and tracks down survivor [[Cleo Zhang]] in Portland, OR, keeping her location secret at her request. Meanwhile, Alice grows suspicious of [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill's]] filtered LTX data (1996) and starts her own covert inquiries, eventually leading to the fateful events of July 1997.
