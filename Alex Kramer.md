## Description

Alex Kramer is a Supervisor at the [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] Headquarters in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Known Information & Connections

* **Role**: Holds a supervisory position at LTX.
* **Concern about Dirt Shipments**: Notably concerned or "obsessed" with the strange, unexplained shipments of dirt being handled by LTX, which are linked to [[Rhea]]'s network activation via [[Signal Dummies]]. His concern contrasts with the apparent ignorance or indifference of higher management.
