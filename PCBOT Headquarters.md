## Overview

**PCBOT Headquarters** is the central command and control facility for the [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|Pacific Coast Board of Tourism]], located in Washington D.C. While presenting a mundane public face, it conceals a secure, [[CIA]]-influenced blacksite operation under the direction of [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Director Henry Caraway]].

In the campaign's late arc, PCBOT HQ is not interesting in itself — it is interesting because its secure network contains the only viable **remote terminal link** to the sealed [[SDEC Facility]]. Alice Redmond and Éric Blanc identified this and pre-mapped a bypass methodology in 1994-96; that methodology, encrypted on Blanc's hard drive, is what makes the PCBOT operation possible.

## Location & Cover Identity

* **Location:** An unassuming office building in Washington D.C.
* **Cover:** The Pacific Coast Board of Tourism. The public lobby and upper floors maintain a meticulous facade — travel brochures, posters of West Coast destinations (ironically including sanitized images of places like [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]), bland corporate art, and staff trained to handle inquiries about travel packages.

## True Nature & Layout

Beneath the tourism veneer lies a secure, multi-level facility resembling a hidden intelligence agency blacksite. Access to the classified levels is via restricted elevators or stairwells requiring high-level clearance.

* **Command Center:** The operational heart of Caraway's faction. Secure communication arrays, consoles for monitoring Mirror-Breaker activities, the control systems for initiating protocols like **"The Departing Flight"** (thermobaric airstrike contingency).
* **Secure Laboratories & Evidence Analysis:** Where the [[Spectator Grubs]] brought by Chrysalis are being examined. Secure cold storage contains samples of **[[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]]**, PCBOT's outdated F7 antidote.
* **Detention & Interrogation ("Guest Suite"):** A spartan, monitored holding area. [[Agent Chrysalis]] is held here.
* **Disposal Facilities ("Baggage Claim"):** An industrial incinerator used for the complete destruction of sensitive materials.
* **Katherine's Office.**
* **Secure Archives & Terminals:** PCBOT's operational records and the secure terminal cluster that — per the 1994-96 SDEC bypass methodology on Blanc's drive — can be used to open a remote terminal session into the sealed [[SDEC Facility]]. This is the operation's actual objective.

## Personnel & Security

* Staffed by PCBOT agents loyal to the Caraway faction and trained security personnel.
* Standard high-security measures (access control, surveillance, armed guards).
* Internal rapid response teams for dealing with breaches.

## Atmosphere

A jarring contrast between the brightly lit, blandly optimistic tourism facade and the cold, paranoid atmosphere of the secure levels.

## Role in Campaign (PCBOT Excursion Arc)

* **Primary Setting:** Main location for the PCBOT Excursion arc (Act III).
* **Infiltration Target:** Players must infiltrate to bring **Alice** (cured) to a position where she can execute the SDEC bypass methodology in person. A diversion staged by [[VTFT (Virginia Track & Field Team)|VTFT]] may aid entry.
* **Key Operational Outcome:** Alice obtains a remote terminal session into the sealed SDEC Facility and extracts [[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]] and surviving SDEC archives.
* **Key Discoveries:** Players also locate [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]] samples, vital for the climax. They may rescue (or fail to rescue) [[Agent Chrysalis]].
* **Confrontation Site:** Location of the final confrontation with Caraway, triggering the "Departing Flight" crisis.
* **Climax Location:** Where Alice performs her final act of chemical decryption using G5/G6, leading to the jamming of the airstrike and her sacrifice/transformation.
* **Transition Point:** With Winston's memoirs in hand (and Alice's bypass already burned), the team can decode the Hexagon Pages and pivot to the endgame at Dead Horse Quarry. An on-site SDEC visit becomes optional rather than required.
