## Overview

**PCBOT Headquarters** is the central command and control facility for the [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|Pacific Coast Board of Tourism]], located in Washington D.C. While presenting a mundane public face, it conceals a secure, [[CIA]]-influenced blacksite operation under the direction of [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Director Henry Caraway]].

## Location & Cover Identity

* **Location:** An unassuming office building in Washington D.C.
* **Cover:** The **Pacific Coast Board of Tourism**. The public-facing lobby and potentially upper floors maintain this facade meticulously, featuring travel brochures, cheerful posters of West Coast destinations (ironically including sanitized images of places like [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]), bland corporate art, and staff trained to handle inquiries about travel packages and regional attractions. This mundane appearance serves as effective camouflage.

## True Nature & Layout

Beneath the tourism veneer lies a secure, multi-level facility akin to a hidden intelligence agency blacksite. Access to the classified levels is likely via restricted elevators or stairwells requiring high-level clearance. The aesthetic shifts dramatically from the public area to a sterile, functional, potentially brutalist government/military design.

Key areas within the classified sections include:

* **Command Center:** The operational heart of Caraway's PCBOT faction. Features secure communication arrays, consoles for monitoring [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations|Mirror-Breaker]] activities, potentially live feeds from sensitive locations (like assets monitoring Sequoia Falls), and the control systems for initiating protocols like **"The Departing Flight"** (thermobaric airstrike contingency).
* **Secure Laboratories & Evidence Analysis:** Facilities equipped for handling and analyzing anomalous materials. This is where the [[Spectator Grubs]] brought by [[Agent Chrysalis|Chrysalis]] are likely examined. Crucially, secure cold storage in this area contains samples of **[[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]]**, PCBOT's outdated F7 antidote.
* **Detention & Interrogation ("Guest Suite"):** A euphemistically named secure holding area. Likely consists of spartan, monitored cells designed for holding captured agents or persons of interest. **[[Agent Chrysalis|Agent Chrysalis]] is held here** for over two weeks prior to the players' arrival.
* **Disposal Facilities ("Baggage Claim"):** An industrial incinerator or similar high-temperature disposal unit used for the complete destruction of sensitive documents, biological samples, or potentially even bodies, ensuring operational secrecy.
* **Katherine's Office**
* **Secure Archives:** Houses PCBOT's operational records, personnel files, intelligence reports, and potentially contingency plans or data acquired from other [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations]]. This is the likely location of the **[[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents]]**, detailing override or bypass procedures for the sealed [[SDEC Facility|SDEC facility]].

## Personnel & Security

* Staffed by PCBOT agents loyal to the Caraway faction and trained security personnel.
* Employs standard high-security measures (access control, surveillance, potentially armed guards) appropriate for a blacksite.
* May have internal rapid response teams or protocols for dealing with breaches.

## Atmosphere

A jarring contrast exists between the brightly lit, blandly optimistic tourism facade and the cold, paranoid, and ruthlessly efficient atmosphere of the secure levels. Fluorescent lighting, utilitarian design, and a pervasive sense of secrecy and state-sanctioned menace likely dominate the classified areas.

## Role in Campaign (PCBOT Excursion Arc)

* **Primary Setting:** Serves as the main location for the "PCBOT Excursion" arc (Phase 5 of Act II in the [[Story Trajectory]]).
* **Infiltration Target:** Players must infiltrate this facility to rescue [[Agent Chrysalis|Chrysalis]]. A diversion staged by the [[VTFT (Virginia Track & Field Team)|VTFT]] may aid entry.
* **Key Discoveries:** Players locate [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]] samples here, vital for the climax. They can also potentially find the [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents|SDEC "Ant Trap" documents]] as a contingency.
* **Confrontation Site:** Location of the final confrontation with [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Henry Caraway]], triggering the "[[PCBOT Headquarters|Departing Flight]]" crisis.
* **Climax Location:** Where [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]] performs her final act of decryption using G5/G6, leading to the jamming of the airstrike and her sacrifice/transformation.
* **Transition Point:** Successfully navigating this location provides the means (Chrysalis or documents) to proceed to the [[SDEC Facility|SDEC facility]] in Act III.
