
**Tags:** #Faction #Corporation

## Overview
A large capital finance firm with diverse holdings, including rail infrastructure. Majority shareholder of [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]].

## Philosophy & Role
* Views holdings through a **purely financial lens**. Decisions driven entirely by bottom-line calculations, profit consolidation, and tax advantages. Embodies corporate indifference.
* Owned by wealthy, eccentric [[Edwin Burberry]] (Connecticut-based).
* Maintained the unprofitable [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] Sequoia Falls branch primarily as a **tax write-off**.
* Saw the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing|ErgoMatic]] merger solely as financially advantageous. Appointed [[Richard Harmon]] as transitional LTX CEO specifically to ensure the merger completed.
* **Unwitting Enabler:** Its detached, profit-driven management **inadvertently served [[Rhea]]'s goals** by facilitating the LTX-ErgoMatic merger, creating the necessary infrastructure for Rhea's transcendence.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Likely remains completely unaware of [[Rhea]] or the true nature of the LTX/ErgoMatic operations. Represents the impersonal financial system that allows horror to propagate unnoticed.
