The Schwartz family are key members of the [[Hammerskull Crew]] based in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]], operating the [[White Stag Bar]].

## Known Members

* **Luther Schwartz**: Owner of the [[White Stag Bar]]. Presumably the leader or a senior figure in the local Hammerskull chapter.
* **Tyler Schwartz**: Manager of the [[White Stag Bar]]. Emails between him and Luther revealed the access code to the [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] (1489) and confirmed [[Calvin Pierce]] stole meth from them.
* [[David Schwartz]]: Mentioned as an enforcer associated with the [[Hammerskull Crew]].

## Activities & Connections

* **[[Hammerskull Crew]]**: Central figures within this neo-Nazi organization.
* **[[White Stag Bar]]**: Operate this bar, which serves as the Hammerskull base and housed [[Methamphetamine]] production (and formerly early [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] development).
* **[[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]**: The Hammerskulls, likely including the Schwartzes, provide muscle, security (e.g., for [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]), and a drug distribution network for BSSA operations.
