## Overview

The **Virginia Track & Field Team** is a [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol|Mirror-Breaker cell]] based in Virginia (operationally near the DC area, which becomes load-bearing in Act III). Cipher: athletic and competition metaphors. Sidelined since the 1994 Caraway coup at PCBOT, having been aligned with [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Lopez]]'s pre-coup faction.

VTFT's diminished influence makes it both *useful* (Caraway doesn't take them seriously) and *limited* (their resources are thin). In the PCBOT excursion, they can run an outside diversion — the GM Workplan assumes this. They cannot be relied on as a heavy paramilitary force.
