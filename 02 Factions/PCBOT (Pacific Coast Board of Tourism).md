## Overview

The **Pacific Coast Board of Tourism** presents to the public as a regional tourism-coordination agency. Its actual mandate is to coordinate the seven West Coast [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol|Mirror-Breaker cells]] and to act as the network's interface with the [[CIA]]. PCBOT was created in the post-[[GANYMEDE (1979)|GANYMEDE]] restructuring as a neutral oversight body. It is no longer neutral.

## History

PCBOT was originally run by [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Jacqui Lopez]] (Director Javelin), who treated the role the way the Protocol intended — light coordination, heavy compartmentalization, the cells left to operate as cells. Lopez's tenure produced the relocation of BNJC to Sequoia Falls and the assignment of [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] as PCBOT's local liaison.

**The Caraway coup (1994)** ended that. [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Henry Caraway]] — backed by the CIA, and politically prepared by the CIA's earlier assassination of Lopez's congressional ally [[Robert Toomey]] — took control of PCBOT. Lopez was sidelined. [[Katherine Hill]] was elevated to a dual BSSA/PCBOT role (ASAC Kayak). EUROPA-era files were transferred away from BNJC to NOPG. PCBOT shifted from light oversight to active centralization, in alignment with BSSA and under CIA reach.

## Current posture (late October 1997)

Caraway is operating PCBOT as a paranoid blacksite. His allies are thin — Katherine is in Montreal running the museum operation, the rest of his security apparatus is loyal but uninformed. He is sitting on [[Agent Chrysalis|Chrysalis's]] Spectator Grub samples without understanding what they are, and increasingly convinced the entire Sequoia Falls situation is about to detonate the careful CIA-backed consolidation he's spent five years building.

(Note: as of the current canon, Chrysalis is **not** at PCBOT — he disappeared after COTU without arriving. The Grub samples are present; Chrysalis himself is not. Caraway is interpreting the samples without the context Chrysalis would have provided. See [[Agent Chrysalis]].)

His response — the contingency he is prepared to deploy if the situation escalates further — is "**The Departing Flight**": a thermobaric airstrike on [[Sequoia Falls, WA]], pre-packaged with a cover story blaming the destruction on residual [[CALLISTO (1949)|CALLISTO]]-era LTX mining explosives detonating in old rail tunnels near COTU. The strike erases BNJC, BSSA's local fronts, LTX/ErgoMatic, the Foxgloves, the witnesses, and the evidence — at the price of a several-square-mile crater. Caraway considers this an acceptable tradeoff.

## Role in the campaign

PCBOT HQ is the Act III primary objective. Not because of Chrysalis (he's not there) and not because Caraway is the campaign's final villain (he isn't) — because the control center on PCBOT's classified floor is the only place from which [[SDEC Facility|SDEC]]'s physical Ant Trap can be remotely disengaged. The team breaks in to execute that override using the [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents]] Alice stole pre-coup, and to jam the Departing Flight strike Caraway triggers in response. If the operation succeeds, Caraway dies at PCBOT HQ.

PCBOT's own internal containment protocol — used if the secure floor is breached beyond recovery — is "**The Scenic Route**," triggered by a disabled hotel keycard.
