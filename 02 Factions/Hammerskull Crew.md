## Overview
A **neo-Nazi organization** operating in the [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] / [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]] region. Base of operations is the [[White Stag Bar]]. Allied with [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].

## Leadership & Members
* **Leader:** [[The Schwartz Family|Luther Schwartz]] (owner of White Stag)
* **Key Members:**
    * [[The Schwartz Family|Tyler Schwartz]] (manager of White Stag)
    * [[The Schwartz Family|David Schwartz]] (enforcer)
    * [[Bruce Kennedy]] (Kittitas County Sheriff's Deputy, involved in [[Syd Foxglove]]'s initial detention)
    * [[Calvin Pierce]] (Distributed stolen [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]], killed by [[Writers]], deceased)
    * [[Sean Fielding]] (Associate, placed at [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] by Hammerskulls/[[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]], deceased)

## Activities & Role
* Provide **muscle and enforcement** for [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] operations.
* Act as **security** for the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]].
* Involved in **drug distribution** ([[Methamphetamine]] production at White Stag, formerly [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] distribution). Provided drug users as test subjects for [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s [[Operation Colossus]] chemical enhancement phase.
* Historically connected to [[CIA]] covert operations funding schemes.
* Currently assisting [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] in hunting [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Active and allied with [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]. Represents a source of violent antagonists and a link between BSSA and local criminal/extremist elements. Their connection to Deputy Kennedy implies influence within the [[Sheriffs Department (Kittitas County)]].
