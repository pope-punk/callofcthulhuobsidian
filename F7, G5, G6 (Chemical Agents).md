## Overview
A suite of psychoactive chemicals developed or researched by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] (F7, G5) or conceived by [[Syd Foxglove]] (G6). These chemicals interact with the baseline **[[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)]]** present in all humans, influencing the balance between systematic and metaphorical processing, rather than interacting with specific [[Royal Jelly]] modifications. They are central to managing [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]] and figure prominently in [[Rhea]]'s endgame plans.

## Specific Agents
* **[[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]:** Primary agent. Suppresses the **System Pole** (pattern recognition) via serotonergic pathways, granting basilisk immunity but causing cognitive/psychological side effects (indirectly impairing Metaphor Pole). Used unethically in [[Project Ozymandias]].
* **[[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)]]:** BSSA's flawed F7 antidote. Restores/enhances the **System Pole** (dopaminergic) but causes fatal cumulative gallium toxicity. Production halted.
* **[[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]]:** Theoretical non-toxic antidote conceived by [[Syd Foxglove]]. Restores/enhances the **System Pole** (dopaminergic) safely. Unknowingly aligns with [[Rhea]]'s goal to suppress the **Metaphor Pole** universally, collapsing the Dialectic for "Induced Susceptibility."

## Thematic Significance
* Represents the **chemical** approach to managing infohazards and manipulating cognition, contrasting with the **cognitive/behavioral** approach of the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]].
* Highlights the tension between pattern-breaking/System suppression (F7) and pattern-following/System enhancement (G5/G6), directly interacting with the engineered [[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)]].
* Underscores BSSA's unethical methods and Rhea's insidious long-term manipulation, turning a potential cure (G6) into a tool for homogenization and control.
