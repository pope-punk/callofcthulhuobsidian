## Overview
Various documents and records containing information about, or derived from, [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]. Highly sought after by various factions, particularly [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].

## Key Documents
* **[[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]]:** Contains the origin story of Winston's Algorithm access via [[Royal Jelly]] and the [[Dead Horse Quarry]] Hive encounter. Includes undecipherable [[Hexagon Pages (Winston's Memoirs)|Hexagon Pages]] at the end, likely decodable via [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]]. Located in the sealed [[SDEC Facility]].
* **[[Winston's 1953 Research List]]:** Handwritten list predicting key technologies ([[Signal Dummies]], GenAssist) needed for [[Rhea]]'s plan. Filed away in ErgoMatic archives in 1953 and unrecovered as of October 1997. Primarily historical context for Oliver's intuitive trajectory — *not* the discovery mechanism for his 1996 realization (which was triggered instead by noticing Syd's spontaneous Ylynthii doodling).
* **[[Snowflake Pattern Diagrams]]:** Original diagrams or blueprints detailing the Algorithm-derived warehouse layouts implemented by [[Winston Foxglove]] at [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]]. [[Sean Fielding]] was tasked by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]/[[Hammerskull Crew]] to find these, likely located in [[LTX Headquarters]] archives.
