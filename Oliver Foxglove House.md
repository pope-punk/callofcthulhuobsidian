## Overview
The personal residence of the late [[Oliver Foxglove]], presumably located in or near [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]]. Site of his hidden safe containing crucial research notes.

## Key Features
* **Home Safe:** Contains [[Oliver Foxglove's Advanced Research Notes]] needed for [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] synthesis. Requires solving the [[Safe Puzzle]] to open.
* **Laboratory Access (Potential):** May contain a personal lab or equipment sufficient for [[Syd Foxglove]] to synthesize [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] once the research notes are obtained, or provide clues to accessing necessary facilities elsewhere (like back at [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] under secured conditions).

## Significance
* Location of the central [[Safe Puzzle]] linking [[Oliver Foxglove Emails]], [[Oliver Foxglove's Cipher Notes]], and [[Ylynthii Numerals]].
* Site where the key to creating [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] is found.
* Potential location for the actual synthesis of G6.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Likely unoccupied since Oliver's death. May be secured by authorities, [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]], or simply left untouched. Key location for the culmination of the investigation into Oliver's legacy and the acquisition of the means to create G6.
