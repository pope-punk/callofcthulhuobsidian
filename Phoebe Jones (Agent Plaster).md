## Description

Phoebe Jones was the assumed identity of **BSSA Agent "Plaster"**. She served on the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] from ~1987 to 1992, handling its finances.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **BSSA Agent:** Operative working for [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]. Codename "Plaster".
* **SCAI Role:** Infiltrated the committee, managing its budget. This position allowed her to funnel funds to "Axon and Dendrite" and obscure purchase orders for specialized equipment used to reactivate [[Operation POUTINE]] relay stations for [[Operation Colossus]].
* **Record Purge (1992):** Following the termination of [[Operation Colossus]], Agent Plaster purged incriminating financial records from the SCAI archives before it was merged back into the state arts commission.

## Status

Current status and whereabouts unknown. Presumed still active within BSSA or retired post-Colossus. Her actions were key to BSSA's cover-up but also left faint traces (like final payment records for [[Maria Chen]]) that [[Éric Blanc]] eventually followed.
