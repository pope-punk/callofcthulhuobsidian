## Overview
Wife of [[Winston Foxglove]] and mother of [[Oliver Foxglove]]; grandmother of [[Syd Foxglove]]. Born March 1st, 1925. Died in a "freak accident" in 1962 — almost certainly orchestrated by [[Rhea]] to give Oliver the [[Traumatic Activation]] he needed to unlock his inherited [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] aptitude.

Her birthday is the basis for the code on Oliver's home safe (see [[Safe Puzzle]]). Oliver picked the date as a private memorial and as a key Syd could intuit but no outsider would think to try.
