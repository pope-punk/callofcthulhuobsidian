## Overview
Andrea Foxglove was the wife of [[Winston Foxglove]] and the mother of [[Oliver Foxglove]]. She died in a "freak accident" in 1962 — almost certainly orchestrated by [[Rhea]] to trigger the traumatic activation of Oliver's latent [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] aptitude.

## Significance
* Her death is the [[Traumatic Activation]] event for Oliver, completing the epigenetic chain that began with Winston's 1922 [[Royal Jelly]] dosing.
* **Her birthday is the basis for the code to Oliver's home safe.** Oliver chose it deliberately, both for sentimental reasons and because it was a date Syd would recognize but no outsider would think to try. The safe contains [[Oliver Foxglove's Advanced Research Notes]] and the vacuum-sealed reagent essential for [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] synthesis. (See [[Safe Puzzle]].)

## Status
Deceased (1962).
