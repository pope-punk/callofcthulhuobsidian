## Overview
The location near [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]] that housed [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s front organization, the Chapel of the Blessed Lamb, from 1986 to 1992. It served as the operational base for [[Operation Colossus]].

## History
* **Establishment (1986):** Founded by [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]] (as Andrew Chamberlain) as a [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] front after faking his death following the [[Polybius]] cult raid. Recruited former cultists [[Jacob Wise]] and [[Erin Cross]].
* **Operation Colossus (1987-1992):** Served as the studio/base for the "Seventh Hour" and "Axon and Dendrite" radio shows which broadcast the [[The Echo]] basilisk via the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. Likely site of early [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]/HFD experimentation or planning.
* **David Rubinsky Execution (Mid 1992):** Likely the site where [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] executed radio host [[David Rubinsky]] after [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] disrupted [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]].
* **Abandonment/Sanitization (Mid 1992):** Hastily abandoned and sanitized by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] concurrently with the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] to cover up [[Operation Colossus]]. Records were likely transferred to the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] storage.

## Significance
* Historical base for [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s first major infohazard weaponization attempt ([[Operation Colossus]]).
* Direct link between [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]], [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]], and the [[The Echo]]/[[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] events.
* Site of [[David Rubinsky]]'s murder.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* The physical site is likely abandoned, possibly demolished or repurposed. May contain residual environmental clues missed by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]. Key historical context gained primarily through [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb Paperwork]] found at the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]].
