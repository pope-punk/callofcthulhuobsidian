## Overview
An abandoned radio transmission facility located near [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]]. Originally part of the [[CIA]]'s [[Operation POUTINE]] network, it was later repurposed by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] (1987-1992) for [[Operation Colossus]] to broadcast [[The Echo]] basilisk via the "Axon and Dendrite" show.

## History
* **CIA Operation POUTINE (1970s - 1986):** Served as a standard numbers station relay. Decommissioned by the CIA in 1986.
* **BSSA Operation Colossus (1987 - 1992):** BSSA reactivated the station. Agent [[Raymond Vaughn (Agent Rococo)|Vaughn]] installed equipment to modulate [[The Echo]] tones based on listener feedback. This operation inadvertently spawned the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN. [[David Rubinsky]] and [[Cleo Zhang]] hosted "Axon and Dendrite" from studios linked to this station.
* **Disruption & Abandonment (Mid 1992):** After [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] disrupted EUROPA (partly by providing Rubinsky/Zhang with a randomizer tool), Agent Vaughn removed key modulation equipment. BSSA hastily sanitized and abandoned the facility, likely destroying primary broadcast tapes and sensitive project files to cover their tracks.

## Significance
* Physical site of [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s unethical [[Operation Colossus]] experiments and the origin point for the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN.
* Contains potential residual environmental clues or documents missed during BSSA's sanitization.

## Key Discoveries (Act II - Rubinsky Puzzle)
Amidst the derelict equipment and hastily removed fixtures, investigators exploring the station can uncover crucial items left behind or hidden during the chaotic end of "Axon and Dendrite":
* **[[Rubinsky's Final Segment Sequence Log]]**: A recording log, printout, or technical note detailing the unique **six-segment sequence** of [[David Rubinsky]]'s final, unaired "testament" episode. This sequence was structured using the SDEC randomizer.
* **[[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]]**: A printed document or technical manual detailing the specific "mapping rules" for the randomizer tool provided by [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] to Rubinsky and Zhang. This document explains precisely how a four-digit input code determines the exact order of the show's six segments.
* These two items form the core components of the [[Rubinsky's Lockbox Puzzle]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Abandoned and derelict. May contain environmental hazards or residual anomalous effects. Key investigation site for understanding [[Operation Colossus]], [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]], and finding the necessary clues for the [[Rubinsky's Lockbox Puzzle]].
