## Overview
A major Canadian city. Served as a refuge for [[Syd Foxglove]] between July and late October 1997 under the protection of [[Éric Blanc]]. Now the focal point for a critical [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] operation.

## Significance
* Location where [[Syd Foxglove]] conducted research and developed the theoretical basis for [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]].
* Connected to [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] via her [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] investigation contacts ([[Éric Blanc]]).
* Site of a former [[CIA]] Operation POUTINE relay, relevant to [[Operation Colossus]].
* **Site of Blanc's Capture:** [[Éric Blanc]] was captured (not killed) here by BSSA in early October 1997.
* **Target of BSSA Operation:** Targeted by BSSA after [[Katherine Hill]]'s independent investigative trail (not the Shutter leak) flagged Blanc as a route to Syd.
* **Projected Ambush Site:** Location of the [[McCord Museum]], where BSSA plans to ambush Syd Foxglove on the next monthly standing-protocol date.

## Key Specific Sites
* [[McGill University]] (Syd's chemistry research site).
* [[McCord Museum]] (Planned BSSA ambush site; Syd and Blanc's standing emergency meeting location).
* [[Éric Blanc's Apartment]] (Investigation site — ransacked, containing Blanc's encrypted computer, calendar, and museum receipts).

## Status (Late Oct 1997)
[[Éric Blanc]] is captured but alive. [[Syd Foxglove]] is unaware and being lured toward the monthly museum protocol. BSSA is actively preparing the McCord Museum ambush. Players need to travel here, coordinate with [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)]], investigate Blanc's apartment, understand the trap, and intervene at the museum — and recover the encrypted computer / drive for later decryption by a cured Alice.
