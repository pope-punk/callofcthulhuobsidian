## Overview
The location of the sealed, defunct headquarters of the [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]]. A key location for uncovering deep lore in the campaign's later stages.

## Key Specific Site
* [[SDEC Facility]]

## Significance
* Holds crucial information ([[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]]) needed to understand the origin of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] access and locate [[Rhea]]'s Hive at [[Dead Horse Quarry]].
* Represents a historical site linked to the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol|Mirror-Breaker network]] and [[Rhea]]'s direct intervention.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* The facility remains sealed under the "Ant Trap" protocol. Players likely need specific means or knowledge to bypass the seal and security. Projected as a penultimate investigation site.
