## Overview

PCBOT HQ is a Washington D.C. office building that runs a tourism front over a CIA-flavored blacksite. [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Henry Caraway]] runs it; [[Katherine Hill]] keeps an office on the secure levels in her ASAC Kayak capacity.

In the late campaign, PCBOT HQ matters because its control center is the only place from which [[SDEC Facility|SDEC]]'s physical Ant Trap can be remotely disengaged. Alice and Blanc pre-mapped the infiltration between 1994 and 1996 using [[Agent Chrysalis]]'s SDEC sonar readings; the override procedure itself comes from the [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents]] Alice quietly stole from PCBOT during the EUROPA period. All of it is on Blanc's encrypted drive.

## Cover and Layout

* **Public lobby:** travel brochures, posters of West Coast destinations including a few sanitized shots of [[Sequoia Falls, WA]], staff handling actual tourism inquiries.
* **Classified levels (restricted elevators / stairwells):** sterile, brutalist, fluorescent.

Inside the secure floors:

* **Command Center.** Comms, Mirror-Breaker monitoring, the controls for the "**Departing Flight**" thermobaric strike contingency, and the console from which SDEC's Ant Trap can be remotely disengaged. The override is the primary objective; the Departing Flight controls become a secondary problem once Caraway reacts.
* **Secure Labs.** Currently working over the [[Spectator Grubs]] Chrysalis brought in. Cold storage holds [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]].
* **"Guest Suite."** Holding cells. **Empty of Chrysalis as of campaign start** — Chrysalis never made it here. (See [[Agent Chrysalis]].)
* **"Baggage Claim."** Industrial incinerator for documents, samples, bodies.
* **Katherine's Office.**
* **Secure Archives.** Operational paperwork, personnel files, contingency dossiers. Of note: the original [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents]] that Alice copied during the EUROPA period are still here — PCBOT never knew she took them, and the originals were never moved. Nothing in these archives is remotely useful for opening SDEC's physical interior; that's a Mirror-Breaker doctrinal limit (see [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]).

## Role in the PCBOT Excursion

* **Why the team is here:** to reach the control center and execute the remote Ant Trap override. Nothing about SDEC's interior comes over the wire — the override only disengages SDEC's seal so the team can physically enter the San Diego facility afterward.
* **VTFT diversion:** [[VTFT (Virginia Track & Field Team)|VTFT]] may stage outside cover to thin Caraway's security.
* **Caraway's reaction:** the operation confirms his deepest fear and pushes him to activate "Departing Flight" against Sequoia Falls. The team must jam the strike via the [[BNJC Radiometry Array]] — which is what Alice's final chemical decryption is for. This is a *separate* climactic beat from the override itself.
* **Caraway's fate:** if the operation succeeds, he dies here.
* **Katherine's possible presence:** if she survived the McCord Museum (player-dependent), she may be at PCBOT HQ when the team arrives. If she didn't, her office is intact and may yield her personal files — Operation Parthenon notes, Project Ozymandias documentation, possibly a journal.

## What Players Can Expect to Find

* G5 samples (cold storage).
* Possibly Katherine's office records.
* Internal documentation of the Departing Flight protocol — useful for the jam.
* The original [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents]] in Secure Archives (Alice's copies are on Blanc's drive; the originals are still here, and recovering them closes the loop on her pre-coup theft).
* The "Scenic Route" internal-breach containment, activated by a disabled hotel keycard.
