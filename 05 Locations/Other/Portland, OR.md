## Description

Portland, Oregon is a major city in the Pacific Northwest.

## Significance to Campaign

* **[[Cleo Zhang]]'s Location**: After surviving the termination of [[Operation Colossus]] and the execution of her co-host [[David Rubinsky]] in Ellensburg (1992), Cleo Zhang fled to Portland. Players may need to track her down here (possibly via the art scene) to gain insight into [[Operation Colossus]], [[The Echo]], and [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]].
* **[[Polybius]] Connection (Potential)**: The real-world urban legend of the Polybius arcade game is often associated with Portland in the early 1980s. This could be linked to [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)|Andrew Chamberlain]]'s early work or other proto-infohazard events in the campaign's backstory.
