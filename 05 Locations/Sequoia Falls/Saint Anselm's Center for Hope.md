## Overview
A [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] front organization operating under the cover of a homeless shelter and treatment center in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. Primary site for [[Project Ozymandias]] ([[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] development/testing and basilisk testing). Publicly run by [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]]. Linked via access code **1489**.

## Layout & Features

- Public Shelter Area.
- Hidden Laboratories (F7/G5 synthesis, [[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)]]).
- Basilisk Testing Wing (Testing effects of [[Black Coral]], [[The Echo]], and **2D [[Fauxflake]] pattern drawings** produced at the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]).
- Detention Areas.

## Role in Syd Foxglove's Captivity (Jan-July 1997)
* **Initial Holding & Testing (Mid/Late Jan):** Syd transferred here immediately after capture. Site of initial [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] testing where his apparent nonreaction occurred, catching [[Katherine Hill]]'s interest.
* **Second Holding Period (Apr/May - July 15):** Syd transferred *back* here from the Church by Katherine to deflect [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]]'s scrutiny away from [[Operation Parthenon]].
* **Dylan's Contact:** Likely site where Dylan made clandestine contact with Syd, confirming his suspicions about BSSA.
* **Staging Point for Intercepted Transfer (July 15):** Katherine ordered Syd transferred from St. Anselm's *back* to the Church. Dylan attempted to intercept this transfer near here. [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s ambush occurred during this interception attempt originating from St. Anselm's.

Key investigation site linking BSSA chemicals to COTU and confirming the testing pipeline for Fauxflake diagrams. Crucial location in Syd's captivity narrative and the events of July 15th.
