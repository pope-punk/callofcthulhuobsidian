## Overview
The residence of [[Sean Fielding]] at 55 Rosebush Court, [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. The investigation here effectively kicked off the players' involvement in the main plot.

## Layout & Features
* Suburban house, showing signs of neglect and a recent party.
* Dirt (potting soil) tracked throughout.
* Empty boxes shipped from fake names ([[Signal Dummies]]) like "Alphonse Spicecube."
* [[Hammerskull Crew]] shirt and engraved knives found.
* Phone messages from various contacts.
* [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] leaflet found.

## Key Discoveries / Events
* **Inciting Incident:** [[Emily Fielding]] brought players here after receiving a letter addressed to Sean from "Alphonse Spicecube" via [[Sebastian International]].
* **Spray Paint:** [[The Hexacle|Hexagons]] spray-painted in red on one wall; "Please God, Forgive Me" on another. These were likely left by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] after killing Sean, marking the site.
* **Initial Reaper Encounter:** Players encountered a [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reaper]] ("Mr. Honeycomb") in the backyard, which triggered disorientation via flashing lights before vanishing.

## Significance
* Starting point of the investigation into Sean's disappearance.
* Introduced players to key recurring elements: [[Signal Dummies]] (via boxes), [[Hammerskull Crew]], [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]], [[The Hexacle]], [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reapers]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Investigated by players early in the campaign. Likely still considered a crime scene or point of interest by authorities (or [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]]) related to Sean's disappearance.
