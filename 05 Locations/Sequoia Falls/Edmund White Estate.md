## Overview
A large, secluded estate in the woods south of [[Sequoia Falls, WA]], a short distance from [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU]] under the I-90 corridor. Formerly owned by [[Edmund White]] (deceased). Used by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] as a safe house and operational base after leaving the FBI and joining [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]]. Between July 17 and September 8, 1997 this was Alice's **primary base of operations**, with the COTU underpass serving as her resupply meet point and hunting-infrastructure access; she walked or biked between the two through the wooded corridor.

## Key Events
* **Alice's Safehouse:** Used by Alice for several years. Likely contains remnants of her investigations into [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].
* **Dylan Hill Interrogation & Death (July 15, 1997):** Alice brought [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]] here after ambushing him. She interrogated him, learned about BSSA operations, was injected with [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]], and subsequently killed him here.
* **Reaper Attack (Sept 8, 1997):** A [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reaper]] tracked Alice here, forcing her to flee permanently into the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] tunnels. From this date forward Alice abandoned the Estate as a base. Planned site for player encounter with this specific Reaper according to future trajectory.

## Alice's mobility pattern (July 17 – Sept 8)

Alice walked or biked between the Estate and the COTU underpass, which were close enough on foot through the wooded corridor south of Sequoia Falls to make a vehicle unnecessary for the personal commute. Her actual hunting vehicles — rental Hondas procured and dropped at the I-90 underpass by [[Agent Chrysalis]] — were never staged at the Estate. The Estate functioned as her rest base, intelligence archive, and (until July 15) interrogation site. After September 8 she lost it entirely and operated only out of the tunnels.
