## Overview
A network of tunnels accessible from beneath the I-90 corridor near [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]]. Used by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] as a base of operations and hunting ground after fleeing the [[Edmund White Estate]]. Players enter via the abandoned shipping container at the COTU site and work their way through three connected zones: the ravine beneath the highway, the disused railway-cum-sewer trunk, and (eventually) the surface manhole near the Redwood Shopping Mall.

## Part 0 — Beyond the Shipping Container

Support pillars for the highway above are sunk into both walls of the ravine here. Remnants of a black, inky substance are smeared across the tunnel walls on both sides nearby in an apparent hasty removal attempt.

A successful Spot Hidden / Investigate / Intelligence check confirms the smears used to be [[The Hexacle|Hexacles]]. Close inspection reveals the remains are dry and flaky compared to the thick viscous ink on their clothing.

* Closer examination reveals that the walls around the second support pillar on the right have cracked severely and have begun to collapse, like gums pulling away from a tooth. It also has a horizontal hairline crack.
* If the characters try to clear the debris out around the edges of the support beam, the hairline crack expands and the column separates. The bottom half slides two meters down the tunnel as the support's foundation falls through the roof of a [[Hive]] twenty feet beneath the players' feet. Dust falls from above.
    * The disturbed Hive material produces a *"sudden organic smell of licorice and nail polish remover"* which lasts only a moment before dissipating.

## Part 1 — Sewers: East and North

**Option: East.** East heads toward [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]]. After about three-quarters of a mile, the tunnel opens up. On the other side of the half-boarded-up mouth, the players find the destroyed body of "Thermos Milkshaft" — a [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]] — handcuffed to a desk with three bullets deep in its body. A hose is out of reach on the other side of the room.

* An invoice for shipments dated mid-July is here. **(GM TODO: author this clue.)** It establishes that this was probably the first Dummy Alice destroyed.

**Option: North.** Heading north leads to a low retaining wall in the tunnel, where the remnants of the old railway turn into the sewers proper. At the base of this retaining wall, there are some sleeping bags and a rope ladder over the barrier to the sewers. There's also a ladder going up to the road.

* If the players try to ascend the ladder, they find that the manhole is padlocked. The key is hidden in the sleeping bags.
* On the surface, the players find themselves near the **Redwood Shopping Mall**, which is now partially destroyed following Thistle and Thorn's cover-up.
* Nearby is a bus station, where they can interview **Tina and Andy Strauss**:
    * Everyone used to know the underpass as a place you couldn't go to. If you tried, you'd get turned around and find yourself in the woods or back on the highway without remembering how you got there. You wouldn't even remember you were trying to go there. It was like the place was a kind of memory hole.
    * There are places like that everywhere. You just forget that you forgot them.
    * But people who came out of [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope|Saint Anselm's]] started congregating there, and seemed able to come and go effortlessly. They acted really weird, and had bizarre religious behavior — totally different from the church's beliefs. They didn't have any kind words for the church.
    * A guy used to come by and deal here. Usually he just would stay between the overpass and the bus station, but I saw him go down into the woods a couple weeks ago and never come back up. (Calvin Pierce.)

**New information gained — North branch:**
* Calvin dealt here. (White Stag connection.)
* Most people can't enter/leave COTU. (Clue about the nature of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]].)
* People discharged from Saint Anselm's can come and go freely. (Saint Anselm's connection.)

## Significance
* [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s current hiding place and operational area (Oct 1997).
* She uses the tunnels to move unseen and potentially stage ambushes on [[Signal Dummies]] based on [[Dylan Hill's Shipping Manifests]].
* Contains evidence of Alice's presence: empty [[Adderall]] bottles, her distinctive red spray-painted [[The Hexacle|Hexacle]] graffiti (possibly over older, black Writer versions), potentially kill sites like where "Thermos Milkshaft" was found.
* Players likely need to navigate these tunnels to track Alice or find further clues related to her activities.
* Potential location for administering [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] cure to Alice in the future trajectory.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Active hunting ground and refuge for the F7-affected [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]. Likely dangerous due to environmental hazards and Alice's unpredictable state.
