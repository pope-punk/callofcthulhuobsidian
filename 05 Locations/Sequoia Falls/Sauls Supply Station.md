## Description

Saul’s Supply Station is a hardware store located in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. It is owned or operated by [[Dan Calhoun]].

## Significance to Investigation

* **[[Dan Calhoun]] Interview**: Player investigators interviewed [[Dan Calhoun]] here. He provided information about seeing [[Sean Fielding]]'s car parked outside after his disappearance and described the "strange jazz woman" ([[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]).
* **[[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]]'s Purchases**: Dan Calhoun confirmed that Alice bought suspicious items here in early September 1997: crazy glue, red spray paint, and a cattle prod.
* **Security Footage**: Calhoun showed the investigators security footage from the store which confirmed the "jazz woman" was indeed Alice Redmond, providing crucial identification early in the investigation.
* **Spray Paint Link**: Players later found a spray paint canister (potentially from [[Sarah Prescott]]'s apartment) that matched the type sold at Saul's, linking Alice's activities across different locations.

## Status

An active local business that served as an important source of early clues and witness testimony regarding Sean Fielding and Alice Redmond.
