## Description

Emily Fielding (b. 1972) is the sister of [[Sean Fielding]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **Initiating Investigation**: Her concern about her brother Sean's disappearance is a primary catalyst for the player investigation. She received voicemails from Sean before he disappeared.
* **Sebastian International Letter**: Received a letter from "Alphonse Spicecube" (a [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]] alias) connected to Sebastian International, which likely prompted her to seek help or attend the timeshare seminar where she met the players. This letter effectively kicked off the players' involvement.

## Status

Presumed living as of October 1997, actively seeking information about her missing brother.
