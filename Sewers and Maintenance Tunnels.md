## Overview
A network of tunnels accessible from beneath the I-90 corridor near [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]]. Used by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] as a base of operations and hunting ground after fleeing the [[Edmund White Estate]].

## Detail Notes

**Part 0. Beyond the Shipping Container

Support pillars for the highway above are sunk into both walls of the ravine here. Remnants of a black, inky substance are smeared across the tunnel walls on both sides nearby in an apparent hasty removal attempt.

Successful investigation check confirms they used to be Hexacles. Close inspection reveals the remains are dry and flaky compared to the thick viscous ink on their clothing.  
- Closer examination reveals that the walls around the second support pillar on the right have cracked severely and have begun to collapse, like gums pulling away from a tooth. It also has a hairline crack horizontally. 
- If characters try to clear the debris out around the edges of the the support beam, the hairline crack expands and the column separates. The bottom half slides two meters down the of the tunnel as the support’s foundation falls through the roof of a hive 20 feet beneath the players’ feet, and dust falls down from above.
	- The disturbed Hive material creates a “sudden organic smell of licorice and nail polish remover”	which lasts for only a moment before dissipating.

## Significance
* [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s current hiding place and operational area (Oct 1997).
* She uses the tunnels to move unseen and potentially stage ambushes on [[Signal Dummies]] based on [[Dylan Hill's Shipping Manifests]].
* Contains evidence of Alice's presence: empty [[Adderall]] bottles, her distinctive red spray-painted [[The Hexacle|Hexacle]] graffiti (possibly over older, black Writer versions), potentially kill sites like where "Thermos Milkshaft" was found.
* Players likely need to navigate these tunnels to track Alice or find further clues related to her activities.
* Potential location for administering [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] cure to Alice in the future trajectory.
