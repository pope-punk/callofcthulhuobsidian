# The Cycles of Rhea: Full Campaign Narrative Outline (Canon Corrected)

## Act I: The Missing Brother & The Tangled Web (Oct 3rd - ~Oct 10th, 1997)

### 1. The Hook (Friday, Oct 3rd)

* **Timeshare Seminar:** Players attend a [[Sebastian International]] timeshare seminar at the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]].
* **[[Emily Fielding]] Recruits:** Outside, Emily Fielding approaches the PCs, seeking help finding her missing brother, [[Sean Fielding]]. She shows them a strange letter addressed to Sean from "Alphonse Spicecube" via Sebastian International.
* **[[Sean Fielding's House]] Investigation:** Players accompany Emily to Sean's house (55 Rosebush Ct).
    * **Clues Found:** Messy house, party remnants, tracked dirt, empty [[Signal Dummies]] boxes (addressed to "Alphonse Spicecube" from [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]]), [[Hammerskull Crew]] shirt, possibly drugs.
    * **Graffiti:** [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s red spray-painted [[The Hexacle]] patterns and "Please God, Forgive Me" message on walls.
    * **[[Reapers]] Encounter:** Players spot a figure matching the "Mr. Honeycomb" cryptid legend in the backyard. It emits blinding flashes/pops when confronted, causing disorientation before vanishing. Players retreat to [[Pastor Mike]]'s house.

### 2. Following Initial Leads (Saturday, Oct 4th - Monday, Oct 6th)

* **Split Investigation (Oct 4th):**
    * **Maxwell & Cody:** Investigate return address (3704 S. Preston Rd - LTX office). Experience [[The Hexacle]] effect at the door (memory loss, disorientation), finding themselves in the Hot Cuppa coffee shop downstairs instead. They later successfully enter the office upstairs.
    * **Mike & Sebastian:** Research at library. Uncover [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]]/[[The Foxglove Family]] history ([[Winston Foxglove]] ousting after 1949 explosions, [[Oliver Foxglove]] death, [[Syd Foxglove]] missing, [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] merger). Learn about Sean's [[Hammerskull Crew]] ties.
    * **Alphonse Spicecube Encounter:** Group reconvenes and confronts the entity in the office space. It's a [[Signal Dummies]] automaton ("Alphonse Spicecube"). Combat ensues; Cody destroys it. Find clues: potting soil, boxes, water hose, [[Spectator Grubs]].
* **Church Visit & Witness Interviews (Oct 5th):**
    * Attempt to visit [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]. Turned away by [[Hammerskull Crew]] security ("No Jazz Clothing"). Obtain newspaper article mentioning church/shelter.
    * Interview [[Dan Calhoun]] at [[Sauls Supply Station]]. Learns Sean's car sighted Sept 27th. Gets description of [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|"Jazz Lady"]] (dirty, pine needles, forced smile, cash) buying cattle prod, red spray paint, glue in early Sept. Sees CCTV confirming identity.
    * Investigate victim addresses ([[Sarah Prescott]], [[Thomas Reeves]]). Find signs of Alice's M.O. ([[The Hexacle]] graffiti, ransacked rooms, stolen cars/keys).
* **Sample Analysis (Oct 6th):**
    * Drop off samples (slime, arm, worm) with [[Prof. Alexander Austen]] at community college. (Results: slime=organic fungus/plant; arm=advanced plastic, high fluorine, capillary action; worm=mostly sugar, 2% artificial G-C only DNA).
    * Set up meeting with [[Mandy Archer]] for Tuesday.

### 3. Escalation & Revelations (Tuesday, Oct 7th - Wednesday, Oct 8th)

* **[[LTX Headquarters]] Visit & Police Contact (Oct 7th):**
    * Infiltrate LTX (possibly posing as journalists). Note strange dirt shipment patterns. Interview [[Alex Kramer]] (obsessed with dirt), [[Jessica Herring]] (unhelpful).
    * Contact Det. [[Colin Reeves]] (using card found at LTX). Learn about missing persons moratorium, [[Edmund White|Balloon Mask Killer]] files pulled by government contact. Learn [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] caught BMK, resembles "Jazz Lady".
* **[[Redwood Shopping Mall]] Encounter (Oct 7th):**
    * Players trace [[Signal Dummies|"Quentin Cinnabon"]] packages to the mall.
    * Confront [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]] in abandoned wing dragging disabled Dummy.
    * Alice opens fire, killing [[Pastor Mike]] instantly.
    * Players grab Alice's dropped satchel ([[Keys (Alice's Satchel)], [[Alice's Journal (Satchel)]]) and flee.
* **[[Mirror-Breaker Organizations]] Contact (Oct 7th/8th):**
    * [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)]] agents "Thistle & Thorn" arrive at Mike's house. Explain they work for [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]], cleaned up mall scene. Confirm Alice = Agent Altissimo (rogue BNJC). Deliver cryptic message/items ([[Labyrinths by Jorge Luis Borges]], musical note puzzle).
* **[[Alice Redmond's House]] Investigation & Attack (Oct 8th):**
    * Research leads to Alice's address. Meet [[Colin Reeves]].
    * Investigate dusty apartment. Find [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] statue under sheet. Find [[Labyrinths by Jorge Luis Borges]] book with note from "[[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)|Nine-String]]", [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)]] syllabus.
    * Crack vault code (0.083). Find [[CALLISTO Report]], [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol Notes]], [[Alice's Map]], preserved [[Spectator Grubs]] samples, dossier on [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]].
    * [[Reapers]] ("Elk-like," honeycomb face) attacks from ceiling. Kills Reeves instantly. Players fight/flee.

### 4. Entering the Underground (Post Oct 8th)

* **[[BNJC HQ]] Debriefing:**
    * Players regroup, use [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]] contact info (keys/notes). Access HQ via elevator (code 13762).
    * Meet agents [[Travis Ward (Agent Tremolo)]], [[Leah Burns (Agent Legato)]]; possibly [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]].
    * Receive official debriefing: [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations]], [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] destruction (officially blamed on [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]]), [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]] faction split (Caraway vs. Lopez/[[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]]), Alice's rogue status/theory of [[Syd Foxglove]] control, [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]] missing, [[Katherine Hill|ASAC Kayak/Director Caryatid]] identity confirmed. Learn about [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] antagonism. Mole [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]] possibly present.
* **Following [[Alice's Map]]:**
    * Map points to 6 locations + 'X'. Players investigate:
        * Location 4: Burned out house (Dylan's?), find remnants.
        * Location 1: [[White Stag Bar]] (reconnaissance, note computer).
        * Location X (Woods near I-90): Enter woods, encounter strange [[Spectator Grubs]], time loss effects, possibly strange deer. Lead towards [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]].
* **[[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] Discovery:**
    * Find horrific scene: aftermath of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]-poisoning cult (canned food idols, emaciated bodies).
    * Find [[Calvin Pierce]]'s body (stabbed weeks ago, love note from Sean).
    * Find [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]] shipping container (paper dated Jan 5, 1950 inside?).
    * Find numerous identical cars containing [[Signal Dummies]]. Find [[Sean Fielding]]'s body in his car trunk (cattle prod marks).
    * Find Alice's apparent base camp: [[Adderall]] bottles, cot, papers, crucial note with **[[Montreal Phone Number]] (514-874-2313)**.
    * Encounter large worm on container. Fight [[Writers|wolf-like Editors]].
* **End of Act I:** Players likely return to [[BNJC HQ]] for another debrief, sharing COTU findings. [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]] *definitely* overhears the Montreal number this time if not before, relaying it to BSSA. Players now possess leads pointing strongly towards [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s fronts ([[White Stag Bar]], [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]], [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]) and the critical [[Montreal, QC]] connection via Syd/Alice.

## Act II: Unraveling BSSA & The G6 Antidote

**(Players now have context, leads pointing to BSSA operations, and the compromised Montreal number).**

### 1. Investigating BSSA Fronts ([[Sequoia Falls, WA]])

* **[[White Stag Bar]] Investigation:**
    * Use [[Sean Fielding]]'s key. Discover basement meth lab. Find emails confirming [[Calvin Pierce]]'s F7 theft & access code **1489** for [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]].
* **[[LTX Headquarters]] Infiltration (Revisit?):**
    * If not done thoroughly before, return using Sean's keycard. Investigate workstation: clues about accessing [[Syd Foxglove]]'s account ([[Doolittle567 Password Clue]]?), researching [[The Snowflake]]. Find clue pointing back to the Church.
* **[[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] Infiltration:**
    * Use code **1489**. Confirm [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] production, [[Methamphetamine]] link. Discover basilisk testing wing ([[Fauxflake]] samples, [[Black Coral]] sample/victim room, [[The Echo]] recordings). Likely confront/neutralize Agent Chisel ([[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)]]). Find Syd's transfer records (July 15th to Church). Possibly find Ylynthii numeral hints.
* **[[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] (Revisit):**
    * Infiltrate past [[Hammerskull Crew]]. Discover sub-sanctuary [[Fauxflake]] apparatus/control room ([[Operation Parthenon]] / [[IO (Emerging 1997)]]). Solve pew puzzle. Find Syd's empty cell with [[Amanda Green Photograph]]. Find [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb Paperwork]] pointing to [[Ellensburg Area, WA]].

### 2. The Ellensburg Connection & Oliver's Secret

* **Tracking [[Amanda Green]] ([[Ellensburg Area, WA]]):**
    * Locate Amanda. Get Syd's [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] login. Learn about [[Monkey Gone to Heaven (Song)]] connection. Hear about [[Axon and Dendrite Recordings]] loaned to Seattle friend.
    * ***Pathway to Blanc (Optional):*** Follow up -> Seattle friend -> [[Maria Chen]] -> [[Éric Blanc]] context revealed.
* **[[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]] Investigation:**
    * Access Syd's account/infiltrate facility.
    * **[[Oliver Foxglove]] Emails:** Find final emails: Algorithm/[[Rhea]] horror, [[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]] at [[SDEC Facility]] importance, **sensitive bio-integration research secured in home safe**, code hinted via Syd's birthday.
    * Confirm Sean's rejected application, [[McGill University]]/[[Montreal, QC]] remote access logs.
    * Explore facility: [[Signal Dummies]]/[[Spectator Grubs]] production linked to [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]] data; potential combat ([[The Mannequin Spider]]?). Find [[Oliver Foxglove]]'s cipher notes (Ylynthii/Date/Keypad process). Find [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] clue.

### 3. The Road to G6 ([[Montreal, QC]] & Oliver's Safe)

* **Report to [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]]:** Share findings from Ellensburg/ErgoMatic.
    * Nine-String confirms [[Montreal Phone Number]] lead is significant. Reveals **[[Éric Blanc]]** as Alice's contact, provides primary number. *[[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]] potentially overhears details.*
    * Confirms [[SDEC Facility]] sealed, [[Agent Chrysalis]] importance. Tasks players re: finding Alice to locate missing Chrysalis.
* **Journey to [[Montreal, QC]]:** Players attempt contact with [[Éric Blanc]] using provided number. Burner phone likely fails or goes unanswered (due to [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]] leak -> Blanc's elimination by BSSA).
* **Montreal Scenario (BSSA Trap):**
    * Players learn (likely via [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]]/[[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]] relaying inability to reach Blanc, or via [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)]] contacts arranged by Nine-String) that Blanc is missing and presumed dead.
    * Guided by UMSE or own investigation, players locate **[[Éric Blanc's Safehouse]]**.
    * Inside, they find it eerily undisturbed but discover Blanc's computer **staged by BSSA**: powered on, logged into email, showing the lure message sent from Blanc's compromised account to [[Syd Foxglove]], directing him to the **[[McCord Museum]]**. Valuable resources like museum blueprints are present.
    * Recognizing the blatant staging and context (Blanc's death, compromised comms), players understand this is a trap BSSA *wants* them to spring.
    * **Museum Ambush:** Knowing the time/place, players coordinate with [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)]] to approach the [[McCord Museum]] cautiously near the rendezvous time. BSSA springs their trap, likely having Syd arrive unsuspectingly. BSSA cuts lights, deploys NVG agents to neutralize players while capturing Syd alive. Players must survive, protect Syd, potentially restore power, and facilitate UMSE extraction. Likely confrontation with/elimination of **[[Katherine Hill]]**.
* **Post-Montreal:** Assuming successful rescue of Syd, proceed to G6 synthesis phase.

## Act III: The Sealed Facility & The Final Choice

### 1. Curing Alice & The Foxglove Legacy

* **Syd's Revelation & The Safe Puzzle:** Rescued Syd confirms [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]] needs Oliver's bio-integration research. Players share Oliver's emails; Syd confirms safe relevance & birthday. Players combine **Ylynthii rules (cell) + Oliver's cipher notes (ErgoMatic) + Syd's birthday = safe code**.
* **[[Oliver Foxglove]] House & G6 Synthesis:** Access safe, retrieve advanced research notes. Syd uses notes + lab access to synthesize [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]].
* **Subdue and Cure:** Return to [[Sewers and Maintenance Tunnels]], locate/subdue [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] (potential [[Katherine Hill]]/[[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)]] interference), administer [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]]. Resolve BSSA/[[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]] vs. [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]] plot.
* **Alice's Debrief:** Cured Alice confirms [[Reapers]]/[[Writers]] reality. Recalls [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] meeting with [[Agent Chrysalis]], his fear/belief [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] destroyed [[SDEC Facility]], him taking [[Spectator Grubs]] samples, and revealing his **fallback location** to be [[PCBOT Headquarters]].
### 2. The Hunt for [[Agent Chrysalis]]

**Objective:** Infiltrate [[PCBOT Headquarters|PCBOT HQ]], rescue [[Agent Chrysalis|Chrysalis]] (or obtain [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents|SDEC docs]]), stop [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Caraway's]] "[[PCBOT Headquarters|Departing Flight]]" airstrike. This is Alice's final act.
* **Infiltration:** Players travel to DC, plan infiltration (possibly aided by [[VTFT (Virginia Track & Field Team)|VTFT]] diversion). Explore facility (tourism cover, blacksite, "[[PCBOT Headquarters|Guest Suite]]", "[[PCBOT Headquarters|Baggage Claim]]"). Find [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]] samples.
* **Player Deductions:** Based on prior clues, *players deduce* G5/G6 enhance pattern recognition needed for decryption, and *players deduce* the [[BNJC Radiometry Array]] can jam signals.
* **Confrontation & Escalation:** Players find Chrysalis. Confrontation pushes the cornered Caraway to initiate the "[[PCBOT Headquarters|Departing Flight]]" airstrike protocol (thermobaric strike on Sequoia Falls, cover story: old LTX explosives). Countdown begins.
* **Climax - Decryption & Jamming:** Players enact plan. [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]] volunteers for chemical decryption. She takes G5 or G6. Decrypts frequencies. Data relayed to [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)|Nine-String]]. Nine-String uses [[BNJC Radiometry Array|BNJC array]] to jam strike.
* **Alice's Fate & Escape:** Alice dies later (G5/G6+F7 pathway) or becomes automaton (G6 pathway). Players escape with Chrysalis OR [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents|SDEC docs]].


### 3. Infiltrating [[SDEC Facility]]

* **Journey to [[San Diego, CA]]:** Travel with Chrysalis (+/- allies) to the [[SDEC Facility]].
* **Bypass Ant Trap:** Chrysalis uses unique credentials/knowledge to bypass the seal.
* **The Dungeon Crawl:** Explore derelict, hazardous facility. Face automated defenses ([[BRACHINUS Class Turrets]]?), remnants of 1992 conflict, possibly the [[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)]].

### 4. [[Winston Foxglove|Winston's]] Truth

* **Finding the Memoirs:** Locate [[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]].
* **Decoding the Hexagons:** Show the [[Hexagon Pages (Winston's Memoirs)]] to [[Syd Foxglove]] (or G6 user) to decode.
* **The Revelation:** Decoded text reveals 1922 [[Royal Jelly]] encounter, [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] origin, and [[Dead Horse Quarry]] [[Hive]] location. Players understand [[Rhea]]'s origin/plan.

### 5. The Endgame Confrontation

* **Journey to [[Dead Horse Quarry]]**.
* **[[Watchers|Watcher]] Encounter:** Intercepted en route. Watcher communicates [[Rhea]]'s nature/goals, demands [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]] for "transcendence".
* **Final Choices:** Players decide: Give G6? Deceive (F7?)? Attack Watcher? Proceed to Hive? Go public?
* **The Hive Finale:** Based on choice, final scene at quarry/Hive entrance. Confront [[Rhea]]'s defenses, attempt destruction, negotiate, or face consequences of decision regarding G6.
