# The Cycles of Rhea: Full Campaign Narrative Outline

---

## Act I: The Missing Brother & The Tangled Web (Oct 3rd - ~Oct 10th, 1997)

### 1. The Hook (Friday, Oct 3rd)

* **Timeshare Seminar:** Players attend a [[Sebastian International]] timeshare seminar at the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]].
* **[[Emily Fielding]] Recruits:** Outside, Emily Fielding approaches the PCs, seeking help finding her missing brother, [[Sean Fielding]]. She shows them a strange letter addressed to Sean from "Alphonse Spicecube" via Sebastian International.
* **[[Sean Fielding's House]] Investigation:** Players accompany Emily to Sean's house (55 Rosebush Ct).
    * **Clues:** Messy house, party remnants, tracked dirt, empty [[Signal Dummies]] boxes (addressed to "Alphonse Spicecube" from [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]]), [[Hammerskull Crew]] shirt, possibly drugs.
    * **Graffiti:** [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s red [[The Hexacle]] patterns and "Please God, Forgive Me" message on walls.
    * **[[Reapers]] Encounter:** Players spot a "Mr. Honeycomb" figure in the backyard. It emits blinding flashes/pops, causing disorientation before vanishing. Players retreat to [[Pastor Mike]]'s house.

### 2. Following Initial Leads (Saturday, Oct 4th - Monday, Oct 6th)

* **Split Investigation (Oct 4th):**
    * Investigate return address (3704 S. Preston Rd – LTX office). Experience [[The Hexacle]] effect at the door; later enter the office.
    * Research at library: uncover [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]]/[[The Foxglove Family]] history, [[Oliver Foxglove]] death, [[Syd Foxglove]] missing, [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] merger. Learn about Sean's [[Hammerskull Crew]] ties.
    * **Alphonse Spicecube Encounter:** Confront the [[Signal Dummies]] automaton in the office. Combat; find clues: potting soil, boxes, water hose, [[Spectator Grubs]].
* **Church Visit & Witness Interviews (Oct 5th):**
    * [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]: turned away by [[Hammerskull Crew]] security. Obtain newspaper article.
    * Interview [[Dan Calhoun]] at [[Sauls Supply Station]]. Description of "Jazz Lady" buying cattle prod, red spray paint, glue. CCTV confirms identity.
    * Investigate victim addresses ([[Sarah Prescott]], [[Thomas Reeves]]) — signs of Alice's M.O.
* **Sample Analysis (Oct 6th):**
    * Drop off samples with [[Prof. Alexander Austen]]. Set up meeting with [[Mandy Archer]].

### 3. Escalation & Revelations (Tuesday, Oct 7th - Wednesday, Oct 8th)

* **[[LTX Headquarters]] Visit & Police Contact (Oct 7th):**
    * Infiltrate LTX. Interview [[Alex Kramer]], [[Jessica Herring]].
    * Contact Det. [[Colin Reeves]]. Learn about missing persons moratorium and [[Edmund White|Balloon Mask Killer]] files pulled. Connects "Jazz Lady" to Alice.
* **[[Redwood Shopping Mall]] Encounter (Oct 7th):**
    * Trace [[Signal Dummies|"Quentin Cinnabon"]] packages to the mall.
    * Confront Alice dragging disabled Dummy.
    * Alice opens fire, killing [[Pastor Mike]]. Players grab Alice's satchel ([[Keys (Alice's Satchel)]], [[Alice's Journal (Satchel)]]) and flee.
* **[[Mirror-Breaker Organizations]] Contact (Oct 7th/8th):**
    * [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)]] agents "Thistle & Thorn" arrive. Explain BNJC connection. Deliver cryptic items.
* **[[Alice Redmond's House]] Investigation & Attack (Oct 8th):**
    * Investigate dusty apartment. Find [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] statue, [[Labyrinths by Jorge Luis Borges]] book with note from "Nine-String", [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)]] syllabus.
    * Crack vault code. Find [[CALLISTO Report]], [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol Notes]], [[Alice's Map]], preserved [[Spectator Grubs]], dossier on [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]].
    * [[Reapers]] attacks from ceiling. Kills Reeves. Players fight/flee.

### 4. Entering the Underground (Post Oct 8th)

* **[[BNJC HQ]] Debriefing:**
    * Players access HQ via elevator. Meet [[Travis Ward (Agent Tremolo)]], [[Leah Burns (Agent Legato)]]; possibly [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]].
    * Official debriefing: Mirror-Breakers, SDEC destruction, PCBOT factional split, Alice's rogue status, Dylan missing, Katherine identified, BSSA antagonism. Mole [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]] possibly present.
* **Following [[Alice's Map]] → COTU**
* **[[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] Discovery:**
    * Aftermath of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]-poisoning, [[Calvin Pierce]]'s body, [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]] shipping container, identical-car ranks of [[Signal Dummies]], [[Sean Fielding]]'s body in trunk.
    * Alice's apparent base camp: [[Adderall]] bottles, cot, papers, note with **[[Montreal Phone Number]] (514-874-2313)**.
    * Discovery of desiccated "Thermos Milkshaft" still wearing PCBOT-issue handcuffs, allowing the players to infer Alice's confrontation with Dylan Hill.
* **End of Act I:** Players return to [[BNJC HQ]]. [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]] *definitely* overhears the Montreal number this time if not before. Players now possess leads toward BSSA fronts ([[White Stag Bar]], [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]], [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]) and the Montreal connection.

## Act II: Unraveling BSSA & The G6 Antidote

### 1. Investigating BSSA Fronts ([[Sequoia Falls, WA]])

* **[[White Stag Bar]]:** Use Sean's key. Basement meth lab. Emails confirming Calvin's F7 theft and access code **1489** for Saint Anselm's.
* **[[LTX Headquarters]] Infiltration:** Sean's keycard. Workstation clues for accessing Syd's account, [[The Snowflake]] research, pointer back to the Church.
* **[[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]]:** Use code 1489. Confirm F7 production, basilisk testing wing ([[Fauxflake]], [[Black Coral]], [[The Echo]]). Confront Agent Chisel. Syd's transfer records (July 15th to Church). Ylynthii numeral hints in former cell.
* **[[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] (Revisit):** Infiltrate past [[Hammerskull Crew]]. Sub-sanctuary Fauxflake apparatus. Solve pew puzzle. Syd's empty cell with [[Amanda Green Photograph]] and more Ylynthii markings. [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb Paperwork]] pointing to Ellensburg.

### 2. The Ellensburg Connection & Oliver's Secret

* **Tracking [[Amanda Green]] ([[Ellensburg Area, WA]]):**
    * Locate Amanda. Get Syd's [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] login. Hear about [[Axon and Dendrite Recordings]] loaned to her Seattle friend. Lead toward [[Brewster's Lockbox]] / [[Maria Chen]] (the Montreal-gate chain).
* **[[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]]:**
    * Access Syd's account.
    * **[[Oliver Foxglove Emails]]:** Find final emails — Algorithm/Rhea horror sparked by *Syd's own Ylynthii doodling*; the realization that GenAssist and the tissue printer are spontaneously replicating Winston's algorithm; [[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]] at [[SDEC Facility]]; sensitive bio-integration research and a vacuum-sealed reagent secured in home safe; code based on Andrea Foxglove's birthday using Oliver's cipher.
    * Also note the **"suspicious log-in from Montreal IP" warnings** in Syd's account — these were what Sean reported to Katherine, providing BSSA's first thread into the Montreal lead independent of Shutter's later leak.
    * Find [[Oliver Foxglove's Cipher Notes]] (Ylynthii → keypad).
    * Explore the facility; [[Signal Dummies]]/[[Spectator Grubs]] production confirmation; potential combat ([[The Mannequin Spider]]?). Find [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] clue.

### 3. The Ellensburg Numbers Station & Brewster's Lockbox

* Investigate the abandoned [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]: old "Seventh Hour" flyers, the [[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]], the [[Brewster's Final Segment Sequence Log]], and the trail to Brewster's lockbox keeper ([[Professor Vance]]).
* Solve [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]]. Retrieve **[[Maria Chen]]'s contact info** in [[Portland, OR]].

### 4. Maria Chen and the Montreal Gate

* **Travel to Portland.** Interview [[Maria Chen]]. With sufficient trust, she discloses **[[Éric Blanc]]'s apartment address in Montreal** — the address BNJC does not have.
* (Parallel branch: the Amanda Green → Seattle friend chain can also reach Chen.)

### 5. Report to Nine-String

* Players report findings. [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)|Nine-String]] confirms Blanc's role and admits he himself has no current address or working channel. He has lost contact with Alice (via Chrysalis), and the burner number to Syd is dead.
* Tasks players to find both Alice (for cure) and Syd (for G6 synthesis), and arranges UMSE field support in Montreal once they have a destination.
* This briefing is overheard at least in part by Agent Shutter, who will alert PCBOT/BSSA the moment the players depart for Montreal.

### 6. Montreal: Apartment, Trap, and Museum

* **Arrival in Montreal.** Coordinate with UMSE.
* **[[Éric Blanc's Apartment]] (Counter-Intelligence Path):**
    * Find it **nearly ransacked**: drawers emptied, signs of struggle near the door, no body.
    * Wall calendar with circled days; **museum receipts**; photographs of Blanc and Alice; partial notebooks.
    * **Blanc's computer**, intact, with an encrypted home volume containing the **PCBOT tunnel schematics and the SDEC remote-access bypass methodology** — locked behind a passphrase only Alice knows.
    * The combined evidence makes the trap clear: Blanc has been captured; BSSA holds his email and is steering correspondents to the McCord Museum on a specific date; Syd is unwittingly on his way there for a long-standing emergency rendezvous.
* **Museum Ambush at the [[McCord Museum]]:**
    * Knowing the time and place, players coordinate with UMSE to approach cautiously near the rendezvous time. BSSA cuts lights, deploys NVG agents, attempts to capture Syd alive and neutralize the players.
    * Players must survive, protect Syd, and exfiltrate. Likely confrontation with [[Katherine Hill]]. Blanc, if recovered at all, most likely dies during the firefight.
* **Exfil with Syd, the encrypted drive, and any rescued/recovered material from Blanc.**

## Act III: The Cure, the Bypass, and the Sealed Facility

### 1. G6 Synthesis and Alice's Cure (The Required Checkpoint)

* **Syd's Revelation & The Safe Puzzle:** Rescued Syd confirms G6 needs Oliver's notes and the sealed reagent. Players share Oliver's emails; Syd confirms safe relevance and Andrea Foxglove's birthday. Players combine Ylynthii rules (from Syd's cell) + Oliver's cipher notes (ErgoMatic) + the birthday → safe code.
* **[[Oliver Foxglove]] House:** Access safe. Retrieve advanced research notes **and** the vacuum-sealed reagent.
* **Synthesize G6:** Syd uses notes + reagent + McGill-or-equivalent lab access to synthesize [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]].
* **Subdue and Cure Alice:** Return to the [[Sewers and Maintenance Tunnels]], locate/subdue Alice, administer G6. Potential interference from BSSA remnants and/or PCBOT scouts.
* **Decrypt the Blanc Drive:** A cured, lucid Alice provides the passphrase. The drive yields the **PCBOT tunnel schematics** and the **SDEC remote-access bypass methodology**. Alice walks the team through the procedure and confirms it must be executed in person at PCBOT — she is the only one who can read the live PCBOT internal network against the 1996 schematics and time the bypass.
* **Alice's Debrief:** Cured Alice confirms Reaper/Writer reality, recalls Chrysalis's panicked flight to PCBOT, and explains how the SDEC remote terminal route exists at all. She commits to accompanying the players to DC.

### 2. PCBOT Excursion (Bypass & Remote Access)

**Objective:** Infiltrate [[PCBOT Headquarters|PCBOT HQ]], use Alice's bypass methodology to obtain a live remote terminal into the sealed [[SDEC Facility]], extract Winston's memoirs and any remaining SDEC archive, and survive [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Caraway's]] "[[PCBOT Headquarters|Departing Flight]]" airstrike protocol.

* **Infiltration:** Travel to DC, plan infiltration (possibly aided by [[VTFT (Virginia Track & Field Team)|VTFT]] diversion). Explore the facility (tourism cover, blacksite, "Guest Suite", "Baggage Claim"). Find [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]] samples.
* **Alice Executes the Bypass:** Following the methodology, Alice (with player support) makes her way to the PCBOT secure terminal room and opens a remote session to SDEC, retrieving Winston's memoirs and SDEC archives.
* **Chrysalis (optional):** Players may locate Chrysalis in the Guest Suite — alive, broken, dead, or somewhere in between. His fate is narrative, not load-bearing for the SDEC access.
* **Caraway Reacts:** The infiltration confirms Caraway's worst fear — containment has failed. He initiates the "Departing Flight" airstrike protocol against Sequoia Falls. Countdown begins.
* **Climax - Decryption & Jamming:** Players deduce that enhanced pattern recognition (G5 or G6) is needed to decrypt the strike frequencies. Alice volunteers for chemical decryption, decrypts the frequencies, relays them to Nine-String, who uses the [[BNJC Radiometry Array]] to jam the strike.
* **Alice's Fate & Escape:** Alice dies later (G5 / G6+F7 pathway) or becomes automaton (G6 pathway). Players escape with whatever SDEC data was successfully extracted.

### 3. Decoding Winston's Memoirs

* Show the [[Hexagon Pages (Winston's Memoirs)|Hexagon Pages]] to [[Syd Foxglove]] (or surviving G6 user) to decode.
* Decoded text reveals the 1922 [[Royal Jelly]] encounter, [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]'s origin, and the [[Dead Horse Quarry]] [[Hive]] location.

### 4. (Optional) On-Site SDEC Visit

* If the team still wants physical access — e.g., to recover artifacts the remote terminal could not extract, or to verify the data — they can travel to [[San Diego, CA]] and physically enter the [[SDEC Facility]]. With the bypass methodology already burned in DC, on-site entry is now possible (or at least less impossible). This becomes a "dungeon crawl" rather than a required-path operation.

### 5. The Endgame Confrontation

* **Journey to [[Dead Horse Quarry]]**.
* **[[Watchers|Watcher]] Encounter:** Intercepted en route. Watcher communicates Rhea's nature/goals, demands [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]] for "transcendence".
* **Final Choices:** Give G6? Deceive (F7?)? Attack Watcher? Proceed to Hive? Go public?
* **The Hive Finale:** Final scene at quarry/Hive entrance. Confront Rhea's defenses, attempt destruction, negotiate, or face consequences of decision regarding G6.
