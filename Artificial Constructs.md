This page serves as an index for artificial entities created or utilized within the campaign, often related to [[Rhea]] or other complex systems.

## Known Constructs

* **[[Signal Dummies]]**: Predictable, biological automatons used by Rhea for logistical tasks and network maintenance.
* **[[Spectator Grubs]]**: Small, insectoid constructs (both organic and synthetic) involved in information relay.
* [[The Mannequin Spider]]: An autonomously-assembled defense unit created from Signal Dummy parts that lurks in the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]]. It killed Jason Tillman on April 3rd of 1997 and Gabby Hollingsworth on September 8th. Finding and neutralizing the spider is an important part of exploring the manufacturing floor. 
* [[BRACHINUS Class Turrets]]: Autonomous defense weapons in the SDEC facility that deploy as part of the Ant Trap protocol, guarding exits to prevent dispersal of potentially hazardous InfoHazards should the seal be breached from the inside.
