## Overview

**Agent Chrysalis** (Christopher Gruber) is a Mirror-Breaker agent currently affiliated with the [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]]. He is notably the **last surviving field agent of the [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]]** following its destruction in 1992. He serves as a crucial, albeit conflicted, support contact and potential guide for the investigators.

## Background & History

* **[[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] Affiliation & Expertise:** Originally an SDEC agent, Chrysalis specialized in field observation and likely operated under their entomology-based metaphorical protocols. His survival was partly luck; he remained in Sequoia Falls monitoring the aftermath of [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]] while his colleague [[Agent Diaphanous (Diane Collins)|Agent Diaphanous]] returned to San Diego with the CICADA sonar readings that, in Chrysalis's mind, doomed the facility.
* **Witness to BSSA Activity:** During the EUROPA shutdown in mid-1992, Chrysalis recognized BSSA Agent [[Raymond Vaughn (Agent Rococo)|Rococo]] surreptitiously removing modulator equipment from the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. He reported this directly to [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Lopez]] at [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|PCBOT]], establishing BSSA's link to the event just before SDEC was destroyed.
* **SDEC Trauma & Core Belief:** The destruction of SDEC and the loss of his colleagues deeply traumatized Chrysalis. He clings firmly to the belief that possessing dangerous, pattern-based information (like Diaphanous's sonar readings) attracts catastrophic destruction from the entity involved. This paranoia dictates his later actions.
* **[[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]] Affiliation:** Following SDEC's destruction, Chrysalis integrated with BNJC, bringing his unique SDEC perspective and field experience.
* **Relationship with Alice:** He developed strong feelings for [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]], which [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)|Nine-String]] likely leveraged when assigning Chrysalis as her covert support contact after she went rogue. His relative distance from BNJC HQ also made him a suitable handler. He maintained the vital secure communication link between Alice and [[Syd Foxglove]] in Montreal while Syd worked on the [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] formula.
* **COTU Incident Involvement:** Accompanied Alice to [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU]] on September 25th, 1997, witnessing the mass casualties from [[Calvin Pierce]]'s [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] distribution. He fled the scene in horror, which created uncertainty at BNJC about Alice's state and actions.

## Flight to PCBOT & Captivity (Late Sept - Mid Oct 1997)

Immediately after the disturbing discoveries at COTU – the [[Spectator Grubs]] and Alice's worsening condition – Chrysalis's SDEC trauma resurfaced intensely. Seeing the alien Grubs as potentially another dangerous data-vector like the sonar readings, he panicked, fearing history would repeat and BNJC would be destroyed. Knowing [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|PCBOT]] was antagonistic (and linked to Alice's poisoning via [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]]), but desperate, he enacted a complex gamble driven by fear, his love for Alice, and a sliver of vengeful hope: he took Grub samples directly to [[PCBOT Headquarters|PCBOT HQ]] in DC. His desperate logic was twofold: either force PCBOT to acknowledge the threat and reconcile with BNJC, *or* draw the destructive entity's attention onto PCBOT itself, thus sparing BNJC and achieving a form of revenge. Before fleeing Sequoia Falls, he told the F7-impaired Alice where he was going. Upon arrival, he was immediately detained by Director [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Caraway]] and held captive for over two weeks, likely interrogated within the '[[PCBOT Headquarters|Guest Suite]]'.

## Planned Role in PCBOT Excursion (Mid Oct 1997)

Chrysalis is the primary objective when the player characters infiltrate [[PCBOT Headquarters|PCBOT HQ]], as his knowledge as the last SDEC agent is essential for accessing the sealed [[SDEC Facility]]. Players find him held captive. During the subsequent confrontation with Caraway and the chaos of the '[[PCBOT Headquarters|Departing Flight]]' crisis, Chrysalis's fate hangs in the balance. He may be rescued by the players, can die during the confrontation or escape, or might survive even if the airstrike succeeds. If rescued, he is able to provide guidance and potentially credentials to access SDEC. If he dies, players need to locate the [[SDEC Ant Trap Protocol Documents]] within PCBOT as their backup plan for entering the SDEC facility.
