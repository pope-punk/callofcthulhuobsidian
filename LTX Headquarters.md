## Overview
The corporate headquarters and primary operational center for [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]], located in the industrial district of [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].
![[Our Story LTX - Copy.png|500]]
## Layout & Features
* **Corporate Offices:** Sterile, Fincher-esque aesthetics. Houses management, HR ([[Jessica Herring]]), etc. Represents corporate indifference.
* **Shipping Operations Center:** Hub for managing regional logistics. Contains data related to shipping manifests, including the anomalous "dirt" shipments.
* **Warehouses (incl. Warehouse 14):** Site of original [[The Snowflake]] pattern implementation. Used for sorting/storing goods, including anomalous dirt packages destined for [[Signal Dummies]]. Warehouse 19 also potentially significant.
* **Historical Archives (2nd Floor):** Contains older company records, potentially including original [[Snowflake Pattern Diagrams]] sought by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] and [[Sean Fielding]].
* **[[Sean Fielding]]'s Workstation:** Located on the 2nd floor. Contains clues related to his mission.

## Significance
* Key node in [[Rhea]]'s network post-merger with [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]].
* Site of anomalous "dirt" shipments linked to [[Signal Dummies]].
* Location where [[Sean Fielding]] attempted to infiltrate and find [[Snowflake Pattern Diagrams]].
* Site where [[Colin Reeves]] was investigating his brother [[Thomas Reeves]]'s disappearance.
