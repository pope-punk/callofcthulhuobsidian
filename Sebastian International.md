## Overview
A shell company, originally founded in the 1970s and bought out by [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] in 1989. It was shuttered but reactivated in May 1997, likely repurposed by [[Rhea]] after the merger.

## Role in Plot
* Operates under the cover of **vacation property management / timeshares**.
* Hosted the timeshare seminar on Oct 3, 1997 at the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]] where [[Emily Fielding]] met the player characters, kicking off the investigation into [[Sean Fielding]]'s disappearance.
* Representative encountered: Jeremy Rivers.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Operational in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] as a likely minor front or observational tool for [[Rhea]]'s network.
