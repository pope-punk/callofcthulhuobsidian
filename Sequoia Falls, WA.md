## Overview
Primary setting for the start of the [[Cycles of Rhea]] campaign in October 1997. A city in the Pacific Northwest experiencing a tech boom ("New Silicon Valley") driven by [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] innovations. Population has grown rapidly (~20k to ~40k). Founded 1883, originally mining/logging town. Noted for high police response to homelessness. Close proximity to [[Dead Horse Quarry]].

## Significance
The central hub for [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]] and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] operations, the corporate HQ of [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]], and the primary location for player investigation in the campaign's first act. Site of numerous key events and character deaths.
