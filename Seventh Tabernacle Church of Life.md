## Overview
A [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] front organization operating under the cover of a church in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. Primary site for [[Operation Parthenon]] (cultivating [[IO (Emerging 1997)]] RONIN). Led publicly by [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]]. Security by [[Hammerskull Crew]].

## Layout & Features
- Public Church Area (strict rules).
 ![[No Jazz Clothes 2 Less Wear.png|250]]
- **Underground Chamber:** Hidden facility for [[Operation Parthenon]]. Contains:
    - Mirrored Ceiling & Modular Pew System (arranged in the 3D **[[Fauxflake]]** pattern).
    - Specialized **Viewing Apparatus** (used to observe pew layout from above for translation into 2D drawings).
    - Control Room (for monitoring IO emergence / chamber conditions).
    - **Holding Cell:** Held [[Syd Foxglove]] from Late Jan - Apr/May 1997.
- Maintenance/Storage Area (contains [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb Paperwork]]).

## Role in Syd Foxglove's Captivity (Jan-July 1997)
* **Fauxflake Refinement Site (Late Jan - Apr/May):** Syd was transferred here from St. Anselm's specifically to use his Algorithm aptitude to refine the [[Fauxflake]] pattern under [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]]'s supervision. His input significantly improved efficacy.
* **Target of Scrutiny:** [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]]'s growing suspicion about activities here led [[Katherine Hill]] to move Syd away temporarily.
* **Intended Return Destination (July 15):** Katherine ordered Syd transferred *back* to the Church to resume Fauxflake work, but this transfer was intercepted by Dylan/ambushed by Alice.
