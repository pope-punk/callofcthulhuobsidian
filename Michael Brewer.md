## Description

Michael Brewer is a former astronomy professor who became homeless and resided in the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] encampment.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **Naming COTU**: He is credited with naming the encampment "the center of the universe" in January 1996, likely referencing his background and the location's unique properties (being accessible despite [[The Hexacle]] protection).
* **[[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] Community**: A key figure in the formation of the community, which consisted primarily of F7-exposed individuals released from [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]].
* **[[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] Exposure**: As a resident and founder, he was certainly exposed to [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]], either during initial BSSA testing or via [[Calvin Pierce]]'s later distribution.

## Status

Michael Brewer had left the Center of the Universe before the F7 overdose incident. However, he was arrested for vagrancy and released back into the custody of [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]]. Discharged Center for Hope patients are given a bus ticket and expected to leave the city immediately - Brewer's continued presence in Sequoia Falls marked him for arrest.
