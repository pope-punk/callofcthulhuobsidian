## Overview
Deacon Anselm Chambers, formerly known as Andrew Chamberlain, is a key operational figure within the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation]] and a significant antagonist. He is the charismatic and manipulative leader of BSSA's primary front organizations in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]: the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] and the [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]]. His expertise lies in the practical application of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] principles to create infohazardous effects and manipulate human perception, a skill honed through his past creation of the infamous mind-altering arcade game, "[[Polybius]]," and his subsequent leadership of a related cult.

## From Arcade Cultist to BSSA Deacon
Andrew Chamberlain's journey into the shadowy world of infohazards began in 1978 when he discovered archived documentation related to [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] at [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]]. Recognizing its potential, he left LTX and, likely under the guise of his company Sierra Entertainment (possibly a shell), created the arcade game "[[Polybius]]" in the early 1980s. This game, built upon Algorithm principles, was designed to induce specific psychological and perceptual effects in its players, quickly gathering a devoted, almost cult-like following in the Seattle area.

In 1984, BSSA, then a relatively new [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations|Mirror-Breaker Organization]], raided Chamberlain's Polybius Cult. However, with the clandestine assistance of [[Katherine Hill]] (then BSSA Agent Caryatid) and [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Henry Caraway]] (then a PCBOT agent), Chamberlain faked his own death. He re-emerged with a new identity: Deacon Anselm Chambers, now a loyal and valuable asset to BSSA, bringing his unique understanding of applied infohazards.

## Architect of BSSA's Infohazard Operations
As Deacon Chambers, he became instrumental in BSSA's efforts to weaponize and control informational patterns:
* **[[Operation Colossus]] (1986-1992):** In 1986, Chambers established the [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] in [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]] as BSSA's front for [[Operation Colossus]]. He recruited former Polybius addicts, [[Jacob Wise]] and [[Erin Cross]], as his lieutenants. From this base, he managed the "Seventh Hour" radio program, which broadcast [[The Echo]] auditory basilisk using the repurposed [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] infrastructure, laying the groundwork for the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN.
* **Sequoia Falls Operations (1995 onwards):** Following the termination of Colossus and BSSA's strategic shift, Chambers relocated to [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. In 1995, he relaunched the Ellensburg front as the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]], the new hub for [[Operation Parthenon]]—BSSA's ambitious project to cultivate the stable [[IO (Emerging 1997)]] RONIN using the [[Fauxflake]] visual basilisk. The [[Fauxflake]] itself, derived from [[The Snowflake]] pattern, was largely Chambers' design, drawing on his Polybius experience. Concurrently, he established [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] as a cover for [[Project Ozymandias]], BSSA's program for developing and testing [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] and other psychoactive compounds.
* **[[Syd Foxglove]] Exploitation (1997):** When [[Fauxflake]] development plateaued in 1996, Chambers and Katherine Hill recognized the need for [[The Foxglove Family|Foxglove]] knowledge. After Syd's capture, Chambers directly oversaw Syd's forced collaboration in refining the [[Fauxflake]] pattern beneath the Seventh Tabernacle.

## Current Status and Motivations (Late October 1997)
Deacon Anselm Chambers is currently active as the manager of BSSA's operations at the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] and [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]], under the overall direction of [[Katherine Hill]]. He is deeply involved in the ongoing [[Operation Parthenon]] and [[Project Ozymandias]]. While charismatic and seemingly devout in his public persona, Chambers is a ruthless operative, fully committed to BSSA's goals of understanding and controlling infohazards, and by extension, human consciousness. His past as a cult leader who directly manipulated minds with Algorithm-derived technology makes him a particularly dangerous figure, embodying the seductive and corrupting power of such knowledge. He represents a direct, local threat to the investigators as they delve deeper into BSSA's activities in Sequoia Falls.
