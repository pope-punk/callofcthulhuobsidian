## Overview
Born in 1960 to CIA agent parents. 

[[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]] Agent Destination. Assigned as liaison between [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]], [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]], and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] starting mid-1993 as part of [[Jacqui Lopez (Agent Javelin)|Jacqui Lopez]]'s faction's plan. Married to[[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill]].

## Key Traits

- Initially served as a double agent, filtering information reported to both [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]] and [[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill.]]
- Diligent investigator; uncovered [[Rhea]]'s network activity ([[Signal Dummies]], dirt shipments) within [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]].
- Grew disillusioned with Katherine Hill / [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].

## History

- **1980s**: CIA field agent involved in POUTINE. Met Katherine during infrastructure transfer from CIA to PCBOT/BSSA. Became entangled and married in 1988. Filled CIA supervisory role with respect to Operation Colossus until officially recruited to PCBOT in 1990. 
- **1992**: Carried out torture and execution of David Brewster in wake of the SDEC incident. Returned to Boise with Katherine Hill. 
- **1993:** Re-deployed to [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] with BNJC as PCBOT liaison. Primary focus - helping keep BNJC away from BSSA facility nearing completion under Seventh Tabernacle.
- **1995:** Became sole source of LTX data after Caraway coup at PCBOT - all records fed through him. He made sure that if any signs of "RONIN leakage" were evidenced in LTX shipping records, BNJC wouldn't find out before BSSA could adjust their strategy accordingly. 
- **1996**: Saint Anselm's Center for Hope is completed with discrete PCBOT funding. 
- **January 1997**: Holds Syd in a cell beneath his home until a space can found for him at the Seventh Tabernacle. An impromptu cell is created beneath the floor of the basement. 
- **March 1997:** Noticed pattern of "dirt" shipments to absurd recipient names ("Thermos Milkshaft," etc.) at LTX. Begins to suspect Basilisk refinement at Seventh Tabernacle could be going the way of EUROPA. [[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill]] dismissed findings as irrelevant.

- **April-June 1997:** Repeatedly attempted to discuss anomalies with increasingly hostile Katherine. Secretly copied key shipping manifests. PCBOT (likely at Katherine's request) revoked his LTX credentials and reassigned him to [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] testing oversight and guarding [[Syd Foxglove]], who he was to transfer to [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]].
- **June/July 1997:** Working primarily at the Center for Hope. Learns about Syd's apparent immunity to the usual effects of Basilisks. Begins talking to Syd about [[Oliver Foxglove]]'s death, and begins questioning his own assumptions that Syd had been exposed to the Echo. He doesn't believe in the existence of monsters, but it seems very clear something is going on outside of the BSSA's control. Appeared infrequently at [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]] during this time.
- **July 13, 1997:** Visited [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]] for last time, claiming he was returning to DC.
- **July 14, 1997:** Tailed by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] from his home to [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]].
- **July 15, 1997:** While attempting to move [[Syd Foxglove]] from The Center for Hope back to the Seventh Tabernacle, under Katherine's orders, he was ambushed by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]. Taken to [[Edmund White Estate]]. Refused to reveal [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] operations (Colossus, Parthenon, Ozymandias) to Alice. Shared his LTX findings but demanded to speak to Caraway before confessing to his reasons for having Syd in his custody. When Alice threatened to torture him into revealing the truth, he attempted to escape, injecting her with massive [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] dose in the process. Subsequently killed by Alice in rage.

## Status

- Deceased (Killed July 15, 1997). His shipping manifests (now held by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]) and notes on F7/G5 (recovered by Alice from his body) are crucial plot items. His murder is a key event precipitating Alice's descent.
