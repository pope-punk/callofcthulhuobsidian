## Overview
The highly sensitive personal research notes secured by [[Oliver Foxglove]] in his home safe shortly before his murder. Contains breakthroughs related to biological integration and the Algorithm.

## Content
* Details Oliver's advanced work on **bio-integration**, **DNA-Machine Code Conversion**, and the **neurological/biological effects of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]**.
* Contains the specific insights into Algorithm/DNA interaction needed by [[Syd Foxglove]] to overcome the theoretical hurdles in synthesizing the non-toxic **[[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]]**.

## Significance
* Represents the scientific key required to create [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]].
* Its acquisition is necessary to cure [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] and potentially confront [[Rhea]]'s endgame plan.
* Confirms the link between the Foxglove Algorithm, advanced biotech, and the potential for chemical countermeasures/enhancements.

## Location & Status (Oct 1997)
* Secured within the safe at the [[Oliver Foxglove House]]. Accessible only after solving the [[Safe Puzzle]]. Finding these notes is the reward for completing the puzzle and a crucial step before synthesizing G6.
