## Overview
A prominent university located in [[Montreal, QC]].

## Significance
* **Syd's Research Site:** [[Syd Foxglove]] used chemistry facilities here between July and October 1997 to conduct research into [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]], building on his own innate Algorithm-derived chemical intuition and his analysis of [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]]. Access was arranged via [[Éric Blanc]].
* **Where Blanc Was When BSSA Hit His Apartment:** Blanc was at McGill supporting Syd when BSSA broke into [[Éric Blanc's Apartment]] in early October. He was captured shortly afterward while returning home; McGill itself remained uncompromised.
* **Possible BSSA Surveillance Point:** Once BSSA had Blanc, his connection to McGill became a low-grade surveillance target — though the focus of the trap shifted entirely to the [[McCord Museum]] standing-protocol date.

## Status (Late Oct 1997)
A functioning university. May retain incidental evidence of Syd's recent research activity. Light BSSA/NOPG surveillance possible, but not the primary stake-out site.
