## Overview
A prominent university located in [[Montreal, QC]].

## Significance
* **Syd's Research Site:** [[Syd Foxglove]] utilized chemistry facilities here between July and October 1997 to conduct research into [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]], based on [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]]'s notes and his analysis of [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]]. Access was arranged via [[Éric Blanc]].
* **Blanc Elimination Site:** Likely the general vicinity where BSSA operatives located and eliminated [[Éric Blanc]] shortly after October 10th, 1997, due to his role as Syd's protector and facilitator.
* **Point of Origin for Lure:** BSSA identified Syd's connection to McGill (likely via surveillance triggered by the [[Montreal Phone Number]] leak) before eliminating Blanc and initiating their lure/ambush plan targeting the [[McCord Museum]].

## Status (Late Oct 1997)
Site of [[Éric Blanc]]'s recent murder. May contain trace evidence related to Blanc or Syd's research activities. Security might be subtly heightened or monitored by BSSA/allied assets ([[NOPG (New Orleans Photography Group)]]?) following the recent activity.
