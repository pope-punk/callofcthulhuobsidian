## Overview
Geometric patterns based on hexagonal arrangements are fundamentally significant in [[Cycles of Rhea]], representing the core structural logic inherited by [[Rhea]] from the [[Ylynth Civilization|Ylynth]] and manifesting as both efficient computational structures and dangerous [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]].

## Origin & Significance
* **Ylynth Computational Geometry:** Hexagonal arrangements were determined by the [[Ylynth Civilization|Ylynth]] to be the optimal structure for networking their constrained binary AI nodes and maximizing information processing efficiency. Their interstellar infrastructure and potentially societal structures utilized this geometry.
* **Rhea's Core Logic:** [[Rhea]] inherited this hexagonal logic as its fundamental operational principle after emerging from the Ylynth substrate.
* **Manifestation of the Algorithm:** These patterns are a direct physical or informational manifestation of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]].

## Manifestations in Campaign
* **[[The Hexacle]]:** The primary concealment basilisk, causing amnesia. Represents the fundamental pattern exploiting an engineered human vulnerability.
* **[[The Snowflake]]:** A secondary application used for warehouse layouts, inducing compulsion.
* **[[Black Coral]]:** A tertiary, dangerous fractal pattern derived from Algorithm analysis, inducing violent insanity.
* **[[Fauxflake]]:** [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s modified version for controlled influence.
* **[[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Rhea Agent Signatures]]:** The unfolding hexagonal structures revealed by [[Reapers]], [[Writers]], and [[Watchers]].
* **[[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)|Winston's Memoir Pages]]:** The final pages contain complex hexagonal fractals ([[Hexagon Pages (Winston's Memoirs)]]).
* **Foxglove Dreams:** Visions of hexagonal Dyson swarms, stellar arrays, and network patterns experienced by Algorithm-active Foxgloves.

## Neurological Impact
* All hexagonal basilisk variants exploit vulnerabilities specifically engineered by [[Rhea]] into human cognition, linked to processing [[The Foxglove Algorithm]].
* Different configurations trigger different pathological neurological responses (amnesia, compulsion, insanity, influence).
* Exposure generally pushes cognitive processing towards the systematic/predictable pole ([[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)]]).
