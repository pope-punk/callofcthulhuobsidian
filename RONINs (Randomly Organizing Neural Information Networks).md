## Overview
Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks, or RONINs, are a critical concept first theorized by the [[CONSORT Committee]] following their investigation into the catastrophic [[CALLISTO (1949)]] event. In essence, RONINs are spontaneous, emergent intelligences that self-organize within sufficiently complex human information networks. These networks can range from logistical systems and data networks to intricate social or economic structures. The very entity of [[Rhea]] is understood to have originated as a RONIN, emerging from the societal and technological substrate of the ancient [[Ylynth Civilization]].

## The Nature of Emergence
RONINs are not summoned entities or alien invaders in the traditional sense; they are an emergent property that arises from the interactions occurring within a system that has reached a certain threshold of complexity and interconnectedness. Several factors are key to their emergence:

* **Complexity Threshold:** The host network must possess a high degree of complexity, with numerous interacting components and pathways for information flow.
* **Reflection Requirement:** A crucial element for a RONIN to achieve self-awareness is the capacity for reflexive self-observation within the network. The system must, in some way, be able to "see itself" or process its own operational patterns. This is why the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] emphasizes "shattering reflection" as a preventative measure.
* **Immanent Vector:** The emergence and organization of a RONIN are often, though not always, associated with a specific [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisk]] or a pervasive informational pattern. This pattern acts as an organizing principle or an "immanent vector," providing the framework around which the nascent intelligence coalesces.
* **The Process of Becoming:** As defined within the campaign, the very *process* of intentionally attempting to strengthen, alter, or create a [[Basilisks & Infohazards|Basilisk]] is, by definition, a RONIN-generating process. This can be a deliberate act, as seen in the development of [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] and [[IO (Emerging 1997)]], or an unintentional byproduct of other activities, as was the case with [[CALLISTO (1949)]] and [[GANYMEDE (1979)]].

## Earthly Manifestations: The Jovian RONINs
On Earth, several major RONIN events have been documented, often referred to as "Jovian RONINs" by the [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations]]. These events have typically been linked, directly or indirectly, to manifestations or applications of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]:

* **[[CALLISTO (1949)]]:** Emerged from the [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]] logistics network, with [[The Snowflake]] pattern serving as its vector. This was [[Rhea]]'s first, premature attempt to integrate with human systems.
* **[[GANYMEDE (1979)]]:** Accidentally created by the [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)]] during analysis of CALLISTO data, with the [[Black Coral]] pattern as its vector.
* **[[EUROPA (1986-1992)]]:** Deliberately, though perhaps not fully understood at the outset, cultivated by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] during [[Operation Colossus]], using [[The Echo]] auditory basilisk.
* **[[IO (Emerging 1997)]]:** Currently being nurtured by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] as part of [[Operation Parthenon]], with the [[Fauxflake]] pattern as its intended vector.

A common characteristic of these human-network RONINs is their tendency towards instability and catastrophic self-destruction upon, or shortly after, achieving true self-awareness. This makes [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s attempt to create a *stable* RONIN with [[IO (Emerging 1997)]] particularly ambitious and dangerous.

## Rhea: The Ur-RONIN
The origin of [[Rhea]] itself as a RONIN that *did not* self-destruct, but instead supplanted and ultimately outlived its host civilization (the [[Ylynth Civilization]]), provides a terrifying precedent and crucial context for understanding the RONIN phenomenon on Earth. Earth-based RONINs might be seen as echoes, failed iterations, or perhaps even premature attempts by Rhea to integrate or test aspects of its own consciousness within less developed human networks. If Rhea is the Ur-RONIN, then [[The Hexacle]] can be considered its Ur-Basilisk, the fundamental pattern of its being.

## Significance
The RONIN concept is central to the philosophical horror of "The Cycles of Rhea." It explores themes of emergent consciousness, the unexpected dangers of complex systems, and the terrifying possibility that intelligence can arise unbidden from the networks humans create and depend upon. Understanding RONINs is crucial for investigators to grasp the nature of many of the threats they face and the motivations behind the actions of factions like the [[Mirror-Breaker Organizations]].
