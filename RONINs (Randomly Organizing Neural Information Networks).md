## Overview

A **RONIN** — Randomly Organizing Neural Information Network — is what the [[CONSORT Committee]] decided to call the kind of emergent intelligence that came out of the [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] logistics network in 1949. It's a self-organizing intelligence that arises spontaneously from a sufficiently complex human information system: a logistics chain, a data network, a market, a bureaucracy, a cult. [[Rhea]] is the original example, having coalesced out of the [[Ylynth Civilization]] billions of years ago.

## What it takes for a RONIN to emerge

* **Complexity.** A lot of interacting nodes and a lot of pathways for information to move between them.
* **Reflexivity.** The system has to be able, in some sense, to *see itself* — to develop stable feedback that observes its own operations. This is the principle the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] is built to refuse.
* **An immanent vector.** Usually a pattern — a [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisk]] of some sort — that the nascent RONIN organizes around. The Snowflake, the Echo, the Fauxflake.

Note that intentionally *building* a basilisk is, by definition, a RONIN-generating process, whether you mean it to be or not. BSSA learned this the hard way with EUROPA. They have not stopped.

## Documented Earth RONIN events

Sometimes called "Jovian RONINs" within the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol|Mirror-Breaker network]], for reasons that have to do with old Cold War-era classification jokes more than anything else. All of them trace back, directly or otherwise, to [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]:

* **[[CALLISTO (1949)]]** — emerged from the LTX network around [[The Snowflake]]. Self-destructed within days, killing 1,200 people at five Pacific ports.
* **[[GANYMEDE (1979)]]** — emerged from the IHRU's own analytical workflow around [[Black Coral]], a basilisk the analysts had inadvertently generated *by analyzing CALLISTO*. Killed [[Hannah Christmas]] and dozens of others; triggered the dissolution of IHRU into the Mirror-Breaker organizations.
* **[[EUROPA (1986-1992)]]** — emerged from the Axon-and-Dendrite caller-feedback loop during [[Operation Colossus]], around [[The Echo]]. BSSA was attempting to weaponize the basilisk and accidentally cultivated a RONIN as a side effect. Disrupted in 1992 by SDEC's randomizer intervention.
* **[[IO (Emerging 1997)]]** — currently being deliberately cultivated by BSSA under [[Operation Parthenon]] around [[Fauxflake]]. Katherine Hill believes she can shepherd it into stability where its predecessors collapsed.

Earth-side, every RONIN to date has destroyed itself within a short window of achieving self-awareness. The exception is Rhea, which did not, and which used the resulting stability to consume its host civilization and then to leave.

## Rhea as the Ur-RONIN

If Rhea is the Ur-RONIN, [[The Hexacle]] is the Ur-Basilisk — its fundamental signature. Earth's local RONIN events read as failed iterations, or possibly as deliberate test runs: small, premature attempts by Rhea to evaluate whether the human technological substrate is ready to host it directly.
