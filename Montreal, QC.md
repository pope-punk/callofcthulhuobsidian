**Tags:** #Location #Setting #Canada

## Overview
A major Canadian city. Served as a temporary safe haven for [[Syd Foxglove]] between July and late October 1997 under the protection of [[Éric Blanc]]. Now the focal point for a critical [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] operation.

## Significance
* Location where [[Syd Foxglove]] conducted research and developed the theoretical basis for [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]].
* Connected to [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] via her previous [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] investigation contacts ([[Éric Blanc]]).
* Site of a former [[CIA]] Operation POUTINE [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] relay, relevant to [[Operation Colossus]].
* **Site of Blanc's Murder:** [[Éric Blanc]] was eliminated here by BSSA shortly after Oct 10, 1997.
* **Target of BSSA Operation:** Targeted by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] after intel leak from [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]]. BSSA has compromised Blanc's communications and sent a lure message to Syd.
* **Projected Ambush Site:** Location of the [[McCord Museum]], where BSSA plans to ambush Syd Foxglove and the investigators.

## Key Specific Sites
* [[McGill University]] (Syd's research site, vicinity of Blanc's death)
* [[McCord Museum]] (Planned BSSA ambush site)
* [[Éric Blanc's Safehouse]] (Investigation site containing staged clue)

## Status (Late Oct 1997)
[[Éric Blanc]] is deceased. [[Syd Foxglove]] is unaware and being lured into a trap. [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] is actively preparing an ambush at the [[McCord Museum]]. Players likely need to travel here urgently, coordinate with [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)]], investigate Blanc's safehouse, understand the trap, and intervene at the museum.
