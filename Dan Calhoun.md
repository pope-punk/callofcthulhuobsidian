## Description

Dan Calhoun is the owner or operator of Saul’s Supply Station, a hardware store in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. He knew [[Sean Fielding]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* [[Sauls Supply Station]]: Runs this local hardware store.
* **[[Sean Fielding]] Acquaintance**: Knew Sean Fielding for about two years. Provided investigators with information about seeing Sean's car parked outside the store on Saturday, September 27th, 1997 (shortly after Sean's actual death). Mentioned Sean frequented "private" bars.
* **Witness to [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]]**: Provided a description of a "strange jazz woman" (Alice Redmond) who came into his store in early September. He noted her dirty appearance, pine needles in her hair, forced smile, large amount of cash, and her purchases: crazy glue, red spray paint, and a cattle prod. His security footage confirmed Alice's identity.
* **Voicemail**: Left a voicemail on Sean Fielding's answering machine, which players discovered.

## Status

Presumed living and working at Saul's Supply Station as of October 1997. Acted as an early source of information for the player investigators.
