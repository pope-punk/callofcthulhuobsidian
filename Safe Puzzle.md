## Overview
The multi-stage puzzle required to open the safe in the [[Oliver Foxglove House]] and retrieve [[Oliver Foxglove's Advanced Research Notes]].

## Components Required
1.  **Syd's Birthday Date:** Revealed or strongly hinted at in [[Oliver Foxglove Emails]] found at [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]]. Confirmed by [[Syd Foxglove]] after his rescue. (The *Input*).
2.  **[[Ylynthii Numerals]] Rules:** Deciphered from intuitive carvings/notes left by [[Syd Foxglove]] in his BSSA holding cell(s) (likely [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] or [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]). Includes understanding the base system (Base-X) and symbols. (The *Number System*).
3.  **[[Oliver Foxglove's Cipher Notes]]:** Found at [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]]. Details the specific process Oliver used:
    * Convert Base-10 Date (Syd's Birthday) -> Ylynthii Base-X representation.
    * Map Ylynthii digits -> Standard 0-9 Keypad digits using Oliver's specific substitution key. (The *Conversion Process*).

## Solution Process
Players must acquire all three components from different locations and combine them logically:
* Use the Ylynthii rules to convert Syd's birthday into the correct Base-X numerical string.
* Use Oliver's mapping key (from the Cipher Notes) to translate the Ylynthii string into a sequence of standard decimal digits.
* Input the resulting sequence into the safe's keypad lock.

## Significance
* Integrates clues from multiple investigation threads ([[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] sites, [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]]).
* Reinforces the connection between the Foxglove family, [[The Foxglove Algorithm]], and its alien origins ([[Ylynthii Numerals]]).
* Gates access to the crucial [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] synthesis information.
