## Overview
The multi-stage puzzle required to open the safe in the [[Oliver Foxglove House]] and retrieve [[Oliver Foxglove's Advanced Research Notes]] (and the vacuum-sealed reagent stored with them).

## Components Required
1. **Andrea Foxglove's Birthday:** Hinted in [[Oliver Foxglove Emails]] (Oliver tells Syd the code is "your grandmother's birthday in my cipher"). Confirmed by [[Syd Foxglove]] after his rescue. (The *Input*.)
2. **[[Ylynthii Numerals]] Rules:** Deciphered from intuitive carvings/notes left by Syd in his BSSA holding cell(s) at [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] and [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]. Includes the base-6 system and the symbol set. (The *Number System*.)
3. **[[Oliver Foxglove's Cipher Notes]]:** Found at [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]]. Details Oliver's process:
   * Convert Base-10 date (Andrea's birthday) -> Ylynthii base-6 representation.
   * Map Ylynthii digits -> standard 0-9 keypad digits using Oliver's substitution key. (The *Conversion Process*.)

## Solution Process
Players must acquire all three components and combine them:
* Use the Ylynthii rules to convert Andrea Foxglove's birthday into the correct base-6 numerical string.
* Use Oliver's mapping key (from the Cipher Notes) to translate the Ylynthii string into a sequence of standard decimal digits.
* Input the resulting sequence into the safe's keypad lock.

## Significance
* Integrates clues from multiple investigation threads ([[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] sites, [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]]).
* Reinforces the connection between the Foxglove family, [[The Foxglove Algorithm]], and its alien origins.
* Gates access to the notes *and reagent* required for G6 synthesis.
