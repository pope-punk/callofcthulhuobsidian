Sean's house at 55 Rosebush Court: dirt tracked through the rooms, meth, signs of a party, police watching from the curb. Upstairs — red spray-painted honeycomb on the wall, the words Please God, Forgive Me. A figure in the backyard, its face a lattice of cells. Pastor Mike raises his pistol; the figure flashes white. The party flees. Gary Cooper is lost in the confusion. They regroup at Mike's house. [TODO: rewrite as PC-voice scene-by-scene.]

    Sat, Oct 4A return address. Hot Cuppa. The thing in the office.

    Beats: Maxwell and Cody chase Spicecube's return address — 3704 S. Preston Road — and find themselves losing time, surfacing again and again in the Hot Cuppa downstairs. Mike and Sebastian dig at the library: LTX, the Hammerskulls, Sean's associates. They regroup and go back. The office door wears a black, sticky hexagon that empties their heads when they look at it; they go in with eyes shut. Inside: potting soil, boxes, the sound of running water. "Alphonse Spicecube" is a featureless plastic mannequin full of gelatinous tubes. Cody destroys it. Clues recovered. [TODO: rewrite.]

    Tue, Oct 7LTX. Detective Reeves. The mall. Mike falls.

    Beats: LTX offices, posing as job applicants — high turnover, dazed employees shipping dirt, the "short trip long trip" pattern in the invoices. Supervisor Alex Kramer fixated on the dirt. They call him: a police moratorium on missing-persons cases; the Balloon Mask Killer files (Edmund White) pulled; the jazz lady again. The dirt traces to an abandoned wing of the Redwood Mall, mailed by one "Quentin Cinnabon." That evening: the mall. The jazz lady — Alice Redmond — dragging a disabled mannequin through the dark. The confrontation collapses. Alice shoots Pastor Mike and runs. Cody grabs her satchel. Cattle prods, they learn, put the mannequins down. [TODO: rewrite.     Mid-OctoberAlice's house. The vault. The elk in the woods.

    Beats: Austen's results — black slime is organic, the arm is advanced plastic (high fluorine, conductive, capillary action), the worm is mostly sugar with artificial G-C DNA. They meet Detective Reeves and go to Alice's. The house is dusty, uninhabited for months. Shrouded statues in the library — one labeled Woman with Lyre, 1993, Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation. A copy of Borges' Labyrinths stamped IHRU property; a note from "Nine-String"; Mirror-Breaker mentioned again. A metal door. The combination — 0.083. Inside: a map of Sequoia Falls with six numbered locations; surveillance photos of Dylan Hill ("Agent Direction"); cash; the CALLISTO report; Mirror-Breaker notes. Then, in the trees outside, the elk-thing — and Detective Reeves dies on its antlers. The party scatters, Maxwell into the woods. [TODO: rewrite.     Beats: The encampment under I-90 — the Center of the Universe. Deserted, recently. Calvin Pierce's body in an LTX shipping container, two weeks dead, stabbed. A worm bigger than the others. Down the slope: an underpass full of identical rental Hondas, each with a mannequin in the driver's seat. Sean's car among them. Sean's body in the trunk.
