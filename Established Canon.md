## Phase 1: The Missing Brother (October 3rd - 4th, 1997)

* **Oct 3rd (Friday):**
    * Investigators attend a [[Sebastian International]] timeshare seminar at the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]].
    * Met by [[Emily Fielding]], who insists they know something about her missing brother, [[Sean Fielding]], and shows them a letter from "Alphonse Spicecube".
    * Travel to [[Sean Fielding's House]] (55 Rosebush Court).
    * House is a mess: dirt tracked everywhere, signs of a party, meth found. Police are watching the house.
    * Upstairs, investigators find strange red spray-painted hexagon patterns ("honeycomb") and the words "Please God, Forgive Me".
    * Encounter a strange figure ("Mr. Honeycomb" / [[Reapers|Reaper]]) in the backyard with a honeycomb-like face. The figure emits blinding flashes when [[Pastor Mike]] raises a gun.
    * Investigators flee the scene, losing Gary Cooper in the chaos, and retreat to Pastor Mike's house.
* **Oct 4th (Saturday):**
    * Maxwell and Cody investigate the return address on the letter (3704 S. Preston Road), experiencing lost time and ending up repeatedly at the "Hot Cuppa" coffee shop downstairs.
    * Mike and Sebastian research at the library, learning about [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]], the [[Hammerskull Crew]], and Sean's associates.
    * Group reconvenes and decides to investigate 3704 S. Preston Road again.
    * Find the door marked with a black, sticky [[The Hexacle|hexagon pattern]] that causes memory loss when viewed directly. Enter with eyes closed.
    * Inside the abandoned office, find potting soil, boxes, and hear running water.
    * Confront "Alphonse Spicecube," revealed to be a featureless plastic automaton [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]] filled with gelatinous tubes. Cody destroys it. Recover clues.

## Phase 2: Pulling Threads (October 5th - 7th, 1997)

* **Oct 5th (Sunday):**
    * Attempt to visit [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] but are turned away by skinheads (likely [[Hammerskull Crew]]) citing "No Jazz Clothing" rules and the service having already started. Obtain a newspaper article.
    * Follow up on Sean's voicemails, leading to [[Dan Calhoun]] at [[Sauls Supply Station]].
    * Calhoun mentions seeing Sean's car Sept 27th and describes a "strange jazz woman" buying suspicious items (glue, red spray paint, cattle prod). Security footage confirms this woman ([[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]).
    * Investigate addresses from Spicecube's packages (Sean Fielding, Thomas Reeves, Sarah Prescott), finding they are also missing and noting police surveillance. Connections emerge: LTX keycards, hexagons, stolen cars.
* **Oct 6th (Monday):**
    * Deliver samples (slime, arm, worm) from the Spicecube automaton to Professor [[Prof. Alexander Austen]] at the community college for analysis. Results expected later.
    * Contact [[Mandy Archer]] (Sean's fling) via her controlling father, setting up a meeting.
    * Packages of dirt start arriving at investigators' home addresses (traced to Sebastian International).
* **Oct 7th (Tuesday):**
    * Investigate [[LTX (Land Transit Express)]] offices under the guise of job interviews. Find high turnover, confused employees shipping dirt, patterns in invoices ("short trip long trip"), but little concrete information. Note Supervisor [[Alex Kramer]]'s obsession with the dirt. [[Pastor Mike]] faxes documents home.
    * Discover Detective [[Colin Reeves]] has also been investigating LTX (searching for his brother Thomas) and left a business card.
    * Call Reeves, learn about a police moratorium on missing persons cases and the files on the Balloon Mask Killer ([[Edmund White]]) being pulled. This links back to Agent [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]], the "jazz lady".
    * Trace dirt packages back to an abandoned section of the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]], mailed by "Quentin Cinnabon".
    * **(Evening - Mall Incident):** Go to the mall to confront Cinnabon. Find [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] dragging a disabled mannequin ([[Signal Dummies|Dummy]]) in a dark section. Confrontation escalates. Alice shoots and kills [[Pastor Mike]]. Alice flees, dropping her satchel. Cody grabs the bag. Investigators realize cattle prods disable the dummies.

## Phase 3: Alice's Trail & The Jazz Club (Post-Oct 7th - Mid-October)

* **Following Day:**
    * Agents Thistle and Thorn ([[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)|UMSE]]) visit Pastor Mike's house, mentioning a cleanup operation by the "jazz club" ([[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]]) for their rogue agent (Alice). Provide a cactus with a note mentioning [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] and the [[Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts]].
    * Research Alice Redmond, find her address (1612 Ashborough Way).
* **Subsequent Days:**
    * Receive analysis results from Professor [[Prof. Alexander Austen]]: Black slime is organic (plant/fungus); arm is advanced plastic (high fluorine, conductive, capillary action); worm is mostly sugar with artificial G-C DNA.
    * Meet with Detective [[Colin Reeves]], plan to investigate Alice's house.
    * **(Alice's House):** House is dusty, uninhabited for months. Find shrouded statues in library (one identified as "Woman with Lyre, 1993, Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation"). Find a copy of Borges' *Labyrinths* marked as [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]] property, note from "Nine-String" [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]], mention of [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]. Discover a vault behind a metal door.
    * Solve combination lock (0.083). Vault contains: map of Sequoia Falls with 6 numbered locations ([[Alice's Map]]), surveillance notes on [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]] (Agent Direction), cash, [[CALLISTO Report]], [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol Notes]].
    * **(Reaper Attack):** While investigating, a [[Reapers|Reaper]] (described as an elk monster) attacks, killing Detective [[Colin Reeves]]. Investigators escape, some heading to the car, Maxwell into the woods.
* **Rendezvous & Jazz Club:**
    * Group reunites. Possess items from Alice's satchel: Keys belonging to Alice, Sean, Thomas Reeves, Sarah Prescott [[Keys (Alice's Satchel)]], and her journal ([[Alice's Journal (Satchel)]]) showing crossed-out names and notes. Also have a saxophone key and [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]] syllabus mentioning [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONINs]], [[Black Coral]], etc..
    * Use code (13762) to enter [[BNJC HQ]] (Blue Note Jazz Collective HQ).
    * Undergo cryptic assessment by [[Travis Ward (Agent Tremolo)]] and [[Leah Burns (Agent Legato)]] involving tarot-like cards and improvisation.
    * Share information, learn about [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]]: Mission to investigate [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONINs]] (like [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]]) after [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] destruction; factional split (Nine-String vs. Caraway); [[Syd Foxglove]]'s potential connection to Alice; Agent Direction's disappearance; distrust of "sculpture people" ([[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]).
    * Gain access to BNJC library, read [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]]/[[Mirror-Breaker Organizations|Mirror-Breaker]] documents (Operational Guidelines, RONIN/Security). Cody Baker gains Uncanny Intuition but loses SAN.

## Phase 4: Mapping the Conspiracy & COTU (Mid-Late October)

* **Surveillance:** Spot someone watching Pastor Mike's house wearing a porcelain mask and leather smock [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)?]], find a tracking device on car.
* **Map Investigation:** Correlate locations on [[Alice's Map]] with known sites:
    * 1: [[White Stag Bar]] (Hammerskull HQ).
    * 2: [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]].
    * 3: Highway I-90 (matches photo of [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]]). Black 'X' marked nearby in woods.
    * 4: Residential area near Alice's house.
    * 5: "The Conifers" housing project (many homeless).
    * 6: Recognized building, identified as [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] (homeless shelter mentioned in church article). Unknown photo #2 shows sculptures matching those at Alice's house ([[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]).
* **Investigating Leads:**
    * Briefly investigate [[White Stag Bar]], note computer in back room.
    * Investigate Location 'X' (woods near I-90). Encounter strange deer-like creatures [[Reapers?]] and worm-like entities [[Spectator Grubs]] that cause lost time when shot. Find signs leading towards highway underpass.
* **COTU Discovery (Oct 10th):**
    * Discover the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] homeless encampment under I-90. Find it strangely deserted despite signs of habitation.
    * Find the body of [[Calvin Pierce]] (Sean's lover) in an LTX shipping container, dead ~2 weeks, stabbed. Find paper dated Jan 5, 1950 inside.
    * Explore bizarre structure made of tarps/blankets. Find statues made of canned food, religious iconography, black paint graffiti ("God"), and several prostrated bodies (dead addicts, apparent starvation/F7 overdose). One woman barely alive. Spot large worm [[Spectator Grubs]].
    * Find underpass filled with identical cars (rental Hondas) containing [[Signal Dummies|mannequins]], plus [[Sean Fielding]]'s car. Discover Sean's body in his trunk.
    * Locate Alice's apparent hideout room within COTU complex: cot, desk, papers, prescription pill bottles ([[Adderall]]), note with phone number **514-874-2313** ([[Montreal Phone Number]]).
    * Confrontation with reaper wolves occurs.
