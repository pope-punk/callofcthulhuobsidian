## Description

This location is the personal residence and former safehouse of [[Éric Blanc]], located at an undisclosed address in [[Montreal, QC]]. It is now a key investigation site following Blanc's death.

## Known Information & Connections

* **Occupant**: Formerly [[Éric Blanc]] (deceased).
* **Function as Safe Haven**: Served as a sanctuary for [[Syd Foxglove]] from July 17 until shortly after October 10, 1997. [[Éric Blanc]] provided Syd with support and access to [[McGill University]].
* **Resources**: Contains valuable items from Blanc's former career, potentially including:
    * Technical documentation on transmission systems ([[Operation POUTINE]]).
    * Blueprints for the [[McCord Museum]].
    * Communication equipment (now likely irrelevant or monitored).
    * Files/intel related to Blanc's investigations (EUROPA, SCAI, Maria Chen).

## Current Status & BSSA Staging (Late Oct 1997)

* **Blanc Deceased:** [[Éric Blanc]] was killed by BSSA shortly after Oct 10th.
* **Site Untouched but Staged:** BSSA operatives, after eliminating Blanc and compromising his email, deliberately left the safehouse physically untouched to avoid alerting Syd prematurely. However, they staged a critical clue intended for investigators:
    * **The Computer:** Blanc's computer will be found powered on, logged into his PGP-encrypted email client.
    * **The Lure Email:** The 'Sent Items' folder will clearly show the recently sent, coded lure message addressed to [[Syd Foxglove]], directing him to the [[McCord Museum]].
* **BSSA's Intent:** This staging is a calculated act of arrogance by BSSA/[[Katherine Hill]], designed to ensure investigators find the lure message, understand a trap is set, and walk into the [[McCord Museum]] ambush alongside Syd.
* **Investigative Value**: Discovering the untouched state combined with the highly suspicious powered-on computer and sent lure message should make the nature of the trap clear to the players. The blueprints and intel files remain valuable resources for planning their intervention. Coordination with [[UMSE (Upper Michigan Succulent Enthusiasts)]] may help interpret findings or assess risk.
