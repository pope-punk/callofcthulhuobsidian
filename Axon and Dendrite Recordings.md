## Overview
Refers to the avant-garde radio show "Axon and Dendrite," hosted by [[David Brewster]] and [[Maria Chen]] (late 1991 - mid-1992) as part of [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s [[Operation Colossus]]. The show embedded the [[The Echo]] basilisk and contributed to the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN emergence. Finding materials related to its final broadcast is key to a puzzle.

## Content of Broadcasts
* Featured experimental audio, strange interviews, discussions of cryptids and weird phenomena.
* Embedded within the audio were modulated tones comprising [[The Echo]] basilisk. Listening could cause unease or hallucinations.
* Broadcast structure typically involved **six distinct segments**.

## Significance & Puzzle Relevance
* Direct evidence of [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s [[Operation Colossus]] methods.
* Contains the [[The Echo]] basilisk itself.
* [[Amanda Green]] possesses memorabilia (posters, potentially fan recordings lent to a friend).
* **Brewster's Final Episode:** The crucial puzzle element isn't necessarily a full recording, but the **[[Brewster's Final Segment Sequence Log]]** found at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]], detailing the specific order of the six segments in Brewster's final, unaired "testament" episode. This sequence is needed as the input for the [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]].

## Location & Status (Oct 1997)
* Original broadcast tapes likely destroyed by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].
* Fan recordings may exist (via [[Amanda Green]]'s friend).
* The **Segment Sequence Log** for the final episode is located at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]].
