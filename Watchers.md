## Overview
A type of [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Editor]] serving [[Rhea]]'s long-term goals through **biological surveillance and data collection**. They observe and record genetic information from Earth's organisms.

## Appearance & Capabilities
* **Camouflage:** Can take various organic forms based on earthly or Ylynthii fauna.
* **Primary Function:** Monitor organisms, track genetic drift, and record significant biological data using **[[Spectator Grubs]]**.
* **Grub Storage/Deployment:** Possess hexagonal holes within their bodies where they store dormant [[Spectator Grubs]]. Can activate and deploy these Grubs onto targets or into the environment. Can also directly deposit biological Grubs into [[Signal Dummies]] to override synthetic Grub instructions.
* **Communication:** Capable of direct communication with humans (as planned for the endgame confrontation). Possess vast knowledge of [[Rhea]]'s history, goals, and philosophy.
* **Defenses:** Likely possess defensive capabilities, potentially related to manipulating local flora/fauna, deploying specialized Grubs, or emitting perceptual distortions.

## Known Actions
* Long-term monitoring of Earth's biosphere.
* Inject biological [[Spectator Grubs|Grubs]] into [[Rhea]]'s technological network via [[Signal Dummies]].
* Planned endgame confrontation: A Watcher is expected to intercept the players en route to [[Dead Horse Quarry]] to deliver [[Rhea]]'s ultimatum regarding [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Active but likely operating discreetly in the background, focused on data gathering. Represent a source of deep lore and potentially immense danger if confronted directly. The primary planned interaction is the endgame dialogue/confrontation.
