## Description

Maria Chen was one of the unwitting hosts of the "Axon and Dendrite" radio show during [[Operation Colossus]] (1987-1992). She survived the operation's termination.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **"Axon and Dendrite":** Co-hosted the show with [[David Brewster]], funded via the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] (a BSSA front). She was primarily a visual artist who also served as a host.
* **SDEC Contact & Disruption:** In mid-1992, [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] contacted Brewster and Chen, providing them with a randomizer tool and [[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules|mapping rules]] to disrupt the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN by altering broadcast structures.
* **Survival & Relocation:** Survived Brewster's execution by chance and fled Ellensburg after Operation Colossus collapsed. Relocated to Portland, OR, living under an assumed name, deeply traumatized and fearing [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].
* **Contacted by [[Éric Blanc]] (Early 1997):** Blanc located her in Portland via SCAI financial records. Due to her fear, she swore Blanc to secrecy regarding her location. As part of that reciprocal arrangement, **she retains Blanc's home address in Montreal** as her only contact route to him in an emergency.
* **Lockbox Contents:** Her current contact information is the primary content hidden within [[David Brewster]]'s lockbox, secured via the [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]]. Finding her is the *only* viable route to Blanc's apartment address — Nine-String and BNJC never had it.

## Role for the Montreal Arc

After the players solve [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]] (or follow the parallel Amanda Green → Seattle friend chain), they reach Chen in Portland. If trust is established (e.g., by demonstrating SDEC-side knowledge and disavowing BSSA), Chen will reluctantly:

* Confirm Blanc's existence and his role as Alice's longtime contact.
* Confirm that Blanc was the person who located her in early 1997 and that they have a strict no-contact protocol.
* Provide **Blanc's Montreal apartment address** — which she has been holding since 1997 and which is not in BNJC's possession.
* Warn the players that if Blanc has gone silent, something is badly wrong.

She will not travel with the investigators and is essentially a one-shot information node.

## Status

Living in hiding in Portland as of Late 1997. The structural gate between the Ellensburg arc and the Montreal arc.
