## Description

Maria Chen was one of the unwitting hosts of the "Axon and Dendrite" radio show during [[Operation Colossus]] (1987-1992). She survived the operation's termination.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **"Axon and Dendrite"**: Co-hosted the show with [[David Brewster]], funded via the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] (a BSSA front). She was primarily a visual artist, but also served as a host.
* **SDEC Contact & Disruption:** In mid-1992, [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] contacted Brewster and Chen. SDEC provided them with a **randomizer tool** and **mapping rules** ([[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]]) to disrupt the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN by altering broadcast structures.
* **Survival & Relocation**: Survived Brewster's execution by chance and fled Ellensburg after [[Operation Colossus]] collapsed in 1992. Relocated to Portland, OR, living under an assumed name, deeply traumatized and fearing [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]].
* **Contacted by [[Éric Blanc]] (Early 1997)**: Blanc located her in Portland. Due to her fear, she swore Blanc to secrecy regarding her location.
* **Lockbox Contents:** Her current contact information is the primary content hidden within [[David Brewster]]'s lockbox, secured via the [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]]. Finding her is crucial for investigating [[Operation Colossus]] further.
* **Potential Player Contact**: Players might locate her after solving the lockbox puzzle. She could reluctantly share information about "Axon and Dendrite," [[The Echo]], [[Operation Colossus]], [[David Brewster]]'s fate, and potentially confirm [[Éric Blanc]]'s involvement if trust is established. They might also contact her via Amanda's Friend.

## Status

Presumed living in hiding in Portland as of Late 1997. Key potential source of historical information about BSSA's past operations.
