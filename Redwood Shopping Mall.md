## Overview
A shopping mall in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. Contains the office for [[Sebastian International]] and was the site of a key early encounter.

## Key Areas
* **Sebastian International Office:** Hosted timeshare seminar on Oct 3, 1997, where [[Emily Fielding]] recruited the player characters.
* **Abandoned Wing / Quentin Cinnabon Site:** Location where players tracked a [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]] recipient name ("Quentin Cinnabon").

## Key Events
* **Timeshare Seminar (Oct 3, 1997):** Starting point of the campaign investigation.
* **Encounter with Alice Redmond (Oct 4, 1997):** Players confronted [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] in the abandoned wing. She was dragging a disabled [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]]. The confrontation resulted in [[Pastor Mike]]'s death. Alice dropped her satchel containing keys and journal during her escape.

## Significance
* Location where the player group initially formed and took on the investigation.
* Site of the first direct (violent) encounter with [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]], revealing her unstable state and connection to [[Signal Dummies]]. Provided players with crucial early items ([[Keys (Alice's Satchel)]], [[Alice's Journal (Satchel)]]).

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Site of [[Pastor Mike]]'s death, likely investigated by police. [[Sebastian International]] office may or may not still be operating.
