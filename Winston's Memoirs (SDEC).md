## Overview
The personal memoirs of [[Winston Foxglove]], written sometime before his death in 1981. Contains the only known direct account of his 1922 encounter with [[Rhea]].

## Location & Status
* **Location:** Stored within the sealed [[SDEC Facility]] in San Diego. Transferred there sometime after [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]]'s dissolution in 1979.
* **Status:** Inaccessible until the [[SDEC Facility]] is unsealed. Protected by the facility's "Ant Trap" protocol and potentially the [[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)]].

## Content & Significance
* **Primary Content:** Details [[Winston Foxglove]]'s life, likely including his experiences at [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]], [[CALLISTO (1949)]], and founding [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]].
* **Crucial Revelation:** Contains his account of the 1922 encounter near [[Dead Horse Quarry]] where he received [[Royal Jelly]] from a [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Rhea Editor]], unlocking his access to [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]. This directly links the family's abilities to [[Rhea]].
* **[[Hexagon Pages (Winston's Memoirs)]]:** The end of the memoirs contains several pages filled with complex, fractal [[The Hexacle|Hexagon]] patterns. These are initially indecipherable but can potentially be decoded by someone using [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]], revealing the full truth and exact location of the [[Dead Horse Quarry]] Hive entrance.

## Role in Campaign
* Represents the "Rosetta Stone" for understanding the origin of the Foxglove Algorithm access and [[Rhea]]'s direct manipulation.
* Finding and decoding the memoirs is likely a key step in the campaign's penultimate phase, leading directly to the final confrontation location.
* [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]]'s possession and analysis of these memoirs (linking the quarry anomaly to Winston) is what triggered [[Rhea]] to destroy the facility in 1992.
