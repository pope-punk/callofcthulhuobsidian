## Overview
A [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol|Mirror-Breaker Organization]], now defunct and sealed. Formerly operated under the cover of an amateur entomology club based near San Diego, CA. Focused on detection and analysis of [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONINs]].

## Methodology & Philosophy
* Utilized **Insect behavior metaphors** and entomological observation codes for communication and operations, adhering to the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]]. ("Can the ant see the colony?")
* Focused on detection and analysis of [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)]] and related phenomena.

## Leadership & Personnel
* **Known Former Agents:**
    * Agent Diaphanous (Detected [[Dead Horse Quarry]] anomaly, presumed deceased)
    * Agent Chrysalis (Survived 1992 destruction, joined [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]])
    * Agent Centipede (Cynthia McCarthy), best friend and former romantic interest of [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)]].

## Operations & Technology
* **Technology:** Utilized "CICADA-class sonar/radar" for detection.
* **EUROPA Intervention (Mid 1992):** Detected the emerging [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN near [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] and correctly identified its link to [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s [[The Echo]] basilisk.
    * **Provided Randomizer Tool:** To disrupt EUROPA, SDEC provided "Axon and Dendrite" hosts [[David Brewster]] and [[Maria Chen]] with a **randomizer tool** and associated **mapping rules** ([[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]]). This tool allowed the hosts to input a **four-digit code** which would then dictate the precise (randomized) order of the show's six segments, breaking the predictable patterns EUROPA relied upon.
* **Key Discovery:** Detected a spatial anomaly near [[Dead Horse Quarry]] using CICADA sonar.
* **Fatal Connection:** Linked the Quarry anomaly to [[Winston Foxglove]]'s memoirs (stored at SDEC), getting too close to [[Rhea]]'s primary Hive entrance.

## History & Status
* Formed post-[[GANYMEDE (1979)]]. Successfully intervened to disrupt [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] in mid-1992 using the randomizer tool.
* **Destruction (Late 1992):** Facility infiltrated and personnel eliminated by a specialized [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Rhea agent]] ([[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)]]). Destruction was a direct response to SDEC detecting the Hive. [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] incorrectly attributed the destruction to EUROPA.
* **Current Status:** Facility is sealed under the "Ant Trap" containment protocol. Contains [[Winston Foxglove]]'s crucial personal memoirs and potentially other valuable research/data. The randomizer tool rules left at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] are a key legacy.

## Containment Protocol
* "The Ant Trap" (Activated 1992 upon facility destruction).
