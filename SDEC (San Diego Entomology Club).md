## Overview

The **San Diego Entomology Club** was one of the seven [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol|Mirror-Breaker cells]] spun out of IHRU after [[GANYMEDE (1979)|GANYMEDE]]. Cover identity: an amateur entomology society. Operational cipher: insect-behavior metaphors. ("Can the ant see the colony?") Focus: RONIN detection and analysis, especially via instrumental signal work.

SDEC produced two of the most important pieces of intelligence the Mirror-Breaker network ever generated — the randomizer tool that disrupted EUROPA in 1992, and the CICADA-class sonar reading that detected the [[Dead Horse Quarry]] anomaly. The second discovery is what got them killed.

The physical facility is the [[SDEC Facility]] in San Diego, sealed since 1992.

## Known agents

* **Agent Diaphanous** ([[Agent Diaphanous (Diane Collins)|Diane Collins]]) — operated the CICADA sonar. Detected the Quarry anomaly. Presumed dead in the 1992 purge.
* **Agent Chrysalis** ([[Agent Chrysalis|Christopher Gruber]]) — field observer. Sole surviving field agent. Joined BNJC after the purge.
* **Agent Centipede** (Cynthia McCarthy) — Jacqui Lopez's best friend and former romantic partner.

## Operations

* **CICADA-class sonar/radar.** SDEC's signature technology. Used to detect anomalous signal and spatial structures. Produced both the Dead Horse Quarry detection and (in 1994-96, post-destruction, through Chrysalis) the 3D tunnel mapping of PCBOT HQ that Alice and Blanc later turned into the encrypted bypass methodology.
* **EUROPA disruption (mid-1992).** SDEC detected the emerging [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]] RONIN, identified its dependence on The Echo broadcasts, and gave the "Axon and Dendrite" hosts a **randomizer tool** plus its [[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules|mapping rules]]. A four-digit code dictated the permutation of the show's six broadcast segments, breaking the predictability EUROPA needed. The intervention worked.
* **The Quarry detection.** While operating CICADA sonar in the region, Diaphanous picked up a spatial anomaly under [[Dead Horse Quarry]]. SDEC connected it to the Hexagon Pages in [[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)|Winston Foxglove's memoirs]] (which SDEC had inherited from IHRU). The connection — that Winston had had a *physical* encounter with whatever was making the anomaly — was correct. It was also fatal.

## The 1992 purge

Rhea dispatched the [[SDEC Infiltrator (Specialized Agent)]] (almost certainly a highly evolved Writer) to clear the building. Personnel were eliminated. The facility's "Ant Trap" containment protocol was triggered to seal whatever survived behind automated defenses.

BSSA — wrongly, but consistently — attributes the destruction to EUROPA. Katherine Hill uses that misattribution as the foundation of her case that the Mirror-Breaker Protocol failed and BSSA's interventionist approach is justified. The Protocol did not fail at SDEC; Rhea did the killing, and no one knows Rhea exists.

## Legacy

* The randomizer-tool mapping rules were left at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] and survive as the basis for the [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]].
* Winston's memoirs and Hexagon Pages remain in the facility.
* Chrysalis carries the institutional memory of the cell. He is, as of late October 1997, off-grid.

## Containment

"The Ant Trap" — activated late 1992. Automated facility-wide lockdown. See [[SDEC Facility]] for breach options.
