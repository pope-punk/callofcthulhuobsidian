## Overview
A series of final, panicked email communications sent by [[Oliver Foxglove]] to [[Syd Foxglove]] in late December 1996 / early January 1997, shortly before Oliver's murder. Archived in Syd's account and recoverable by accessing his email at the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]].

## Content & Significance

These emails contain Oliver's fragmented but desperate attempt to warn Syd about his discoveries.

1. **Algorithm/Rhea Horror:** Oliver realizes the true nature of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] and its connection to an external intelligence ([[Rhea]]). He expresses fear that the [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]]-[[ErgoMatic Manufacturing|ErgoMatic]] merger is the substrate Rhea has been engineering through his work. The trigger of this realization, as Oliver explains it, was noticing that Syd had been doodling in symbols (the [[Ylynthii Numerals]]) Oliver had only ever seen in his own and his father's private notation. From there he traced his recent ErgoMatic projects — the [[GenAssist Universal Tissue Printer]], the [[DNA-Machine Code Conversion System]] — and discovered they were spontaneously replicating the structural backbone of Winston's algorithm.
2. **SDEC / Memoirs:** He mentions trying to reach "the bug people" (SDEC) because his father's surviving research was archived with them after Winston's death, and getting no reply. He urges Syd, if anything happens, to find Winston's memoirs at the [[SDEC Facility]] — Oliver believes those memoirs explain everything.
3. **Home Safe — Research and Reagent:** Oliver states clearly that he has pulled his most sensitive personal research off ErgoMatic's servers and secured it in the family safe. Stored *with* those notes is a small **vacuum-sealed packet of a gene-altering reagent** — the only such material outside ErgoMatic's controlled stock, and the essential precursor for any non-gallium F7 antidote.
4. **Safe Code Hint:** Strongly hints that the code is based on **Oliver's mother's birthday** (i.e., **Andrea Foxglove's** birthday), encoded using Oliver's personal cipher — which Syd, by virtue of his innate fluency, will recognize as a Ylynthii-base process.

## Role in Campaign
* Provides direct confirmation of [[Rhea]]'s threat from Oliver's perspective.
* Points to the **[[SDEC Facility]]** as the location of crucial lore ([[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]]).
* Provides the **location** (home safe), the **key date** (Andrea's birthday), and the **cipher hint** needed for the [[Safe Puzzle]], which unlocks both the research and the reagent required for synthesizing [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Digital records accessible via Syd's account on the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] network. Finding and understanding these emails is a critical step in Act II.
