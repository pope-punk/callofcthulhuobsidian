## Overview
A series of final, panicked email communications sent by [[Oliver Foxglove]] to [[Syd Foxglove]] in late December 1996 / early January 1997, shortly before Oliver's murder. Found by accessing Syd's account at the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]].

## Content & Significance
These emails contain Oliver's fragmented but desperate attempt to warn Syd about his horrifying discoveries:

1.  **Algorithm/Rhea Horror:** Convey Oliver's realization of the true nature of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] and its connection to an external intelligence ([[Rhea]]). Expresses deep fear about the [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]]-[[ErgoMatic Manufacturing|ErgoMatic]] merger enabling Rhea's plan.
2.  **SDEC / Memoirs Importance:** Explicitly mentions the critical importance of [[Winston Foxglove]]'s personal memoirs ([[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]]) held by the sealed **[[SDEC Facility]]**. Implies these memoirs contain essential truths or context about the Algorithm's origin.
3.  **Home Safe & Research:** States clearly that Oliver has secured his **most sensitive personal research** (related to his bio-integration / DNA-conversion work) in his **home safe** for security against corporate/[[Rhea]] interference.
4.  **Safe Code Hint:** Strongly hints that the **code for the safe is based on [[Syd Foxglove]]'s birthday**.

## Role in Campaign
* Provides direct confirmation of [[Rhea]]'s threat from Oliver's perspective.
* Points directly towards the **[[SDEC Facility]]** as the location of crucial lore ([[Winston's Memoirs (SDEC)]]).
* Provides the **location** (home safe) and the **key date** (Syd's birthday) needed for the [[Safe Puzzle]], which unlocks the research required for synthesizing [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Digital records accessible via [[Syd Foxglove]]'s account on the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] network. Finding and understanding these emails is a critical step in Act II.
