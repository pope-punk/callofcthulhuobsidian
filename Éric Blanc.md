## Professional History

Éric Blanc was the CSIS/SCRS technical contact for the CIA's [[Operation POUTINE]], which used the Trois-Rivieres station as its primary location (as it was targeting Canadian Maoists in Quebec City specifically). Shortly before Operation POUTINE concluded in 1986, he resigned over a conflict [...] and became a private investigator for a litigation firm on retainer for a French-Canadian commercial insurance company based in [[Montreal, QC]].

After being contacted by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] in 1993 regarding [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]], he traveled to Sequoia Falls to assist in technical forensics, helping dissect radio machinery to try to find the source of the [[The Echo]] which was known by both to have appeared in Axon and Dendrite. Finding the technical instrumentation at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] already heavily compromised by an apparent hasty clean-up operation, the two traveled to the remaining POUTINE stations throughout the US and Canada together to investigate them for signs of activity or re-activation during EUROPA.

Over the course of their investigation, the two interviewed a number of persons in the vicinity of Ogunquit, Burlington, Trois-Rivieres, Juneau and Duluth, finding that, inexplicably, Juneau, Alaska had also experienced Axon and Dendrite broadcasts from its numbers station. Eric assisted in using anecdotal information about these A&D broadcasts (specifically their signal radius) to reconstruct the signal's propagation mode, as it was not immediately clear how Axon and Dendrite was broadcast live in both locations simultaneously.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **Intelligence Background**: Possessed resources, contacts, and knowledge from his former intelligence career, including technical documentation on transmission systems (relevant to [[Operation POUTINE]]) and potentially blueprints for sensitive locations like the [[McCord Museum]].
* **[[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] Contact**: Connection established through contacts Alice made during her [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] investigation (likely identifying the Montreal POUTINE relay). Mentioned by [[Maria Chen]] as a "French Canadian intelligence officer" she met in 1994. His existence and connection to Alice are known only to [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]] within BNJC.
* **EUROPA Investigation (1993-94)**: Assisted Alice Redmond in technical forensics related to "Axon and Dendrite" and the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. Their formal collaboration was 'off the books,' and records were later removed from [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)]] archives to protect Blanc's ability to investigate discreetly after the 1994 [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]] coup. Interviewed [[Chrissy Perkins]] at the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] but found no initial leads due to purged records by Agent Plaster ([[Phoebe Jones (Agent Plaster)|Phoebe Jones]]). Blanc continued investigating the relay station network unofficially.
* **Located [[Maria Chen]] (Early 1997)**: Methodically tracked Maria Chen to Portland via SCAI financial records and equipment purchase orders, keeping her location secret at her request.
* **[[Syd Foxglove]]'s Protector (July - Oct 1997)**: Provided sanctuary, support, and arranged access to [[McGill University]] chemistry facilities for [[Syd Foxglove]] from July 17, 1997 onwards, while Syd worked on [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]].

## Elimination by BSSA (Post-October 10, 1997)

Following the intelligence leak of the [[Montreal Phone Number]] from [[BNJC HQ]] by [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)]], [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] operatives, acting under [[Katherine Hill]]'s direction, moved decisively in Montreal.
* **Location & Method:** Blanc was located and neutralized by BSSA operatives, likely near [[McGill University]] where he was supporting Syd. BSSA subjected him to torture to gain access to his PGP-encrypted email account and communication keys before disposing of his body.
* **Objective:** Silencing Syd's protector and compromising his secure communication channel were key BSSA objectives.

## Status

**Deceased.** Killed by BSSA shortly after October 10th, 1997. His PGP-compromised email account is now being used by BSSA to lure Syd (and by extension, the investigators) into a trap at the [[McCord Museum]]. His safehouse remains untouched but staged by BSSA.
