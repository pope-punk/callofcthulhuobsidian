## Overview
A dangerous visual [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisk]] accidentally created by [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]] researchers during the analysis of [[CALLISTO (1949)]] data. It was the immanent vector for the [[GANYMEDE (1979)]] RONIN.

## Appearance
* A complex **visual fractal pattern**. Specific appearance details limited, but likely intricate and possibly self-similar.

## Mechanism & Effect
* **Targets Pattern Recognition:** Exploits cognitive vulnerabilities, likely related to [[The Foxglove Algorithm]].
* **Induces Violent Insanity:** Causes acute, severe psychosis and violent behavior.
* **Compels Reproduction:** Critically, exposure compels the victim to reproduce the pattern (drawing, etc.), enabling rapid propagation.

## History & Significance
* **Creation (July 1979):** Emerged accidentally from mathematical tools used by [[IHRU (InfoHazard Response Unit)|IHRU]] to analyze [[The Snowflake]]/CALLISTO data.
* **GANYMEDE Event:** Triggered the [[GANYMEDE (1979)]] RONIN. Spread rapidly within IHRU, leading to mass casualties ([[Hannah Christmas]]) and facility destruction.
* **[[Katherine Hill]] Interaction:** Katherine Hill, possessing latent [[Seer Configuration]] potential, was exposed to a reproduction during GANYMEDE. This exposure acted as her **traumatic activator**. Her Seer Line cognitive disruption resulted in **immunity** to Black Coral's standard pathological effects but **amplified her underlying psychosis**, profoundly shaping her worldview and methods.
* **BSSA Use:** [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] possesses samples/reproductions and uses it in [[Project Ozymandias]] at [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] to test [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]].

## Status (Late Oct 1997)
* Original outbreak contained. [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] retains copies for testing. Remains a dangerous infohazard. Key element in Katherine Hill's backstory and activation.
