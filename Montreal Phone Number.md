## Description

A crucial clue discovered by the investigators, consisting of the phone number **514-874-2313**.

## Discovery

* **Location**: Found written on a note or other item within the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] encampment.
* **Timing**: Discovered by the player investigators around October 10th, 1997, amidst the evidence left after the [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] incident and the discovery of [[Sean Fielding]]'s and [[Calvin Pierce]]'s bodies. Likely left behind by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] during her time based there.

## Significance

* **Connection to Syd Foxglove**: This number served as the primary contact for [[Syd Foxglove]] while he was in hiding in [[Montreal, QC]], likely connecting to his protector, [[Éric Blanc]].
* **Investigative Lead**: Represented a critical pathway for the investigators to locate Syd and potentially gain access to [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]].

## Compromise & Current Status

* **Intelligence Leak**: Almost immediately after the players discovered the number and reported it during a debriefing at the [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]], the information was overheard by [[Steven Rober (Agent Shutter)|Agent Shutter]], a mole working for [[NOPG (New Orleans Photography Group)|NOPG]]/[[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]].
* **Syd's Recapture**: Shutter relayed the number to his handlers, allowing BSSA to pinpoint Syd's location and recapture him shortly after October 10th.
* **Current Usefulness**: Due to Syd's recapture, calling this number (likely a burner phone) is expected to be fruitless; Syd would likely hang up and destroy the phone if contacted by unknown parties. The clue's value shifted from a direct contact method to confirmation of the Montreal connection after the fact.
