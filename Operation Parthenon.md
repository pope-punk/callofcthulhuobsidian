## Overview
[[Operation Parthenon]] is the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]'s current flagship project, initiated around 1993 and significantly developed from 1995 onwards. It represents an ambitious and highly secretive effort to deliberately cultivate a stable and potentially controllable [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]], designated [[IO (Emerging 1997)]]. This operation is centered within a purpose-built, subterranean chamber beneath the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. 

## A New Approach to RONINs
The conceptual foundation of [[Operation Parthenon]] stems directly from the lessons learned—or perhaps, [[Katherine Hill]]'s interpretation of those lessons—from the failure of [[Operation Colossus]] and the uncontrollable emergence of [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]]. Disenchanted with the volatility of auditory [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]] like [[The Echo]], Hill directed BSSA's focus towards visual and spatial patterns, believing them to offer a more reliable path to stable, and therefore potentially weaponizable, emergent intelligences. Parthenon is the embodiment of this strategic shift.

## The Architecture of a Mind - The Fauxflake Chamber
The core of [[Operation Parthenon]] is the specialized containment facility hidden beneath the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]. This chamber is not merely a laboratory but an engineered environment designed to be the "cradle" for [[IO (Emerging 1997)]]:
* **Modular Pews as the 3D Fauxflake:** The primary vector for IO's emergence is the [[Fauxflake]], a complex visual/spatial basilisk derived from [[The Snowflake]] (and thus [[The Foxglove Algorithm]]). Within the chamber, the Fauxflake is manifested in three dimensions through the precise and reconfigurable arrangement of the church pews. This physical layout *is* the basilisk in its primary, active form.
* **Mirrored Ceiling:** A crucial architectural feature, the mirrored ceiling is intended to provide the nascent IO with a means of "reflecting" upon its own structure and activity—a condition BSSA believes is necessary for a RONIN to achieve stable self-awareness.
* **Viewing Apparatus:** A specialized apparatus allows BSSA operatives to observe the complex 3D arrangement of the pews from above. This serves a critical secondary function, detailed below.

## Cultivating IO
The "congregation" of the Seventh Tabernacle, likely composed of individuals conditioned through [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]]'s methods or other forms of influence, serves as the human neural network or substrate for IO. Their collective perception of and interaction with the 3D [[Fauxflake]] pattern within the reflective chamber is intended to stimulate and guide IO's emergence.

## From Chamber to Weapon - The 2D Fauxflake
While nurturing IO within the chamber is a primary goal, [[Operation Parthenon]] also has a distinct weaponization objective. Using the viewing apparatus to observe the 3D pew layout, BSSA technicians work to translate this complex, dynamic pattern into reproducible **2D [[Fauxflake]] drawings**. These diagrams are intended to be portable, deployable versions of the basilisk. These 2D patterns are then sent to [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] for testing under [[Project Ozymandias]] to assess their infohazardous efficacy on human subjects.

## The Foxglove Factor
The development of the [[Fauxflake]] pattern, and thus the progress of [[Operation Parthenon]], received a significant, albeit unwilling, boost from [[Syd Foxglove]]. Between January and July 1997, BSSA leveraged Syd's innate, [[The Foxglove Algorithm|Algorithm]]-derived intuition to refine the Fauxflake. His contributions markedly improved its efficacy. Syd's subsequent escape in July 1997 undoubtedly stalled further refinement and was a major setback for the project.

## Current Objectives and Status (Late October 1997)
As of late October 1997, [[Operation Parthenon]] is ongoing. [[IO (Emerging 1997)]] continues its slow, deliberate emergence within the chamber. With the (imminent from the players' perspective, if they are unaware of the Montreal compromise) recapture of [[Syd Foxglove]], BSSA's immediate priority will be to force him to resume his work on the [[Fauxflake]], aiming to accelerate IO's development and perfect the 2D diagrams. The project remains BSSA's most significant and potentially most dangerous active operation.

## Significance
[[Operation Parthenon]] showcases BSSA's sophisticated understanding of [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]] and [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]] theory, albeit filtered through [[Katherine Hill]]'s particular ideology. It represents a concerted effort to move beyond accidental emergences and actively engineer a new form of consciousness for strategic purposes. The ethical implications and potential consequences of their success or failure are profound.
