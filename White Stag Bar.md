## Overview
A bar located in the industrial district of [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. Serves as the primary headquarters and hangout for the [[Hammerskull Crew]] neo-Nazi organization.

## Ownership & Personnel
* **Owner:** [[The Schwartz Family]] (Hammerskull leader)
* **Manager:** [[The Schwartz Family]]
* **Patrons/Members:** [[Hammerskull Crew]] members, including [[The Schwartz Family]], [[Calvin Pierce]] (formerly), [[Sean Fielding]] (formerly).

## Features
* **Public Bar Area:** Likely rough, unwelcoming to outsiders.
* **Basement Lab:** Hidden laboratory used for [[Methamphetamine]] production. Previously used (possibly by BSSA/Hammerskulls) for early [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] synthesis or storage. Site where [[Calvin Pierce]] stole pure F7 used in the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] poisoning. Contains emails between Schwartz brothers detailing the theft and providing access code (1489) to [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]].

## Significance
* Central hub for the [[Hammerskull Crew]].
* Direct link between Hammerskulls, drug production/dealing, and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s chemical operations ([[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] theft).
* Key investigation site providing access codes and context for the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] disaster.
