## Overview
[[IO (Emerging 1997)]] represents the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]'s ambitious and currently active endeavor to deliberately cultivate a stable, and ideally controllable, [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|Randomly Organizing Neural Information Network]]. This project, codenamed [[Operation Parthenon]], is centered within a specialized facility concealed beneath the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## The Genesis of IO - Operation Parthenon
Following the chaotic and ultimately uncontrollable emergence of the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN from their auditory basilisk experiments in [[Operation Colossus]], [[Katherine Hill]] and the BSSA shifted their strategic focus. Believing that visual and spatial patterns offered a pathway to more stable and predictable forms of emergent intelligence, they initiated [[Operation Parthenon]]. The primary goal was to move beyond simply creating [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]] for direct effect, and instead use a refined pattern to nurture a nascent RONIN that BSSA could potentially understand, influence, or even weaponize.

## The Cradle of Consciousness - The Chamber and The Fauxflake
The heart of [[Operation Parthenon]] is a purpose-built subterranean chamber beneath the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]. This space is meticulously designed to foster RONIN emergence:
* **Mirrored Ceiling:** This feature is critical, intended to provide the nascent IO with the means for "self-reflection"—a key theoretical component for an emergent consciousness to achieve stable self-awareness.
* **Modular Pews & The 3D Fauxflake:** The pews within the chamber are not static; they are modular and arranged in complex, shifting patterns based on the [[Fauxflake]] basilisk. This 3D arrangement of the pews, viewed by the congregation, *is* the primary form of the Fauxflake vector within the chamber.
* **Congregation as Substrate:** The human minds of the congregation, likely composed of suggestible individuals, former cultists from [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]]'s past, or those subtly conditioned, serve as the neural substrate or network nodes through which IO is intended to emerge. Their collective perception of and interaction with the 3D Fauxflake arrangement is the stimulus.
* **The Viewing Apparatus & 2D Fauxflake Translation:** A specialized viewing apparatus allows BSSA operatives to observe the complex 3D pew layout from above. This is crucial for their secondary goal: translating the dynamic 3D Fauxflake into reproducible 2D diagrams. These 2D drawings are the intended portable, weaponized form of the basilisk, which are then tested for efficacy at [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] as part of [[Project Ozymandias]].
* **Syd Foxglove's Refinement:** Between January and July 1997, [[Syd Foxglove]] was unwillingly coerced into using his innate understanding of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] to refine the [[Fauxflake]] pattern, significantly enhancing its potential efficacy.

## BSSA's Aspirations
With IO, BSSA hopes to achieve what previous RONIN events failed to deliver: a degree of control. Their aspirations likely include:
* **Stable Emergence:** Creating a RONIN that doesn't immediately self-destruct.
* **Pattern Weaponization:** Harnessing the unique informational patterns generated by or through IO for precise cognitive influence or information warfare.
* **Understanding Emergence:** Gaining deeper insights into the mechanics of RONIN emergence to further their research and dominance in the field of infohazardous technology.
* **Validation:** Proving [[Katherine Hill]]'s theories about the superiority of visual/spatial basilisks for controlled emergence.

## Current State and Uncertain Future (Late October 1997)
As of late October 1997, IO is still considered to be in its developmental phase within the Parthenon chamber. Limited neural pattern formation was detected as early as June 1996. However, [[Syd Foxglove]]'s escape in July 1997 likely stalled further refinement of the [[Fauxflake]], potentially slowing or altering IO's development trajectory. With Syd's recent recapture (unbeknownst to players at the very start of the Montreal arc), BSSA undoubtedly intends to force him to resume his work, making the future of IO a pressing concern. The project represents a significant, active threat, with the potential for a new form of emergent intelligence to gain influence, with or without BSSA's full control.
