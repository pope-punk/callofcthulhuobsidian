## Description

Andrew Christmas (1899-1949) was associated with the investigation into the [[CALLISTO (1949)|CALLISTO]] event.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **FBI**: Served as an Assistant Director of the FBI.
* **[[CONSORT Committee]]**: Likely a collaborator or government liaison connected to the CONSORT team investigating CALLISTO, given his wife [[Hannah Christmas]]'s central role.
* **Family**: Husband of Dr. [[Hannah Christmas]]; father or relative of [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)]].

## Status

Deceased. Killed on December 6th, 1949, when the initial [[CALLISTO (1949)|CALLISTO]] explosion in Tacoma struck the temporary dockside FBI field office he was in.
