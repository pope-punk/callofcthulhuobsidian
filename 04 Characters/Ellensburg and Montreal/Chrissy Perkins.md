## Description

Chrissy Perkins was the cheerful, well-meaning, and genuinely supportive head of the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] between 1987 and its merger post-1992. She was completely unaware of the committee's function as a [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] front for [[Operation Colossus]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **Led SCAI:** Headed the committee, overseeing its legitimate arts funding activities.
* **A&D Connection:** Knew [[Cleo Zhang]] and [[David Rubinsky]] through the committee's funding of "Axon and Dendrite," though she couldn't recall exactly how they were introduced (it was [[Raymond Vaughn (Agent Rococo)|Agent Rococo]]). Knew little about the show's sudden cancellation.
* **Interviewed (1993/94):** Was interviewed by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] and [[Éric Blanc]] during their initial investigation into [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]]. She was cooperative but ultimately unhelpful, as incriminating records had been purged by Agent Plaster ([[Phoebe Jones (Agent Plaster)|Phoebe Jones]]). She might recall this interview if approached again.
* **Post-SCAI:** Likely continued working within the Washington State Arts Commission after the SCAI was merged back into it.

## Status

Presumed living, potentially still working in state arts administration. Could serve as a valuable historical contact for investigators digging into "Axon and Dendrite" or [[Operation Colossus]], potentially corroborating the Alice/Blanc prior investigation.
