## Description

David Rubinsky was one of the unwitting hosts of the "Axon and Dendrite" radio show during [[Operation Colossus]] (1987-1992). He was executed by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] in mid-1992.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **"Axon and Dendrite"**: Co-hosted the avant-garde radio art show with [[Cleo Zhang]], which was used by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] to broadcast [[The Echo]] basilisk from the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. Funded via the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]].
* **[[Operation Colossus]]**: Unwitting participant in the BSSA operation.
* **SDEC Contact & Disruption:** In mid-1992, [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] contacted Rubinsky and Zhang, likely revealing some danger associated with the broadcasts. SDEC provided them with a **randomizer tool** and **mapping rules** ([[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]]) to disrupt the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN by altering the predictable structure of [[The Echo]]-laden broadcasts.
* **Final Testament & Security Measure:** Aware that SDEC had likely been compromised and that BSSA was hunting him, Rubinsky prepared a final, unaired "testament" episode of Axon and Dendrite. He deliberately structured its **six segments** using a specific **four-digit code** entered into the SDEC randomizer. He documented this final sequence ([[Rubinsky's Final Segment Sequence Log]]). His desperate motivation was to use the SDEC-specific system as a security measure, believing only surviving SDEC agents or their allies could decipher the code needed to access sensitive information he wanted to protect from BSSA.
* **The Lockbox:** In his final weeks, Rubinsky packed a lockbox with [[Cleo Zhang]]'s contact information and a short letter, set its combination to the same four-digit code he had used to permute his final "Axon and Dendrite" broadcast, and mailed it to SDEC's San Diego address. He believed only SDEC personnel could derive the combination from the [[Rubinsky's Final Segment Sequence Log|segment log]] using the [[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules|mapping rules]]. He did not know that SDEC had just been destroyed by [[Rhea]] (for unrelated reasons threaded back to SDEC's own [[The Echo|Echo]] investigation — see [[Post-SDEC Events]]). The package was returned undeliverable; by the time it came back to Ellensburg, Rubinsky was dead. His neighbor [[Professor Vance]] retrieved the unopened package from his porch and has held it ever since. See [[Rubinsky's Lockbox Puzzle]].
* **Execution:** Captured and executed by BSSA operatives at the [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] in mid-1992. The order came from [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Caraway]], who — fully aware of BSSA's Echo weaponization and moving to monopolize the SDEC investigation against [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Lopez's]] resistance — wanted Rubinsky and [[Cleo Zhang|Zhang]] dead before any investigation could reach them. The operatives' execution of the order was panicked and haphazard; Zhang escaped, partly because [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] subtly obstructed the sweep. The Chapel and the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] were sanitized in the same urgent cleanup. BSSA then pushed the cover story that EUROPA had destroyed SDEC — a misattribution [[Katherine Hill]] weaponized to argue that the [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol]] had failed. See [[Post-SDEC Events]] and [[Operation Colossus]].
