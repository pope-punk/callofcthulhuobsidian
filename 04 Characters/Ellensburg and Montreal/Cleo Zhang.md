## Description

Cleo Zhang was one of the unwitting hosts of the "Axon and Dendrite" radio show during [[Operation Colossus]] (1987-1992). She survived the operation's termination.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **"Axon and Dendrite":** Co-hosted the show with [[David Rubinsky]], funded via the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] (a BSSA front). She was primarily a visual artist who also served as a host.
* **SDEC Contact & Disruption:** In mid-1992, [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] contacted Rubinsky and Zhang, providing them with a randomizer tool and [[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules|mapping rules]] to disrupt the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN by altering broadcast structures.
* **Survival & Relocation:** [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Caraway]] ordered both her death and Rubinsky's during the Operation Colossus cleanup. BSSA operatives executed Rubinsky at the [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]], but the parallel sweep that was supposed to take Zhang was panicked and haphazard, partly because [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] subtly obstructed the order (an early seed of his later marital conflict with Katherine). She fled Ellensburg, relocated to Portland, OR under an assumed name, and has lived in hiding ever since, deeply traumatized and fearing [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]. She does not know a kill order was ever issued — only that BSSA "missed" her. See [[Post-SDEC Events]].
* **Contacted by [[Éric Blanc]] (Early 1997):** Blanc located her in Portland via SCAI financial records. Due to her fear, she swore Blanc to secrecy regarding her location. As part of that reciprocal arrangement, **she retains Blanc's home address in Montreal** as her only contact route to him in an emergency.
* **Lockbox Contents:** Her current contact information is the primary content hidden within [[David Rubinsky]]'s lockbox, secured via the [[Rubinsky's Lockbox Puzzle]]. Finding her is the *only* viable route to Blanc's apartment address — Nine-String and BNJC never had it.

## Role for the Montreal Arc

After the players solve [[Rubinsky's Lockbox Puzzle]] (or follow the parallel Amanda Green → Seattle friend chain), they reach Zhang in Portland. If trust is established (e.g., by demonstrating SDEC-side knowledge and disavowing BSSA), Zhang will reluctantly:

* Confirm Blanc's existence and his role as Alice's longtime contact.
* Confirm that Blanc was the person who located her in early 1997 and that they have a strict no-contact protocol.
* Provide **Blanc's Montreal apartment address** — which she has been holding since 1997 and which is not in BNJC's possession.
* Warn the players that if Blanc has gone silent, something is badly wrong.

She will not travel with the investigators and is essentially a one-shot information node.

## Status

Living in hiding in Portland as of Late 1997. The structural gate between the Ellensburg arc and the Montreal arc.
