## Overview
Girlfriend of [[Syd Foxglove]]. Resides in or near [[Ellensburg Area, WA|Ellensburg]]. Connected to the historical [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] front. Serves as a crucial link for the investigators in Act II.

## Known Information & Connections
* **Relationship**: Girlfriend of [[Syd Foxglove]]. Deeply concerned about his disappearance/safety.
* **Photograph**: A photograph of her with Syd was found in his empty holding cell beneath the [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]], providing the initial lead to find her.
* **Location**: Resides in the Ellensburg area. Tracking her down involves following the lead from the photograph and potentially investigating [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] records.
* **"Axon and Dendrite" / [[Monkey Gone to Heaven (Song)]]:** Was a fan of the "Axon and Dendrite" radio show. Introduced [[Syd Foxglove]] to the show and likely the song "Monkey Gone to Heaven" by the Pixies. Possesses memorabilia, like posters or potentially recordings.

## Information Provided to Investigators (Planned - Act II)
* **Syd's Computer Access:** Possesses Syd's username/password (`doolittle567`?) or other credentials needed to access his old workstation computer at the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]].
* **[[Monkey Gone to Heaven (Song)]] Clue:** Confirms Syd's connection to the song/band (linking to the password clue found earlier).
* **[[Axon and Dendrite Recordings]] Lead:** Reveals she lent her recordings of the "Axon and Dendrite" show to a friend in Seattle. This friend is the link to contacting [[Cleo Zhang]], who in turn can provide information about [[Éric Blanc]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Presumed living in the Ellensburg area. Key contact person for players needing access to Syd's ErgoMatic data and the path towards [[Éric Blanc]]. Likely cooperative if players can prove they are trying to help Syd.
