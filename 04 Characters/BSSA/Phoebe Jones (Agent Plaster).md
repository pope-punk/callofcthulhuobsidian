## Description

Phoebe Jones was the assumed identity of **BSSA Agent "Plaster"**. She served on the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] from ~1987 to 1992, handling its finances. As of October 1997 she has been pulled back into active duty operating the compromised email account of [[Éric Blanc]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **BSSA Agent:** Operative working for [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]. Codename "Plaster".
* **SCAI Role:** Infiltrated the committee, managing its budget. This position allowed her to funnel funds to "Axon and Dendrite" and obscure purchase orders for specialized equipment used to reactivate [[Operation POUTINE]] relay stations for [[Operation Colossus]].
* **Record Purge (1992):** Following the termination of [[Operation Colossus]], Agent Plaster purged incriminating financial records from SCAI archives before it was merged back into the state arts commission. (Traces of this purge are what [[Éric Blanc]] eventually used to reconstruct the operation in 1996-97 — which in turn led to BSSA flagging him.)
* **Blanc Email Operation (Oct 1997 - present):** After BSSA captured Blanc and extracted his credentials, Plaster took over Blanc's email account. She poses as Blanc to engage anyone who reaches out, identifying and luring contacts from Alice's network. Her decades of institutional knowledge of EUROPA-era tradecraft let her maintain the deception convincingly. **Subtle Tell:** A real intelligence officer would scrutinize unfamiliar correspondents far more rigorously than "Blanc" does. Players paying attention to tone may recognize that "Blanc" warms up too easily.

## Role in the Montreal Trap

Plaster's purpose is the soft side of Katherine's two-pronged trap. While BSSA strike teams set up at the [[McCord Museum]] for the standing-protocol date, Plaster works the email account to:
* Confirm Syd's presumed travel and movements (when he, unknowingly, sends a message in clear).
* Lure any investigators who route their first attempt at contact through the [[Montreal Phone Number]] voicemail into the museum on the correct day.
* Filter out random callers and identify Mirror-Breaker-affiliated correspondents.

## Status

Active. Operating the compromised Blanc email account from a BSSA-controlled location, in coordination with Katherine Hill's Montreal field team.
