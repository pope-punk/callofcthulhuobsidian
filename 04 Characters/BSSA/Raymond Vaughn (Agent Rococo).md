## Description

Raymond Vaughn, known by the code name **Agent Rococo**, is a technical specialist affiliated with the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]**: Served as the technical implementation specialist for [[Operation Colossus]].
* **[[Operation Colossus]]**: Installed the modulator at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] used to alter [[The Echo]] tones during the "Axon and Dendrite" radio show broadcasts. This created the secondary feedback loop contributing to [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]]'s emergence.
* Introduced [[Cleo Zhang]] and [[David Rubinsky]] to the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] to secure funding for 'Axon and Dendrite', likely leveraging connections within the art scene.
* **[[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)]] Intervention**: Surreptitiously removed the modulator from the Numbers Station in 1992 following SDEC's intervention. Escaped back to Boise. Was recognized as a BSSA agent by [[Agent Chrysalis|Agent Chrysalis]] (then SDEC).

## Status

His status after the conclusion of Operation Colossus in 1992 is not detailed in the provided documents. He successfully escaped Ellensburg.
