## Description

Clancy Martell, known by the code name **Agent Chisel**, is the primary chemical researcher for the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]**: Key personnel involved in BSSA's chemical operations.
* **[[Project Ozymandias]]**: Lead chemical researcher for this project. Responsible for the refinement of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] (from earlier HFD) and the development of [[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)|G5]].
* **[[Operation Colossus]]**: Involved in the chemical research component of Colossus, developing proto-F7/HFD.
* **Location**: Has a workspace/laboratory within the [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]].
* **Potential Encounter**: The `Future Story Trajectory` suggests players will likely encounter and kill Agent Chisel during their investigation of the Center for Hope.

## Status

Active BSSA chemical researcher as of October 1997, based at Saint Anselm's.
