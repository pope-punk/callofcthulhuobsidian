## Description

Agent Diaphanous was an operative belonging to the [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|San Diego Entomology Club (SDEC)]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]]**: Active agent within the organization before its destruction.
* **Discovery**: While operating SDEC's "CICADA-class sonar/radar," Agent Diaphanous detected the spatial anomaly near [[Dead Horse Quarry]] which was linked to [[Rhea]]'s [[Hive]]. This discovery, connected to [[Winston Foxglove]]'s memoirs, likely precipitated the attack on SDEC.

## Status

Presumed deceased. Killed during the destruction of the [[SDEC Facility|SDEC facility]] by a [[Writers|Rhea Writer]] in late 1992. [[Agent Chrysalis|Agent Chrysalis]] was the known survivor.
