## Description

Travis Ward — **Agent Tremolo** — is a Senior Field Agent within [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]]. Mid-40s Black man with an unbelievably deep voice. First contact for outsiders at BNJC HQ. As of late October 1997 he is the cell's de facto operational center of gravity — not because he runs it, but because his director has stopped acting.

## Position inside the cell

Tremolo is read in to the *fact* of [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]]'s covert operation but not to its specifics. He knows:

* Alice is rogue and in the field.
* [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)|Nine-String]] is supporting her, off-books, via [[Agent Chrysalis]].
* She is almost certainly involved in [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]]'s disappearance.

He does **not** know:

* The vehicle procurement chain (the rental Hondas, the [[Edmund White Estate]] base, the I-90 / COTU drop-off mechanism).
* What Alice was actually doing in the weeks leading up to her F7-injection — i.e., how close she had gotten to the BSSA front network before Dylan's transfer of Syd forced her into direct action.
* The full extent of her current decline. Chrysalis's reports come to Nine-String, not to him.

## Covert insubordination

Nine-String has effectively **sealed the cell** to protect BNJC from BSSA exposure. He cannot bring Alice in without surfacing his own concealment of Dylan's death and the entire off-books operation, both of which are visible threads PCBOT could weaponize. The lockdown is institutionally sound and operationally paralytic.

Tremolo's response is to circumvent it without ever being seen to do so. He is **covertly insubordinate** — he cannot tell his director he is working around him, and he cannot tell the players he is working around his director. So he uses the investigators as the moving piece Nine-String has refused to deploy.

Practically, this means:

* **The cryptic assessment scene at BNJC HQ is tradecraft cover.** The improvisation prompts, the tarot-style cards, the deliberately oblique disclosures — all of it functions as deniable interaction. Nine-String, looking at the records afterward, sees Senior Field Agent Tremolo following Mirror-Breaker assessment protocol with new outside contacts. What is actually happening is that Tremolo is feeding the players the operational threads he himself can't pull on without exposure.
* **He hands the players information selectively.** Identifies [[Katherine Hill]] by name. Confirms Alice's rogue status. Confirms Dylan is missing. Hints at the factional split. Implies — without stating — that the official story doesn't fit.
* **He cannot vouch for the players openly.** They have to act in a way that lets him keep plausible deniability with his director. If Nine-String concludes Tremolo has been steering them, the trust breach inside the three-person leadership circle (Nine-String / Tremolo / Chrysalis) is potentially terminal — and as of late October 1997, Chrysalis is already gone.

## Misreadings and theories

Tremolo has had to construct his own working theory of what happened to Alice in the absence of either ground-truth from Chrysalis or institutional candor from Nine-String. His current best guess is that [[Syd Foxglove|Syd]] may have used [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] to influence Alice cognitively — a misreading shaped by BNJC's pre-existing Foxglove paranoia and by his lack of access to F7 as an explanatory mechanism. He is wrong; F7 is the actual operator. But he doesn't have the chemistry to know that, and Nine-String hasn't read him in to what little Nine-String himself knows about it.

## In play

* **First contact** for the players at BNJC HQ. Runs the assessment with [[Leah Burns (Agent Legato)|Legato]].
* **Source of more than he is supposed to disclose.** Players who push will get answers Nine-String would not have authorized.
* **At risk.** Every meaningful disclosure to the players is a step further outside his director's lockdown. The longer the campaign runs, the more exposed he becomes inside BNJC.

## Status

Active BNJC agent as of October 1997. Operating in a deniable adjacency to his own director's sealed-facility posture.
