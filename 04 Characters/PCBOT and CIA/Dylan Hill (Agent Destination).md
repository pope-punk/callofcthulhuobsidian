## Overview

Born 1960 to CIA-officer parents. PCBOT Agent Destination. Assigned as the local PCBOT liaison to [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]] and [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] from mid-1993. Married to [[Katherine Hill]].

A career intelligence officer who spent his last six months gradually understanding that he was on the wrong side of his own marriage.

## Key traits

* Filtered information reported in both directions — to BNJC and to Katherine — through a single channel he controlled.
* Diligent. Uncovered the [[Signal Dummies]] / dirt-shipment pattern in LTX records before anyone else did.
* Increasingly disillusioned with Katherine and BSSA across spring–summer 1997.

## History

* **1980s.** CIA field agent on [[Operation POUTINE|POUTINE]]. Met Katherine during the CIA-to-PCBOT/BSSA infrastructure handoff. Married 1988. Continued CIA supervisory role with respect to [[Operation Colossus]] until officially recruited to PCBOT in 1990.
* **1992.** Carried out the torture and execution of [[David Rubinsky]] in the wake of SDEC's intervention. Returned to Boise with Katherine.
* **1993.** Redeployed to Sequoia Falls as PCBOT liaison under Lopez's faction plan. Primary unstated assignment: keep BNJC away from the BSSA chamber being built under [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life|Seventh Tabernacle]].
* **1995.** Became BNJC's sole channel for LTX records after Caraway's coup formalized the PCBOT liaison position. Filtered out any indication of basilisk-related "leakage" from LTX shipping data.
* **1996.** [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] completed with discreet PCBOT funding.
* **January 1997.** [[Syd Foxglove]] held briefly in a sub-floor cell at Dylan's home — **401 Rosewood Ct** — until space was prepared at the Seventh Tabernacle. The basement held a mattress on a frame, a shackle/manacle attached to a water-heater pipe, and (at some point during the months that followed Syd's transfer) a [[Spectator Grubs|Spectator Grub]]. By the time the players reach the address in October 1997 the house has been burned to the ground; the basement remnants are still inferable. Who burned it, and why, is unresolved.
* **March 1997.** Noticed the pattern of "dirt" shipments to absurd recipient names ("Thermos Milkshaft" and the rest). Began to suspect [[Operation Parthenon]] was incubating something analogous to EUROPA. Katherine dismissed the concern.
* **April–June 1997.** Repeatedly tried to surface the anomaly with an increasingly hostile Katherine. Quietly copied key shipping manifests. PCBOT (presumably at Katherine's prompting) revoked his LTX credentials and reassigned him to [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] testing oversight and guarding Syd, who he was supposed to transfer to Saint Anselm's.
* **June–July 1997.** Working primarily at Saint Anselm's. Watched Syd fail to react to F7 in any of the standard ways. Started talking to Syd about [[Oliver Foxglove]]'s death and stopped believing Syd had been exposed to The Echo. Dylan does not believe in monsters; he believes BSSA has lost control of whatever it built, and that bothers him professionally.
* **July 13, 1997.** Visited BNJC for the last time, claiming he was returning to DC.
* **July 14, 1997.** Tailed by Alice from his home to Saint Anselm's.
* **July 15, 1997.** Ambushed by Alice while transferring Syd from Saint Anselm's to the Seventh Tabernacle. Taken to [[Edmund White Estate]]. Refused to reveal BSSA operations to Alice. Shared his LTX findings but demanded to speak to Caraway before going further. When Alice escalated toward torture, Dylan attempted to escape and injected her with a full F7 syringe in the struggle. She killed him with her saxophone.

## Status

Deceased (July 15, 1997). [[Dylan Hill's Shipping Manifests|His shipping manifests]] and notes on F7/G5, now in Alice's possession, are critical clues. His death is the inciting incident for Alice's rogue arc.
