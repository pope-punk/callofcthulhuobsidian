## Description

Robert Toomey was a government ally associated with [[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Jacqui Lopez]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[Jacqui Lopez (Director Javelin)|Jacqui Lopez]] Ally**: Supported Lopez's faction within the [[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)|PCBOT]] structure, likely opposing the consolidation efforts of [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Henry Caraway]] and [[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill]].
* **Assassination**: Mysteriously assassinated by the [[CIA|CIA]] around mid-1994. His death coincided with Caraway's CIA-assisted coup of PCBOT and the sidelining of Jacqui Lopez, suggesting his removal was politically motivated to clear the way for Caraway's faction.

## Status

Deceased. Assassinated mid-1994.
