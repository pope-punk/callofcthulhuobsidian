## Description

Jacqui Lopez, known by the code name **Director Javelin**, was formerly associated with leadership roles within the Mirror-Breaker network. She was known as **Director Journey** during her time at the head of PCBOT, and Director Javelin a. 

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[VTFT (Virginia Track & Field Team)]]**: Originally the leader of this Mirror-Breaker organization.
* **[[PCBOT (Pacific Coast Board of Tourism)]]**: Formerly led PCBOT, the oversight organization.
* **Factional Conflict**: Represented a faction opposing [[Henry Caraway (Agent Hotel)|Henry Caraway]] and [[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill]]. She won the debate to relocate [[BNJC (Blue Note Jazz Collective)|BNJC]] to Sequoia Falls to investigate EUROPA, assigning [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]] as liaison.
* **Ousting**: Was sidelined/removed from PCBOT leadership during Caraway's CIA-assisted coup in 1994. Her government ally, [[Robert Toomey]], was assassinated around the same time.
* **Potential future use:** A [[Seer Configuration|seer]]?  

## Status

Alive, and still in charge at VTFT.
