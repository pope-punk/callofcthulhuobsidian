## Description

Martin Kavanaugh was the CEO of [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] from the early 1990s until mid-1996.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **LTX Leadership**: Oversaw LTX during a period of stagnation when the Sequoia Falls branch was maintained primarily as a tax write-off by majority shareholder [[American Meridian]].
* **[[ErgoMatic Manufacturing|ErgoMatic]] Partnership**: Initiated the "strategic partnership" with [[Oliver Foxglove]]'s ErgoMatic around 1995. LTX's motivation was likely to revitalize its antiquated operations with ErgoMatic's groundbreaking technology.
* **Replacement**: Replaced as CEO in mid-1996 by [[Richard Harmon]], who was installed by [[American Meridian]] specifically to push through the LTX-ErgoMatic merger.

## Status

His status after being replaced as CEO is not mentioned in the documents.
