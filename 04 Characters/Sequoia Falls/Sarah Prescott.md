## Description

Sarah Prescott was an employee of [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] living in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Known Information & Connections

* **[[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] Employee**: Worked for LTX. Her employee keycard was found at her apartment.
* **Address**: Lived at 881 3rd Ave NE, Sequoia Falls.
* **Added to the manifest as bait**: Per the Master Timeline, [[Rhea]] added humans to [[Dylan Hill's Shipping Manifests|Dylan's shipping manifest]] in late July 1997 specifically to bait [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]] into killing civilians. Sarah's home address appeared on the manifest the same way a [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]]'s mailing site would; [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]] forecloses Alice's capacity to distinguish.
* **Captured by Alice on August 10, 1997.** Alice arrived at the apartment in a rental Honda procured by [[Agent Chrysalis]] at the I-90 underpass drop-off. She cattle-prodded Sarah, dragged her out of the apartment (the drag marks remain), and sealed her in the trunk of Sarah's own Toyota. **Sarah did not die immediately.** She died of dehydration over the following several days in the sealed trunk — Alice's standard method for dispatching what she believes are Signal Dummies, applied with the same protocol regardless of whether the captive is actually a Dummy.
* **The abandoned rental Honda.** Alice left her rental Honda parked at the apartment complex when she drove off in Sarah's Toyota. Whether this was an attempt to fake a "normal absence," an F7-confused error about which vehicle was hers, or a procedural artifact she cannot articulate is unclear and is meant to remain unclear. The Honda is still in the parking lot at 881 3rd Ave NE as of October 1997 and is a discoverable clue trail back to the rental agency Chrysalis was using.
* **Body and Toyota disposition.** The Toyota — with Sarah's body in the trunk — was driven by Alice to the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU]] underpass and parked among the row of rental Hondas containing dehydrated Signal Dummies. The Toyota is the visually anomalous "civilian car" in the COTU inventory alongside [[Thomas Reeves]]'s Nissan and [[Sean Fielding]]'s car.
* **Crime Scene**: Her apartment contained an LTX jacket and [[The Hexacle]] graffiti left by Alice. Drag marks across the floor lead toward the parking lot. Players investigated her apartment.
* **Keys**: A set of keys including a Toyota key, house key, and keychain with phone number 509-312-3036 were found in Alice's dropped satchel, presumed to be Sarah's.

## Status

Deceased — died of dehydration in the trunk of her own car several days after capture on August 10, 1997. Her name appears crossed out in [[Alice's Journal (Satchel)|Alice's journal]].
