## Overview
(Deceased Late Sept 1997) Member of the [[Hammerskull Crew]] and friend/lover of [[Sean Fielding]]. Played a key role in the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] mass poisoning event.

## Key Traits & Affiliations
* **Affiliation:** [[Hammerskull Crew]].
* **Relationships:** Friend/lover of [[Sean Fielding]]. Likely knew other Hammerskulls ([[The Schwartz Family]], etc.).
* **Background:** Possibly formerly homeless or recruited by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]/Hammerskulls from a vulnerable background.

## Role in Plot
* **F7 Theft:** Stole pure [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] from the [[White Stag Bar]] basement lab (likely in late September 1997). Emails between [[The Schwartz Family|Schwartz brothers]] confirm this theft.
* **COTU Poisoning:** Distributed the stolen pure [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] within the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] homeless community. This caused the mass casualty event (starvation via food worship).
    * **Motivation:** This action was likely directed or manipulated by [[Rhea]]. Rhea's goal was probably to clear the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] encampment (which interfered with [[The Hexacle|Hexacle]] protection of a Hive entrance) and/or to flush out or eliminate [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]].
* **Victim:** Found murdered at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] by players on Oct 10, 1997. Killed by a [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Writer]] after distributing the F7, likely silenced to prevent tracing the action back to [[Rhea]]. Had a bloodstained love note from [[Sean Fielding]] on his body.

## Significance
* Directly responsible for the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]] tragedy, albeit likely as a pawn of [[Rhea]].
* His theft links the [[White Stag Bar]] lab to the events at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]].
* His relationship with [[Sean Fielding]] provides another connection between plot threads.
* His murder by a [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Writer]] confirms [[Rhea]]'s direct involvement in the COTU incident.

## Status
* Deceased (Killed Late Sept 1997 by a [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Writer]]). Body found at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]].
