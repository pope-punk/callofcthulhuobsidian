## Description

Thomas Reeves was an employee of [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] living in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. He was the brother of police detective [[Colin Reeves]].

## Known Information & Connections

* **[[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] Employee**: Worked for LTX. His employee keycard was found at his apartment.
* **Family**: Brother of Detective [[Colin Reeves]]. Colin's investigation into Thomas's disappearance connected him with the player characters.
* **Address**: Lived at Lakeview Place, #203, Sequoia Falls.
* **Added to the manifest as bait**: Like [[Sarah Prescott]], Thomas was added to [[Dylan Hill's Shipping Manifests|Dylan's shipping manifest]] by [[Rhea]] in late July 1997 to bait [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]] into killing civilians she could not distinguish from [[Signal Dummies]] under [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]].
* **Captured by Alice on September 8, 1997.** Alice arrived at the Lakeview Place apartment in a rental Honda procured by [[Agent Chrysalis]] at the I-90 underpass drop-off. She cattle-prodded Thomas (the prod marks on his pet husky are from the same encounter — F7-broken targeting), sealed him in the trunk of his own Nissan, and left. **He did not die immediately.** He died of dehydration in the sealed trunk over the following several days — the same protocol Alice uses on actual Signal Dummies, applied without distinction.
* **The abandoned rental Honda.** Alice left her rental Honda parked at the Lakeview Place lot when she drove off in Thomas's Nissan. The Honda is still there as of October 1997 and is a discoverable clue trail back to the rental agency [[Agent Chrysalis|Chrysalis]] was using.
* **Body and Nissan disposition.** The Nissan — with Thomas's body in the trunk — was driven to the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU]] underpass and parked among the row of rental Hondas containing dehydrated Signal Dummies, alongside Sarah's Toyota and (eventually) Sean's car.
* **Same day as the Reaper attack.** September 8 was also the day a [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reaper]] tracked Alice to the [[Edmund White Estate]] and forced her permanent retreat into the tunnels.
* **Crime Scene**: Evidence at his apartment included cattle prod marks on his pet husky and [[The Hexacle]] graffiti left by Alice. Players investigated his apartment.
* **Keys**: A set of keys including a Nissan key, large key, small key, and "Lakeview Place" fob were found in Alice's dropped satchel, presumed to be Thomas's.

## Status

Deceased — died of dehydration in the trunk of his own car several days after capture on September 8th, 1997. His name appears crossed out in [[Alice's Journal (Satchel)|Alice's journal]].
