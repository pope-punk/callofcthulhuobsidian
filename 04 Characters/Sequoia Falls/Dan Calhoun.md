## Description

Dan Calhoun is the owner or operator of Saul’s Supply Station, a hardware store in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]]. He knew [[Sean Fielding]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* [[Sauls Supply Station]]: Runs this local hardware store.
* **[[Sean Fielding]] Acquaintance**: Knew Sean Fielding for about two years. Provided investigators with information about seeing Sean's car parked outside the store on Saturday, September 27th, 1997 (one day after Sean's capture by Alice; Sean was at that moment still alive in the trunk, dying of dehydration over the following days). Mentioned Sean frequented "private" bars.
* **Witness to [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]]**: Provided a description of a "strange jazz woman" (Alice Redmond) who came into his store on that September 27th visit. He noted her dirty appearance, pine needles in her hair, forced smile, large amount of cash, and her purchases: crazy glue, red spray paint, and a cattle prod. His security footage confirmed Alice's identity. This is the visible signature of Alice operating without [[Agent Chrysalis]]'s logistical support — Chrysalis vanished on September 25, and Alice was now running her own supply runs in a captured vehicle for the first time.
* **Voicemail**: Left a voicemail on Sean Fielding's answering machine, which players discovered.

## Status

Presumed living and working at Saul's Supply Station as of October 1997. Acted as an early source of information for the player investigators.
