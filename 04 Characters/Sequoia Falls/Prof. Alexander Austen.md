## Description

Professor Alexander Austen is a chemistry professor at the local community college in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **Analysis of Samples**: The player investigators brought samples recovered from their investigation to Professor Austen for analysis. These included:
    * Black sludge from the door of the office where the Alphonse Spicecube automaton was stationed (identified as organic, possibly plant/fungi).
    * The severed arm of the automaton (identified as scientifically advanced but known, high fluorine content, capillary action).
    * The "worm" found at Signal Dummy locations (identified as 98% sugar, trace fats, 2% artificial DNA using only G/C nucleotides).
* **Role**: Serves as a source of scientific analysis and exposition for the strange biological and synthetic materials encountered by the players.

## Status

Presumed active professor at the community college as of late 1997/early 1998 (based on timing of player interactions).
