## Description

Colin Reeves was a police detective in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **Investigation**: Was investigating the disappearance of his brother, Thomas Reeves (an [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] employee and one of [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]]'s victims). Left his business card at LTX, leading players to contact him.
* **Shared Information**: Informed players about the police moratorium on missing persons cases in Sequoia Falls and that someone with clout had requested all files on the Balloon Mask Killer (connected to [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]).
* **Collaboration with Players**: Met with players and accompanied them to investigate [[Alice Redmond's House]].

## Status

Deceased. Killed by a [[Reapers|Reaper]] ("Elk-like" entity) at [[Alice Redmond's House]] on October 8th, 1997, while investigating alongside the players.
