## Description

Bruce Kennedy is identified as a Sheriff's Deputy in Kittitas County (where Ellensburg is located) and a member of the [[Hammerskull Crew]].

## Known Actions & Connections

* **[[Hammerskull Crew]]**: Member of this neo-Nazi organization.
* **Kittitas County Sheriff's Department**: Holds a position as a deputy, suggesting Hammerskull infiltration of local law enforcement.
* **[[Syd Foxglove]]'s Capture**: Detained Syd Foxglove after Syd reported his father [[Oliver Foxglove]]'s murder (Jan 11, 1997). Kennedy planned to frame Syd for the murder to eliminate [[The Foxglove Family]], likely on behalf of Hammerskull/BSSA interests, but [[Katherine Hill|Katherine Hill]] intervened to secure Syd for BSSA instead.

## Status

Presumed active Kittitas County Sheriff's Deputy and Hammerskull member as of 1997.
