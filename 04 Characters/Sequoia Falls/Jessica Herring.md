## Description

Jessica Herring is the HR Manager at the [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] Headquarters in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Known Information & Connections

* **Role**: Holds the position of HR Manager at LTX.
* **Office**: Her office was noted as being locked during the player investigators' visit.
* **Awareness**: Aware of the recent employee disappearances (like [[Sean Fielding]], [[Sarah Prescott]], [[Thomas Reeves]]) connected to LTX. Player character Maxwell Harrison interacted with her. She was described as seeming "useless" by the players, likely indicating she offered no helpful information or was obstructive.
