## Description

Edmund White was the **Balloon Mask Killer (BMK)**, a historical serial killer investigated by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]] prior to the events of the campaign. He owned a large, isolated estate on the outskirts of [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Known Information & Connections

* **Balloon Mask Killer**: Identified as the BMK, a serial killer active in the late 1980s, known as "the first serial killer of the internet age." [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]] solved the case during her time with the FBI.
* **[[Edmund White Estate]]**: Owned the estate (Location 'X' on Alice's Map) which was associated with the BMK case (identified as "Balloon Mask Killer’s vacation home"). Alice later used this estate as a safe house (July 15 - Sept 8, 1997).
* **Legacy**: The BMK case files were requested by someone with clout during the current investigation, suggesting interest in Alice's past or methods.

## Connection to Current Events

While Edmund White himself is historical, his property became entangled in the current events:
* **Alice's Safe House**: [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]] used his estate after fleeing her apartment. Preserved [[Spectator Grubs|Spectator Grub]] samples were collected here by Alice.
* **[[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)]]'s Death**: The estate was the site of Dylan Hill's torture and eventual death at Alice's hands (July 15, 1997). Evidence like [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] vials, needles, and blood can be found.
* **[[Reapers|Reaper]] Attack**: A Reaper attacked Alice here on September 8th, 1997, forcing her to abandon the location and relocate to [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]]. The Reaper likely still uses the estate as a hiding place.
* **Access**: The woods around the estate may require a low dose of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] to navigate safely.
