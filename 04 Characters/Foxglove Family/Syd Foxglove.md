## Overview

Sydney "Syd" Foxglove (b. 1977). Son of [[Oliver Foxglove]] and [[Susan Foxglove]]; grandson of [[Winston Foxglove]]. Biotech researcher at [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]]'s BAD division until his father's murder; [[Scions|Foxglove Configuration]] inheritor activated by his mother's death in 1995. The third Foxglove generation to be both a tool of Rhea's plan and a target for whichever faction currently most wants the Algorithm in their possession.

The campaign's most-pursued person. The only person who can synthesize [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]]. As of late October 1997 he is in [[Montreal, QC]] and on his way into BSSA's [[McCord Museum]] trap.

## Algorithm aptitude

Syd's relationship to [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] is the most intuitive of the three Foxglove generations. Winston experienced the Algorithm as conscious insight; Oliver experienced it as subconscious creative drive; Syd experiences it as fluency. He produces [[Ylynthii Numerals]] without having been taught them. He completed the [[GenAssist Universal Tissue Printer]] essentially by feel. He dreams in lattice structures and stellar arrays.

Outside the Algorithm, Syd is a goth-leaning twenty-year-old. He likes the Pixies ("[[Monkey Gone to Heaven (Song)]]" is the source of the [[Doolittle567 Password Clue]] on his ErgoMatic account). He is haunted, sharper than he gets credit for, and unprepared for the situation he is in.

## What happened to him in 1997

* **January 10.** Watched his father get killed by a [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Reaper]] at the family home.
* **January 11.** Detained by Deputy [[Bruce Kennedy]] (Hammerskulls); brief plan to frame him for Oliver's death.
* **January 11–July 15.** BSSA custody. Initial F7 exposure under [[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)|Agent Chisel]] revealed Syd as atypically resistant — Katherine misread this as cognitive distinctiveness rather than the Foxglove-Configuration immunity it actually is. Transferred to [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]]. Coerced into refining the [[Fauxflake]] for [[Operation Parthenon]]. His work measurably improved the pattern.
* **Spring 1997.** [[Dylan Hill (Agent Destination)|Dylan Hill]]'s growing scrutiny pushed Katherine to move Syd back to [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] for "evaluation" — a maneuver to keep Parthenon quiet. Fauxflake refinement stalled. Dylan made clandestine contact with Syd during this period and resolved to extract him.
* **July 15.** Katherine ordered Syd transferred back to the Church to resume Fauxflake work. Dylan intercepted the transfer to rescue him. [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]], who was tailing Dylan, ambushed them both. She freed Syd, sent him to [[Montreal, QC]] to live under [[Éric Blanc]]'s protection, and went on to kill Dylan in the interrogation at [[Edmund White Estate]].
* **July 17–late October.** In Montreal. Blanc arranged chemistry access at [[McGill University]]. Syd worked on G6 — non-gallium antidote to F7 — but lacked his father's research notes and the sealed [[THEBES]] reagent in Oliver's home safe. Blanc moved Syd from his apartment to a small rental nearby within the first weeks, and established a standing fallback rendezvous at the [[McCord Museum]] on a designated day each month.

## What he understands

Syd has the **clearest theoretical picture** of any character in the campaign. His [[The Foxglove Algorithm|Foxglove Algorithm]] fluency, his six months of direct [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]] captivity (including the [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]] exposure that revealed his configuration-immunity to [[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)|Chisel]] and Katherine), and his pre-existing technical access to [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] put him in a position to synthesize what no other living protagonist can:

* That [[Rhea]] exists as an entity behind the substrate work.
* That the Algorithm is what's being silently replicated through [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing|ErgoMatic]]'s products ([[DNA-Machine Code Conversion System|DNA-Machine Code Conversion]], the [[GenAssist Universal Tissue Printer]], the merger logistics).
* That F7 is the pharmacology of pattern-recognition suppression — the same mechanism behind the [[The Hexacle|Hexacle]]'s amnesia effect, the basilisks' neutralization, and his own anomalous failure to react when BSSA dosed him.

He has none of the field-operational picture. He doesn't know about the [[Signal Dummies]] specifically, the [[Dylan Hill's Shipping Manifests|shipping manifests]], or the COTU encampment. His clarity is theoretical and chemical, not tactical.

## Who he is in contact with

Only [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]], via [[Agent Chrysalis|Chrysalis's]] encrypted relay. **He has no direct contact with [[Nigel Christmas (Nine-String)|Nine-String]] or BNJC** — Chrysalis is the entire channel. Syd's theoretical clarity therefore reaches only an F7-impaired field agent who cannot process it, and not the cell leadership that could act on it. This is one of the campaign's central information-topology gaps: the person who understands what is happening cannot tell the people who could intervene.

## What he believes he is doing

Synthesizing [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] to cure Alice — a fellow agent he liked and trusted, who saved him on July 15, and who he knows from Chrysalis's reports is F7-impaired and increasingly erratic. He understands his work as a recovery effort for an injured colleague.

**He does not know Alice has been killing people in her impaired state.** Chrysalis does not know, so Chrysalis cannot tell him. From Syd's vantage, G6 is a chemistry race to restore a friend before her cognitive decline becomes irreversible. From a vantage no one in the operation actually holds, it is also a chemistry race to *stop her from killing more civilians* — a goal he is unwittingly serving and has no way to weigh against the others. When the cure is finally administered in the tunnels and a lucid Alice has to confront the body count, Syd learns at the same moment what his work has been doing in two registers simultaneously.

## Where he is now

Out of contact with Blanc. Doesn't know Blanc was captured in early October. Sees the next protocol date (October 20) approaching. Intends to honor it.

BSSA will be at the museum waiting for him.

## Significance

Three things converge on Syd. He is the only person who can synthesize G6, which is the only safe cure for Alice, which is the only path to decrypting Blanc's drive, which is the only entry into PCBOT, which is the only route to SDEC. Lose Syd and the entire Act II→III chain collapses. He is also — separately, and from BSSA's perspective — the only person who can keep [[Operation Parthenon]] advancing. And from Rhea's perspective he is one of two Foxglove Scions still standing (the other being himself in the future) and a critical link in the production chain for the Algorithm-derivative biotech Rhea now wants.

The world wants Syd alive for incompatible reasons. The world is mostly going to fail him.
