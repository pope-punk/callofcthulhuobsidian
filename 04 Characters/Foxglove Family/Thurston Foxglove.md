## Description

Thurston Foxglove (1865-1934) was the founder of the original Foxglove family business.

## Known Actions & Connections

* **Foxglove Containers and Transport**: Founded the company in Sequoia Falls in 1891 to serve the Harrison Gold Prospect (later [[Dead Horse Quarry]]).
* **Family**: Father of [[Winston Foxglove]]. Passed the business to Winston upon his death in 1934.

*(Note: Thurston's role is primarily historical, establishing the family business that Winston later transformed into LTX. There is no indication in the documents that he had any awareness of or involvement with Rhea or the Algorithm.)*
