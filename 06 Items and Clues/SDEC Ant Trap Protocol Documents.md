## Overview

Internal PCBOT paperwork describing the procedure by which [[SDEC Facility|SDEC]]'s "Ant Trap" — the automated containment lockdown triggered in 1992 — can be remotely disengaged from PCBOT's control center in Washington D.C. The override authority is one of the responsibilities PCBOT inherited during the post-1992 cleanup and has, under Caraway, never used.

## Provenance

The originals live in PCBOT's Secure Archives (see [[PCBOT Headquarters]]). The copies the players are actually working with were quietly taken by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice]] during the EUROPA investigation, between 1992 and 1994, before Caraway's coup compartmentalized her out of PCBOT's interior. She has been carrying them in encrypted form ever since — eventually folded into the contingency package on [[Éric Blanc]]'s drive at [[Éric Blanc's Apartment]]. PCBOT does not know they were copied.

(Open authorial question: the specific mechanics of *when* and *how* Alice took them, and whether she had a concrete plan at the time to open SDEC herself, are TBD. The current canon supports any of: opportunistic during a routine PCBOT visit; deliberate theft in the weeks after the 1992 purge while she was helping Lopez gather evidence; or pulled from EUROPA-related file requests where they were filed adjacent to materials she was legitimately allowed to see.)

## Role in the Campaign

The documents are the operational core of the [[PCBOT Headquarters|PCBOT excursion]]. The override they describe is what the team executes in the control center; it is the *only* thing extractable from PCBOT-to-SDEC, in keeping with [[Mirror-Breaker Protocol|Mirror-Breaker doctrine]] (SDEC has no networked archive — see principle 6).

* **As decrypted from Blanc's drive:** Alice's copies, plus the override procedure she and Blanc worked up against [[Agent Chrysalis]]'s sonar readings between 1994 and 1996. The override has to be run live at PCBOT's console; Alice is the only one fluent enough to execute it end-to-end.
* **As recovered in person at PCBOT:** The originals are still in the Secure Archives. Finding them closes the loop on Alice's pre-coup theft and confirms (to the players) that the operational package on Blanc's drive is real, current, and from the source.
