## Overview
The string `doolittle567`, found by [[Sean Fielding]] on [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] letterhead during his stint at [[LTX Headquarters|LTX]]. The hint, in turn, was probably extracted from Syd during his captivity. It is a password clue for [[Syd Foxglove]]'s [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing]] account.

## Decoding
The hint points to the Pixies album *Doolittle* and the lyric "this monkey's gone to heaven / 5, 6, 7" from [[Monkey Gone to Heaven (Song)]]. The actual password is whatever combination Syd chose around those references — most likely `monkey567`, `doolittle567`, or a close variant.

## How It Reaches the Players
* Sean's notes / desk at the LTX offices (Act II revisit with Sean's keycard).
* Optionally corroborated by [[Amanda Green]]'s vinyl copy of *Doolittle* and the [[Amanda Green Photograph]] (Syd in a "Death to the Pixies" T-shirt).

## Significance
Grants access to Syd's ErgoMatic account, which is the gateway to [[Oliver Foxglove Emails]] and the Montreal-IP login warnings.
