## Overview
A map of the [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] area found in the vault within [[Alice Redmond's House]].

## Content
* Marks six specific locations within Sequoia Falls, forming a large hexagonal pattern when connected.
* The locations likely correspond to key sites related to [[Signal Dummies|Dummy]] activity, [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] operations, or areas of [[Rhea]] influence.
* Location 'X' (under the I-90 overpass) is particularly significant, leading to [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]].

## Significance
* Unwittingly maps the boundaries of [[Rhea]]'s influence or perhaps the placement of key [[The Hexacle|Hexacles]].
* Serves as a primary guide for the players' initial investigation phases in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Location & Status (Oct 1997)
* Found by players in [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s vault.
