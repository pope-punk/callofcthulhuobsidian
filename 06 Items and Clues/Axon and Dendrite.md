## Overview
"Axon and Dendrite" was an avant-garde late-night radio program broadcast from the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] under cover of the [[Special Committee for Artistic Innovation]] from 1987 to 1992. Hosted by [[David Brewster]] and [[Maria Chen]] (both unwitting), it was BSSA's vehicle for [[Operation Colossus]] — embedding [[The Echo]] auditory basilisk into the call-in segments via [[Raymond Vaughn (Agent Rococo)|Agent Rococo]]'s modulator. The complex caller-tone feedback loop it built up across five years of broadcasts was the substrate for [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]]'s emergence.

The show developed a cult following, particularly in Sequoia Falls and (inexplicably, via a still-unexplained simultaneous broadcast) Juneau, Alaska. Listener accounts shifted over time from religious visions to cryptid hallucinations, tracking the show's evolving themes.

## Disrupted (Mid-1992)
[[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] detected EUROPA and gave the hosts the randomizer tool ([[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]]) that broke up the broadcast structure. Vaughn pulled the modulator. The show stopped airing in mid-1992.

* Recordings (the **[[Axon and Dendrite Recordings]]**) and the show's final-segment ordering ([[Brewster's Final Segment Sequence Log]]) are key Ellensburg-arc clues.
* The show is the entry point to [[Maria Chen]] via [[Amanda Green]]'s Seattle friend.
* Confirms BSSA's involvement in [[Operation Colossus]] and explains EUROPA's emergence to the players.
