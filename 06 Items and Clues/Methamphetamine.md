## Description

**Methamphetamine** is a powerful stimulant drug appearing in the campaign, primarily linked to the [[Hammerskull Crew]] and BSSA's early chemical experiments.

## Known Information & Connections

* **[[White Stag Bar]]**: Serves as a front for a meth lab run by the [[Hammerskull Crew]][cite: 5, 37]. Production continued here after [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] research moved.
* **[[Calvin Pierce]]**: Stole meth from the White Stag. May have distributed alongside or confused with F7 at [[COTU (Center of the Universe)]].
* **[[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]]**: Meth found at this location, linking BSSA's front operation to the drug trade]. 
* **[[Operation Colossus]]**: Methamphetamine was used experimentally by BSSA (sourced via Hammerskulls) in 1986 to try and enhance the effects of [[The Echo]] basilisk, particularly as users were found highly susceptible. This component was terminated after a test subject suicide.
* **[[Hammerskull Crew]]**: Directly involved in production/distribution via White Stag[cite: 5, 115, 134]. [[Sean Fielding]] had connections to them.
* **BSSA Adulteration**: BSSA frequently adulterates F7 with stimulants (including meth or related amphetamines) to counteract cognitive side effects during testing.
	* Metamphetamine is an essential part of Project Ozymandias.
