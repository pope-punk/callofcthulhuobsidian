## Overview
**THEBES** is an ErgoMatic-internal designator for the gene-altering precursor compound Oliver Foxglove pulled off the company's servers in late 1996 and sealed in his home safe. It is the essential reagent for any non-gallium [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)|G6]] synthesis. Without THEBES — or access to ErgoMatic's tightly controlled production stock — Syd cannot make G6.

## In-Universe History
Developed in ErgoMatic's BAD (Biomedical) division under Oliver's intuitive direction in the mid-1990s, ostensibly as a refinement reagent for the [[GenAssist Universal Tissue Printer]]'s integration chemistry. Its actual function is closer to a controlled mutagen: it nudges DNA expression along Algorithm-aligned pathways. Oliver did not fully understand what he was building until his December 1996 realization (see [[Oliver Foxglove]]).

*(The name carries a thematic resonance Oliver almost certainly chose deliberately — Thebes, the city of Oedipus, where the Foxglove pattern of "father killed, son inherits the curse" plays out in a way Oliver was beginning to recognize in his own family.)*

## Storage and Status
* **One sealed sample** rests in Oliver's home safe, vacuum-packed alongside [[Oliver Foxglove's Advanced Research Notes]]. Recoverable only via the [[Safe Puzzle]].
* **The controlled ErgoMatic stock** is locked behind BAD-division biometrics. Syd cannot reach it without re-entering ErgoMatic under his own credentials, which BSSA has every reason to be watching for.
* **Katherine Hill plausibly wants it back**, both because she suspects what it is and because the same compound is theoretically useful in [[Operation Parthenon]] and [[Project Ozymandias]] research.

## Role in Campaign
The "reagent" half of the safe payoff. Without it, the G6 chain stalls and Alice cannot be cured — which in turn means the encrypted PCBOT methodology stays encrypted.
