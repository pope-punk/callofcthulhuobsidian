## Overview
[[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] stands as the cornerstone of the [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]'s chemical arsenal, a potent psychoactive compound developed under the auspices of [[Project Ozymandias]]. Born from BSSA's conviction that direct chemical intervention is necessary to navigate the treacherous landscape of [[Basilisks & Infohazards|infohazards]], F7 is designed to shield agents by fundamentally altering their perception of patterns, but its protection comes at a steep cognitive and psychological price.

## The Genesis of F7 - Project Ozymandias
The path to F7 began with earlier research into a prototype known as HFD during [[Operation Colossus]]. Recognizing the need for a reliable countermeasure against the mind-altering patterns they sought to understand and weaponize, BSSA dedicated significant resources to [[Project Ozymandias]]. Under the lead of Agent Chisel ([[Clancy Martell (Agent Chisel)]]), and with oversight from [[Katherine Hill]] and [[Anselm Chambers (Deacon)]], the project aimed to synthesize a chemical agent capable of rendering individuals immune to the direct effects of [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]]. Production was initially explored at the [[White Stag Bar]]'s basement lab before being centralized at the more sophisticated facilities within [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]], where the first full synthesis of modern F7 was achieved in late 1994.

## Mechanism of Action - Dampening the System Pole
[[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]], a heavily modified LSD derivative incorporating fluorine compounds, operates primarily on the brain's serotonergic pathways. Its key function is to induce a general suppression of the **[[Cognitive Dialectic (Metaphor vs. System)|System Pole]]** of human cognition – the faculty responsible for logical, analytical, and pattern-following thought. By dampening this pole, F7 effectively prevents the brain from fully processing or "locking onto" complex infohazardous patterns like [[The Hexacle]], [[Black Coral]], or [[The Echo]]. To an F7 user, these patterns appear as meaningless shapes or sounds, their harmful neurological impact neutralized. This allows users to perceive and navigate areas or entities normally obscured or defended by such patterns.

## The Price of Immunity - Side Effects and Unintended Consequences
While F7 offers a shield against [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisks]], its suppression of the System Pole has severe repercussions:
* **Cognitive Impairment:** Users experience difficulty with memory, sequential thought, and logical reasoning.
* **Psychological Effects:** Paranoia, disorganized thinking, and a significant blurring of the lines between reality and delusion are common.
* **Foreclosure of categorical distinctions.** With the System Pole suppressed, users lose the capacity to maintain stable categorical distinctions that depend on pattern-matched features rather than direct sensory presence. At high doses this manifests as an inability to reliably differentiate human beings from [[Signal Dummies]] — both are humanoid-shaped, both move, both can be present at an LTX-network address, and the cognitive operation that would normally tell them apart is exactly the operation F7 has dampened. The same mechanism produces the inverse error: anomalous categories that *don't* fit a user's pre-F7 working theory are sorted into "perceptual hazard, hallucination, ignore." [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice's]] processing of [[Rhea's Agents (Editors, Reapers, Writers, Watchers)|Rhea's biomorphic agents]] as basilisk-induced hallucinations rather than as real predators is this failure mode in action.
* **Stimulant Counteraction:** To mitigate these debilitating cognitive side effects, BSSA frequently adulterates F7 doses with stimulants such as [[Adderall]] or [[Methamphetamine]].
* **Overdose Syndrome:** As witnessed at the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU (Center of the Universe)]], massive F7 overdose leads to a paradoxical state of food worship accompanied by starvation, alongside extreme psychological distress.
## Role in the Narrative
[[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] is a pervasive element in the campaign. It is the source of [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]'s profound cognitive impairment and a driver of her actions. Its unethical testing on homeless individuals recruited through [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] led directly to the formation and subsequent tragedy at the [[COTU (Center of the Universe)|COTU (Center of the Universe)]] after [[Calvin Pierce]] stole and distributed a pure batch. F7 stands as a stark example of BSSA's ruthless pragmatism and the dangerous, often unforeseen, consequences of their attempts to control human cognition.
