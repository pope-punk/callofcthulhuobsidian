## Description

Several pages consisting of fractal hexagon patterns found at the end of one of [[Winston Foxglove]]'s personal memoirs. These memoirs are located within the sealed [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] facility.

## Function & Significance

* **Encoded Information**: The patterns contain the true, encoded details of [[Winston Foxglove]]'s pivotal 1922 encounter with the [[Rhea]] [[Hive]], including its location at [[Dead Horse Quarry]].
* **Decoding Key**: The information can only be decoded by someone with deep intuitive understanding of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] (like [[Syd Foxglove]]) or someone under the influence of [[G6 (Non-Gallium Antidote)]] (which enhances systematic pattern recognition). Showing the pages to such an individual allows decryption.
* **Infohazard**: The pages themselves likely function as a complex visual infohazard, related to [[The Hexacle]]. Viewing them directly without the proper cognitive state or insight could be dangerous.
* **Revelation**: Successfully decoding these pages reveals the location and nature of Winston's encounter, confirming the link between the Foxgloves, the Algorithm, Rhea, and the primary Hive entrance. This is a key step towards the campaign's endgame.
