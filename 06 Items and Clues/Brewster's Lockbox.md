# Brewster's Lockbox

## Overview
A physical lockbox prepared by the late [[David Brewster]] in mid-1992, before his execution by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)|BSSA]]. Accessing this lockbox is the structural gate that allows the investigators to locate [[Maria Chen]] — who is, in turn, the only person who can give them the apartment address of [[Éric Blanc]] in Montreal.

## Physical Description
A medium-sized, fire-resistant metal lockbox, dark grey, with a standard combination lock. Practical, unornamented.

## Origin & Keeper
* **Origin:** Prepared by [[David Brewster]] in his final days as he realized BSSA was hunting him and that SDEC had been compromised.
* **Keeper:** Entrusted to a former colleague near Ellensburg ([[Professor Vance]]).
* **Brewster's Motivation:** He needed to protect Chen, who had survived the BSSA purge. He devised a security measure tied to the SDEC randomizer tool, believing only true SDEC allies could decipher the passphrase, keeping the contents safe from BSSA.

## Acquisition Method
The lockbox cannot be opened directly; it must be obtained from [[Professor Vance]]. This requires:
1. Solving the [[Brewster's Lockbox Puzzle]].
2. Finding both the [[Brewster's Final Segment Sequence Log]] and the [[SDEC Randomizer Mapping Rules]] at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]].
3. Using the rules to reverse-engineer the four-digit code Brewster used for his final broadcast sequence.
4. Locating Vance and providing the code as a passphrase.

## Contents
* **Primary:** Current (as of 1997) contact information for [[Maria Chen]] in [[Portland, OR]] — her assumed name, address, and phone.
* **Secondary:** A short final letter from Brewster urging caution and naming Blanc as a trustworthy outside contact in passing (but not providing Blanc's address — Chen has that).

## Narrative Role
* The campaign's structural gate between the Ellensburg arc and the Montreal arc. Without it (or the parallel Amanda Green → Seattle friend chain), the players have no way of obtaining Blanc's apartment address. BNJC does not have it.
* Reflects Brewster's final act of defiance and his attempt to ensure the truth about Operation Colossus could eventually be revealed.

## Status (Late Oct 1997)
Held by [[Professor Vance]]. Locked. Awaiting retrieval by investigators who can provide the correct four-digit passphrase derived from the puzzle solution.
