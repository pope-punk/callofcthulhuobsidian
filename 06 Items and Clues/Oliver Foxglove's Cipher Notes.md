## Overview
Older research notes created by [[Oliver Foxglove]] (likely pre-December 1996) detailing his personal cipher method. Found at the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]].

## Content & Process
These notes explain the *process* Oliver used for encoding dates into keypad codes; they do not contain the safe code itself. The process:

1. **Start with a Base-10 Date** (Month / Day / Year).
2. **Convert to [[Ylynthii Numerals]]** — convert the Base-10 date into the Ylynthii base-6 system. Requires knowing the Ylynthii symbols and base conversion rules (recoverable from Syd's cell-wall markings).
3. **Map to Keypad** — substitute the resulting Ylynthii digits onto a standard 0-9 keypad using the specific substitution scheme Oliver describes in these notes.

## Significance
* Provides the **methodology** required to solve the [[Safe Puzzle]].
* Links the puzzle to the alien **[[Ylynthii Numerals]]** system, reinforcing the cosmic origins of the Algorithm and underlining that Oliver deliberately gated the safe behind a process only someone Algorithm-fluent (i.e., Syd) could reproduce.

## Role in Campaign
* Found during the investigation of the [[ErgoMatic Manufacturing Facility]] (in Oliver's old research files).
* Must be combined with **Andrea Foxglove's birthday** (hinted in [[Oliver Foxglove Emails]]) and the rules of [[Ylynthii Numerals]] (learned from Syd's BSSA cell) to derive the final safe code.
