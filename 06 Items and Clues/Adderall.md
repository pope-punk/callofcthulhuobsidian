## Description

**Adderall** is a prescription stimulant medication (amphetamine salts) used in the campaign by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]] for a specific, non-standard purpose.

## Function & Use

* **Counteracting [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]] Side Effects**: Alice uses Adderall not for ADHD, but to temporarily mitigate the severe cognitive impairment and confusion caused by her exposure to [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]. It helps her maintain focus and operational capacity despite F7's pattern-suppressing effects.
* **Maintaining Mission Focus**: Allows Alice to continue her hunt for what she believes is a new [[RONINs (Randomly Organizing Neural Information Networks)|RONIN]], despite her compromised mental state.

## Known Information & Connections

* **[[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)]]**: The primary user identified in the documents. Empty Adderall bottles may be found in locations she frequents, like her apartment or temporary hideouts.
* **[[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]]**: Its use is directly linked to mitigating F7's debilitating effects.
* **[[G5 (Gallium-stabilized Amphetamine Complex)]]**: Adderall is a conventional amphetamine, distinct from the exotic, gallium-stabilized G5 complex developed as part of Rhea-related chemical research. It highlights Alice using a mundane tool to fight against the effects of an extraordinary substance.

## Significance

* Demonstrates Alice's desperate measures to remain functional.
* **Clue that other amphetamines (G5, [[Methamphetamine]]) have distinct and important effects on F7 patients.**
* Provides a potential clue for investigators tracking Alice (finding empty bottles).
* Highlights the severe neurological impact of F7.
