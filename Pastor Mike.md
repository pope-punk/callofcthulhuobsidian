**Tags:** #Character #PC #Deceased

## Overview
One of the original player investigators. A local pastor in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] who hosted the group at his home through the first week of investigation and assisted at the [[Sean Fielding's House]], the 3704 S. Preston Rd LTX office, and the [[LTX Headquarters]] interview.

## Status
**Killed by [[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]]** at the [[Redwood Shopping Mall]] on October 7, 1997, when Alice opened fire on the investigators as she fled with a disabled [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]]. His death is the campaign's first PC casualty and the moment that escalates the investigators' confrontation with the Mirror-Breaker world.
