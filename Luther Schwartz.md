## Overview
Owner of the [[White Stag Bar]] in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]] and presumably the senior local figure in the [[Hammerskull Crew]]. Together with his brother **Tyler Schwartz** (the bar's manager) and [[David Schwartz]] (an enforcer), the Schwartz family runs the local meth pipeline and provides the muscle BSSA leans on for security at [[Seventh Tabernacle Church of Life]] and elsewhere.

See also: [[The Schwartz Family]] for the broader family entry.

## Status
Active in Sequoia Falls. Hostile if confronted directly; likely to be encountered during the White Stag investigation.
