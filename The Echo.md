## Overview
An **auditory** [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisk]] developed and weaponized by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] between 1986-1992 as part of [[Operation Colossus]]. It was the immanent vector for the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN.

## Appearance (Auditory)
* Consists of complex sequences of **algorithmically modulated audio tones**. Precise tones likely derived from [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] research and refined via feedback loops.

## Mechanism & Effect
* **Targets Auditory Processing:** Exploits vulnerabilities in how the brain processes complex auditory patterns.
* **Induces Fear & Hallucinations:** Causes intense fear, paranoia, religious visions (angels/demons), and later cryptid hallucinations (matching the themes of the "Axon and Dendrite" show used for propagation). Effects were particularly strong in [[Methamphetamine]] users.
* **Feedback Modulation:** [[Operation Colossus]] used technology to modulate the Echo tones in real-time based on listener vocal pitch, creating complex feedback loops that enhanced its effects and contributed to [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]]'s emergence.

## History & Significance
* **Development (1986-1992):** Created by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] during [[Operation Colossus]] using repurposed [[CIA]] tech at the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]] and the [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb|Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]] front. Embedded in "Seventh Hour" and "Axon and Dendrite" radio shows.
* **EUROPA Event:** Triggered the [[EUROPA (1986-1992)]] RONIN.
* **Disruption (Mid 1992):** Broadcasts disrupted by [[SDEC (San Diego Entomology Club)|SDEC]] intervention, terminating EUROPA. Key tech removed by [[Raymond Vaughn (Agent Rococo)|Agent Rococo]].
* **BSSA Use:** [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]] retains recordings of [[The Echo]] and uses it in [[Project Ozymandias]] at [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] to test the efficacy of [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)]].

## Status (Oct 1997)
* No longer being actively broadcast. However, recordings exist and are used by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]. Represents [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]]'s failed attempt at weaponizing audio basilisks, leading them to focus on visual/spatial patterns like [[Fauxflake]]. Listening to recordings remains dangerous.
