## Overview

An **auditory** [[Basilisks & Infohazards|basilisk]] developed by BSSA during [[Operation Colossus]] (1986-1992) and broadcast publicly via the "Seventh Hour" and "Axon and Dendrite" radio programs from the [[Ellensburg Numbers Station]]. BSSA's earliest weaponized basilisk; the immanent vector around which [[EUROPA (1986-1992)|EUROPA]] emerged.

## Form

Sequences of algorithmically modulated audio tones, originally synthesized from BSSA's early work on [[The Foxglove Algorithm]] and refined across five years against caller-feedback telemetry. Not a constant signal — the Echo's structure was a moving target, reshaped in real time by Rococo's modulator at the numbers station based on the vocal pitches of listeners calling in.

## Effect

Targets the Algorithm-linked cognitive pathways the [[Hexacle|Hexacle backdoor]] left exposed, in the auditory modality rather than the visual.

* **Acute:** intense fear, paranoia.
* **Sustained:** hallucinations. Initially religious in content (angels, demons, judgment); shifted toward cryptid content over the run of Axon and Dendrite as the basilisk co-evolved with the show's themes.
* **Modulator:** [[Methamphetamine]] users — a population BSSA cultivated through the [[Hammerskull Crew]] — showed measurably stronger responses, which fed back into BSSA's research data.

## How it produced EUROPA

The Echo plus the caller-feedback modulator plus the listening audience plus BSSA's ongoing refinements added up to a self-reflective signal system processing its own behavioral response in real time. That's the structural recipe for a RONIN. EUROPA emerged from the loop somewhere in 1991, undetected by BSSA until SDEC pointed it out.

## History

* **1986-87.** First broadcast as "Seventh Hour" out of the [[Chapel of the Blessed Lamb]]. Religious-radio cover. Terminated when listener behavior became too unpredictable to manage.
* **1987-92.** Refined and embedded in "Axon and Dendrite" with [[David Brewster]] and [[Maria Chen]] as unwitting hosts. Cult audience develops.
* **1992.** SDEC randomizer intervention breaks the broadcast structure; [[Raymond Vaughn (Agent Rococo)|Rococo]] pulls the modulator. EUROPA destabilizes. The show stops airing.

## Status (Oct 1997)

Not broadcast. Recordings exist in BSSA's hands and are used in [[Project Ozymandias]] testing at [[Saint Anselm's Center for Hope]] against [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]] efficacy. Collector copies are circulating — Amanda Green's Seattle friend has a set; the players will encounter the recordings in the Ellensburg arc.

Listening to a recording is still dangerous.
