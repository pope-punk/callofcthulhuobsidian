## Overview
Original diagrams, blueprints, or documentation detailing the specific six-pointed fractal warehouse layouts designed by [[Winston Foxglove]] based on [[The Foxglove Algorithm]].

## History & Location
* Implemented at [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] warehouses starting in 1935. Credited with revitalizing the company.
* Served as the **immanent vector** for the [[CALLISTO (1949)]] RONIN emergence, causing "passive compulsion" effects in workers interacting with the pattern on a large scale.
* Original documentation likely stored in the **historical archives on the 2nd floor of [[LTX Headquarters]]** in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Significance
* Represents a direct application and physical manifestation of [[The Foxglove Algorithm]].
* Highly sought after by [[BSSA (Boise Society for Sculptural Appreciation)]], who tasked [[Sean Fielding]] (via [[Hammerskull Crew]]) with infiltrating [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] to retrieve these documents, likely to compare with or improve their own [[Fauxflake]] pattern for [[Operation Parthenon]].
* Finding these diagrams could provide players with direct insight into Algorithm patterns and BSSA's motivations.

## Status (Oct 1997)
* Presumed extant within [[LTX Headquarters]] archives. Investigating [[Sean Fielding]]'s activities at LTX may lead players to search for these.
