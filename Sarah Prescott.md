## Description

Sarah Prescott was an employee of [[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] living in [[Sequoia Falls, WA]].

## Known Information & Connections

* **[[LTX (Land Transit Express)|LTX]] Employee**: Worked for LTX. Her employee keycard was found at her apartment.
* **Address**: Lived at 881 3rd Ave NE, Sequoia Falls.
* **[[Alice Redmond (Agent Altissimo)|Alice Redmond]] Victim**: Targeted and killed by the [[F7 (Fluoroxepram)|F7]]-affected Alice Redmond on August 10th, 1997. Alice likely mistook her for a [[Signal Dummies|Signal Dummy]]. Her name appeared crossed out in [[Alice's Journal (Satchel)|Alice's journal]].
* **Crime Scene**: Her apartment contained an LTX jacket and [[The Hexacle]] graffiti left by Alice. Drag marks indicated her body was removed. Players investigated her apartment.
* **Keys**: A set of keys including a Toyota key, house key, and keychain with phone number 509-312-3036 were found in Alice's dropped satchel, presumed to be Sarah's.

## Status

Deceased. Killed August 10th, 1997.
